  Camden April 3 '91

Have just sold a couple of books—$2 enclosed (suppose you got the one four days ago)—nothing new with me—think ab't​ you every day—dark wet weather—am partly busy with the proofs of the little 2d annex for L​ of G.​ to conclude it—Have been visited on business & feel tired out

—Love to you sister dear— Walt Whitman
