  GLENDINNING, McLEISH & CO. 32 WALKER STREET. Cor. Church St. New York, July 17 1886 Dear Sir

Your postal recd​ and I was glad that you had accepted my small offering, I was almost afraid to send it thinking it might offend you, and since receiving it, it has occurred to me that perhaps you might have some matter of published manuscript (say parts of Specimen Days) that you would be willing to dispose of and I would be glad to purchase. If so let me know what, and for how much

By so doing you will oblige

Yours G M Williamson Mr Walt Whitman     desires to purchase MSS—answer | July '86
