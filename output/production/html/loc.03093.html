 loc.03093.001_large.jpg Dear Sir:1

Can you kindly inform where I can get a complete, original copy of your poem entitled "Leaves of Grass"? I want it as you first wrote it. Not a revised edition.

By answering the above you will greatly oblige

Yours sincerely Ralph E. Moore Stony Point N.Y. Box 283 Rockland Co.  loc.03093.002_large.jpg  loc.03093.003_large.jpg  loc.03093.004_large.jpg

Correspondent:
Little is known about Ralph E. Moore of Stony Point, New York. He may be the twenty-two-year-old laborer listed as "R. E. Moore" in the 1892 State Census of New York. At that time, he was living with E. H. Moore, probably a brother, who was eighteen.
