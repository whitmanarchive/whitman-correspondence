Boston, Sunday night, | April 24. Dear Brother,

Mr. Baker, In compliance with your request, I execute and return the new and explanatory deed forthwith, depending upon you that it is all right, as I know nothing at all about such things.

I paid Mr. Adams, the Commissioner, one dollar, which you may send me, if you please.

Yours &c

Walt Whitman
