  Camden Sunday forenoon July 3

Am feeling poorly to-day—the result of the long spell of hot weather—(two poor little babies have died from it in this block the last week)—I send you and George a nice little copy of "Specimen Days"—

—Love to you all—

—If the weather changes, & I feel better I sh'd like to drive down & stay five or six hours, some day early the coming week—

  Mrs. Susan Stafford Glendale New Jersey
