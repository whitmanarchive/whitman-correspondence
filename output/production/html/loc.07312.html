  Superintendent's Office. Asylum 
 for the Insane London. Ontario London, Ont., 4 Aug 1889

"All quiet on the Potomac" a real nice day—cloudy, not hot, no wind, a sleepy day

No word from any one since I wrote last. Nothing new here: Spent the morning writing a batch of letters (11 foolscap pp. of them) to the Inspector on various subjects. Have just been to Catholic service in our little Asylum chapel. I ought to hear something pretty decisive about the meter this week but I never can be sure when any thing is going to happen or be done in that matter—Willy Gurd has no time sense

Love to you R M Bucke
