Camden N J —U S America June 16 evn'g: '91

Y'rs rec'd, will send you budget of portraits—will send Dr [Johnston] his six G B F soon as I get bound ones (in a day or two probably)—Hot weather here, the most oppressive ever known—I am standing it pretty well so far—look out for next Lippincott mag.

Love to Dr & all the boys— Walt Whitman
