Camden New Jersey Jan: 6 '89

Y'rs of 3d recd here. The big book "Complete Works" &c: is $6. It contains "Democratic Vistas"—With four portraits extra on loose sheets it w'd be $6.50—If you desire it, send p o money order—& send word whether you prefer by mail or express—

Walt Whitman

I can send in a little paper bound Vol: "Dem: Vistas"—50cts—if wanted—
