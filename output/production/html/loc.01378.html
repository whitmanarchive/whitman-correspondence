  Camden NJ America Thursday Evn'g Oct 4 '88—

Still keeping my sick room but fair spirits & no worse—great debility of legs & without body strength & control—Book printing has proceeded & sheets now at bindery—will send you a copy of Nov: Boughs soon—Herbert Gilchrist comes occasionally—is well. Dr Bucke is well—busy—writes daily—Cold here—I have a new wood stove—better—Love to you, to y'r father & all

W W
