  Williamsburgh, March 31. 145 Grand St. Mr. Whitman, Dr Sir,—

Mr. Twombly and myself are about to commence the publication of a new weekly journal; devoted to the interests of Brooklyn, (for which we have received much encouragement,) and think we could give you a good position upon it. Our printing office will be here, but we wish a publication office near the City Hall, and an editor who will take charge of it. I wish you would call on us, at 145 Grand St., as soon as you can if you desire to have a connection with us.

Respectfully Yours Aaron Smith     from Aaron Smith of Williamsburgh about new paper
