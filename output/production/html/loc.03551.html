  Delaware County Institute of Science, (Organized, A.D., 1833) Media, Pa.​ , Dec. 29th 1884 Walt Whitman Esq Camden, New Jersey Dear Sir:

My friend Col.​ Jewel wrote me that you would come here & Lecture for us. Could you come in Jan'y​ say about 29th inst or Feb.​ 5th or 12th & give us your Lecture on Lincoln, We would pay you 25$—Our resources are small or we would offer more.

Resply​ Jno: B Robinson for comtee Dela. Co Ins.
