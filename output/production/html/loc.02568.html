  J. H. JOHNSTON,  
  JEWELER SPECIALTIES: DUPLICATE WEDDING PRESENTS. DIAMONDS, WATCHES, FINGER RINGS. THE REPAIRING OF FINE WATCHES AND JEWELRY A SPECIALTY. 150 BROWERY, COR. BROOME ST.  
 New York, Sept 9 1885 Dear Walt:

Glad to hear from you once more.

Many thanks for the books you sent. I inclose $2.00 for the two and for the balance all the thanks you desire.

Do you mind telling me how much of a Mortgage there is on your house?

Alma is well. She goes with me tonight to see Robson & Crane in the Comedy of Errors.

Sincerely yours J H Johnston   see notes July 5 1891
