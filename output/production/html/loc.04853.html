  74 Clinton Place New York City Nov 2. 1890 Dear Sir.

My friend Mr Stedman tells me that he thinks you would allow me to call on you. Since I left England it has been my greatest wish to have the great pleasure of seeing you that I hope to be able to roam over to Camden on Tuesday next for that purpose, and to thank you as one who has already found a friend in your works

faithfully yours Gleeson White.
