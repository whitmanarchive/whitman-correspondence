  Camden N J—U S America Aug: 6 '91—

Yrs of 26th July this mn'g—(this is the 3d)—Rather blue with me this week—but I keep up & eat my meals—(peaches & tomatoes are at their height & I alternate with them)—Good affectionate long letters f'm Dr J & J W W Bolton—Horace here daily—am writing nothing for print—letter f'm friend Johnston N Y jeweller yesterday—give my aff: regards to Forman, & to all friends—

Walt Whitman   Aug 6
