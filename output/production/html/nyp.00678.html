Camden April 16 '89

Much disappointed the last two days at not getting word f'm you. Pray this may find you comfortable. Nothing new or different with me—have had bad ten days, head, stomach, &c—Suspicion however of slight lull to-day—Some good words of me in big printed quarters (such as N Y World, Herald, &c)—Sit here in big chair ab't same—Love to you and N[elly]—

W W
