Camden N J—U S America July 14 '91

Hot wave here—am sitting here by window as usual—Y'r's & Dr J[ohnston]'s letters & papers come right—thanks—Suppose you have seen Dr B[ucke] before this—Am much the same considering but badly depress'd to-day—hope for better things by and bye—God bless you—

Walt Whitman
