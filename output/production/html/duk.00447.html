 Proprietor. Burlington, Vt. 189 May 3—'90 Bro Walt,

Han is gaining yet of course impatient— Any word or notice of you she studies closely—

Hard times with me for money—

I sent a painting to New York—to Aquila, Rich, 84 William St—reported safe—and carefully hang​ hung​ up— —

I am wearing a batterd​ battered​ old hat—Shame on Burlington Vermont——allmost​ almost​ begging for victuals—or provisions at times—Yet hope to surpass my troubles yet.

Charlie
