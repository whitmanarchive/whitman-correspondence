Camden March 19 '89

Have rec'd the Saturday Review with notice (bad enough yet essentially taking back their old insults & charges) in wh' y'r name & Dr B[ucke]'s are flung about—will probably send it to you to-morrow—(Horace T has taken it away temporarily)—Nothing new with me—if anything different it is I am feeling easier—a dark half raining warmish day here—with me sitting the same alone in big chair—sleep & eat fairly yet—Best love—

Walt Whitman
