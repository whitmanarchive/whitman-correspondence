  Feb. 13. 1890. Dear Walt,

Thank you for papers &c. How are you? I am not sick, but am much worried on account of the failure of the man to pay me, to whom Wm. loaned money.

I had the influenza, & was ill, but the worry takes all life out of me. I don't want any legal trouble over it.

With love as ever— E. M. O'C.
