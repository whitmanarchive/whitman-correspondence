  Camden May 19 '89

All goes much the same, & fairly. I have just sent to Mrs: O'C a Boston Transcript, May 16, with a 20 or 30 line notice of O'C's death—Have not seen yet any acc't of funeral—I get out a little every day in the wheel chair. The pocket-book ed'n will be bound & ready in a week—

Walt Whitman
