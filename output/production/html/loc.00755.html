The price of the "Thoughts"1 is $10.

I reserve the right of using it in any future edition of my poems.

I suggest, if accepted, it be placed at the top of the 2d, 3d, or 4th column, first page.

If declined, please keep the MS. till I call. I will call Wednesday morning.

Walt Whitman Jan. 16, '60.2

Notes

  • 1. There are no extant copies of the New York Sunday Courier for 1860. "Thoughts" appeared in the third edition of Leaves of Grass, 408-411. [back]
  • 2. Endorsed (by Walt Whitman): "Private note from Walt Whitman | To Editor Sunday Courier." [back]
