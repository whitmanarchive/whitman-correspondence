  431 Stevens st​ Camden Feb​ 13—p m Dear John Burroughs

Yours of 6th only rec'd​ this afternoon by me—as I have just return'd​ to day from a week down at a farm in Jersey—

—I hope you will adopt Birds & Poets—& then the Flight of the Eagle—As the more I think of them, the better I like both—(I mean directly & indirectly, & for wear)—Come on to Mrs. Gilchrist's 16th or 17th—I will prepare them—I will be there—it is 1929 north 22d street—I still keep pretty well for me
