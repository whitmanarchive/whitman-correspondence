  Medical Superindent's 
 Office. Insane Asylum 
 London Ontario 28 April 1891

Well I am here at the office again. Have been over most of the day and am feeling first rate—not particularly strong, but in good spirits and feel that things are on the right track—that I am going to be O.K. again. I have had quite a spell of it—been in bed and in my room since 10th say 17. days—a long sick spell for me—it will last me (I hope) for a while—The weather has turned cold and blustery but the place is looking well. My work has fallen behind at the office (tho Beemer has done first rate while I was sick) shall have to pitch in now and catch up.

Best love RM Bucke
