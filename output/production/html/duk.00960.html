Camden New Jersey Dec: 24 1890 Dear Ed

Y'rs came this mn'g, & was welcome (& you w'd be y'rself)—I am here yet, a peg additional dropping out every successive month or so but in many things the same subject continued—bladder trouble & the grip (aggravated cold in the head & stomach) are the worst—but I still keep pretty fair spirits & (fortunately) a stout strong right arm considering. Things in the house are ab't same—Mrs: Davis has just been in, is well—Warren has gone over to Phila—I am sitting here in the big chair with wolf skin spread over back—fine sunny day out—cold—no sleighing here—write when you can, dear boy, & I will too—God bless you—

Walt Whitman
