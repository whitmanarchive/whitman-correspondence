Rees Welsh & Co.,  
 . . .  
 Philadelphia, June 28 1882 My dear Ben: Ticknor

I wish you would do me the favor to have the steel plate, (we used in L of G) properly enveloped & sent by express, directed to Rees Welsh & Co: 23 south 9th street, (as above.) I have forgotten the address of your plate printer in B[oston] who has it.

Yours (notwithstanding) Walt Whitman
