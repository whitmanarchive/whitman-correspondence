  Grand Pacific Hotel Honoured Sir— & Dear Poet—

I beg you to accept my appreciative thanks for your great kindness in sending me by Mr. Stoker the little big book of poems—"As a Strong Bird" etc, &c.—

Since I am not personally known to you I conclude Mr. Stoker "asked" for me—it was     good of him—I know he loves you very much.

God bless you Dear Sir—believe me to be with much respect

Yours affectionately Ellen Terry. Chicago— January 4th, 88.     from Ellen Terry See notes March 30 1888
