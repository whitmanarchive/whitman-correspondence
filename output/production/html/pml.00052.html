Camden— Sunday afternoon Oct:​ 8 '82​

Never have we had a week of more perfect, sunny, fresh, yet mellow and rich, autumn weather—to-day is the eighth day of it—("In this resplendent summer" began Emerson 25 years ago in an autumn lecture, "it has been a luxury merely to live")—I suppose you have rec'd​ the "Specimen Days" I sent a while since—I am well as usual—reeling out my weeks & months about the same as ever—often think of you all—the books are selling quite well—

W. W.
