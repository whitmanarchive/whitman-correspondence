  March 5th 75 Walt Whitman

The articals​ articles​ you Sent me I received that is the paper a letter & a Picture and your good wishes all arrived at once.

I have not had time to anser​ answer​ yours untill​ until​ now. I had an auction yestarday​ yesterday​ 4 of March to sell my Personally​ personal​ troperty​ property​ as I told you in my other letter I have rented my farm of 35 acers​ acres​ for 5 years

I would like to see you very well and it is "possiably​ possible​ " We may meet again. if I can get an increase of Pension Probaly​ Probably​ I will come and see you. Why can not you com​ come​ & see my family I have a wife & littel​ little​ Girl 5 years old this month When we received your Picture She kissed it and likes to show it to every one that comes in the house[no handwritten text supplied here]I felt proud of it myself[no handwritten text supplied here]I will have to get a frame for it and hang it up on the wall

Walt my dear old Friend how I would lik​ like​ to grasp your hand and give you a kiss as I did in the days of yore. What a satisfaction it would be to me. We give posesion​ possession​ the first of April. We move about half a mile from this place my Post office will be the same (address)

your​ yours​ in hast​ haste​ Reuben Farwell Walt Whitman   Reuben Farwell, March. 1875. (sent papers & a few lines April 21, '75)

I will try and write more next time

Good by​ bye​ and God bless you Walt Whitman

Reuben Farwell
