Camden N J—U S America Evng Sept 3 '91

Dr B[ucke] is back safe—the Majestic came in N Y yesterday mn'g—Dr will be here to–morrow evn'g—J W W[allace] will arrive here Sunday ab't—Mrs: O'Connor is here—Horace is well—I am having very bad days & nights—head, belly & bladder—am sitting up now—have just had a light supper—respects & love to you & all the little church—

Walt Whitman

the Rev[iew] of Rev[iews] recd—Y'r letter rec'd to-day—
