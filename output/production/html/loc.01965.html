  N. Cambridge, Mass. Jan. 1, 1867.

A happy New Year, my dear friend!—And here is a lot of luck for your new vol. of "Leaves."

How are you, these days?

I had a delightful letter from W.D. O'C. yesterday. Thank him for me.

Ever Yours J.T. Trowbridge Walt Whitman.     Trowbridge—Jan 8-67—
