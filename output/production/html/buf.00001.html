 buf_es.00057.jpg Camden New Jersey Oct: 30 '801

I to-day forward by mail (same address as this card) my two Vols: according to your letter (enc​ rec'd​ : thanks)2—Will you please send me a postal notifying me on their safe arrival?

Walt Whitman  buf_es.00058.jpg

Correspondent:
Frederic Almy was a graduate of Harvard College, a friend of Jane Addams, and the founder in 1885 of the Saturn Club in Buffalo.

Notes

  • 1. This postcard is addressed: Frederic Almy | 151 Pawtucket street | Lowell Mass:. It is postmarked: CAMDEN | OCT 30 | N.J. ; LOWELL MASS | NOV 1 | 9AM | CARRIER. [back]
  • 2. Almy sent $10 to Whitman on October 27. [back]
