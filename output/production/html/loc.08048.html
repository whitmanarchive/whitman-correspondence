[5.21(?).1891] Dear M P—

This is the best I can do for y'r request—The pieces have not hitherto been publish'd at all but are to be finally included in the little vol: to come out (such "out" as it may be, probably small one) in ab't three weeks or longer—If Mr R[obinson] can publish them before it will do, & the proprieties conform'd to—The price is $10—

New Poetry. By Walt Whitman
