  Camden Oct: 19 '91 Dear sister

I am fairly to-day sitting here alone as usual—My Bolton, Eng.​ friend still here. Had buckwheat cakes & coffee for b'kfast​ —have not yet sent the big book to Dr Bingham but shall soon.

Best love to you & and hope this will find you comfortable—y'rs​ rec'd​ & welcomed always—$1 enc'd​ —

W W
