Attorney General's Office, Washington. March 15, 1870. Andrew & Thom. Rome, Dear friends,

I suppose the MS. of "Passage to India" came safe, last week—to be put in type as requested—with p. o. order for $10—I write now merely to ask you to read the proof carefully by copy—correct carefully—& then send me two proofs—put it through as soon as convenient.

Walt.

I have a piece in next Galaxy—about three or four sticks full—about Spring—I wish you would just copy it in your paper & send me 10 copies—
