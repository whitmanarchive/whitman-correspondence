  Camden July 2d Evn'g

Havn't heard f'm you since dear W[illiam]'s death—Suppose you are in O st. for the present—I send you "Unity" and "Liberty"—I send U to Eldridge and Burroughs—

Nothing markable in my condition—am doubtless declining—suppose you get the papers I send—Hot weather.

Best love always— Walt Whitman
