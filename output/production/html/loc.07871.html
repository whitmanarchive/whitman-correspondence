Camden N J U S America Jan: 5 '91

Thank you for the "Review of Reviews," wh' I was just wanting—Y'r letter too rec'd & welcomed—letter also f'm Mr Johnstone very acceptable—

Cold weather here—comfortable with me—a rush of visitors to-day & last evn'g—just sold a big book & got the money—am still writing a little for publication—word just f'm Dr Bucke, he is getting on well—Snow & sleighing there—O'Connor's book (tales) is settled to be pub'd by Houghton & Co. Boston—Aff. remembrances to J W W[allace] & all the friends—

W W
