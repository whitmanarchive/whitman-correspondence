  Camden New Jersey June 26 '88—US America

The doctor says I hold long substantially—wh' is the best I can send you this day—I have had a hard forenoon bad weakness &c.—but a little better & sitting up now —fearful hot weather here—best love & remembrance to you & father— I am a little apprehensive ab't him—

Walt Whitman
