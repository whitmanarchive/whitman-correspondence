  Boston Oct 6th 1863. My dear sir,

Having an opportunity to send by Miss Lowe the $20. From H. Lee, & B. H Silsbee of which I wrote you, I am very glad to take advantage of it—

You will find the amt enclosed—Hoping that we may be able to send you more hereafter,

I am very truly yours L B Russell.

Walt Whitman Esq.
