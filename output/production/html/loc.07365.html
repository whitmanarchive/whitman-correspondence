Camden N J— April 19 '87

I was down at Glendale Sunday—all well—all ab't as usual—Your father better, his back hurts some, but I think it will pass over—Ed was away—Harry has been here to-day—is getting along favorably —I have been to New York–Send you a paper with acc't—Love to Ruth, [Jo?], and all—

Walt Whitman
