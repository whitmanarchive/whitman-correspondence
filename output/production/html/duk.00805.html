328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey Aug: 5

Your card rec'd some days since—I had a sun stroke two weeks ago—makes me weak since (legs and bones like gelatine)—but I guess I am recuperating—My Phila: publisher McKay was just over here to pay me the income on the last six months' sales of my two Vols. L of G. and S Days—the am't was $22: & six cts.
