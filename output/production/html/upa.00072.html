  Kirkwood (Glendale) New Jersey U S America April 24—

Down here on one of my visits—Mr and Mrs S and all the young folks well as usual—E is still over at Laurel Mills, & is well & hearty—H runs the store here—(the store is doing middling well)—M and V and R and G all right & growing fast—

—Begins to look like spring—apple trees all in bloom, & the sprouting wheat a rich emerald, beautiful—(but just to-day is raw and half-raining & darkish)—the l[ecture] went off fairly, it was good fun for me, grave as the subject was—I sent you a short report—I am surprised about B—my health & strength fair

W W
