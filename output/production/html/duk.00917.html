Camden Evn'g March 17 '89

Matters not very different—the Transcripts rec'd—read & sent to O'Connor—O'C had a very bad spell ag'n since I wrote you ab't him (epileptic fits)—but was better three days ago, but weak & in bed—Dr B[ucke] here yet—

I sit here alone same as ever, in my big old chair with the thick wolf-skin back—Mrs Spaulding call'd to-day.

Walt Whitman
