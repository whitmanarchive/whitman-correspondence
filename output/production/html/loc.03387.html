  University Club. Dublin. March 8, 92 Dear Sir.

I hear that you are sick & write a line to send you my love & all manner of kind wishes. I was one of the first to welcome you & your poetry on this side of the Atlantic. I wrote as Arthur Clive in the Gentleman's Magazine long ago & ever since have ever found     your books a great comfort & delight to me & have caused many to buy them

I remain dear Sir Yours very affectionately Standish O'Grady
