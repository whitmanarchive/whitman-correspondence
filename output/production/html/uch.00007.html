 N J Jan: 25 '83 My dear friends

Would "the Bible as Poetry" suit you for paper of Feb: 3d or 10th? The price is $15—I would like 50 copies of the W W head with motto &c about same size as your issue, printed on heavier paper, with the "Critic" imprint left off—will pay (for paper & print) 4 cents a copy—$2.00—If put in type send proof of "Bible" here early in week—

Walt Whitman
