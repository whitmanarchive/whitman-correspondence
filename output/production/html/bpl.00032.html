  328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey May 30 '87

I wish to warmly thank Elizabeth Porter Gould and yourselves for the article ab't myself & the war hospitals—Nothing I have received has touch'd me deeper or been more comforting & agreeable to me.

If convenient I sh'd like to have you send this note to E P G. with my thanks and regards—

Walt Whitman
