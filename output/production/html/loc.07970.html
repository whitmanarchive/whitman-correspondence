  Camden PM Oct: 2 '90

Yr's of 30th Sept: rec'd—Mr Baker (from Ing:) is in Phila conferring with Horace ab't place, date, &c. &c.

—We will see what comes of it all—I rode out to Harleigh Cem't'y yesterday after noon—they have commenced the tomb, broke ground yesterday & will build the vault proper—the stone quarried at Quincy Mass: will be a month yet—Splendid day yesterday enjoy'd my hour's drive R Moore Sup't took me—grip and bladder bother on me

Walt Whitman
