  Belmont May 16. '89.

Yr paper & card rec'd. Wife and I read the newspaper notice as we were coming up the hill in the evening, we said Hurrah!—when the chair (wheeled) was mentioned. I am delighted. This is development, or stage, No 2,—the phaeton being No 1. I suppose it seems sort o' humiliating, but you are too glad to get out to care for that. I wish to be remembered especially to "Ed." I mean give him my regards. & to Dr. B. whose last I shall answer soon

W. S. K. See notice of W D O'C in Trans'pt May 16. I guess it is by Hurd.   See notes May 1889
