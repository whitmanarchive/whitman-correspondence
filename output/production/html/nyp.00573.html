Camden Nov. 2 '87 Dear Sir

The $16.50 on acc't of photo. sales, came safely to hand & this is the receipt—with best thanks—

Herbert Gilchrist hurried off, & I did not have a chance to mention to him ab't the London agency for the photos. I should suggest however the selection of some established shop or name in London as the best place—some place where such things are in their line—

I like best Nos. 3, 6 and 8 of the photos. Would send those only—

Am ab't as usual—

Walt Whitman
