January 10  
 3 o'clock p. m. Dear William,

Mr. Trowbridge is going to start for Boston to-night in 7 o'clock train—should you wish to send any word, (or any thing else) to Nelly personally, he will deliver it. I suppose you can find him at Mr. Newton's at the barracks cor 17th & I till about 6 p. m.

Walt.
