  Editorial Rooms. The Youth's Companion. Boston, Mass. Jan 14th, 1891 Dear Sir,

We are very glad you have sent "Ship Ahoy!" to The Companion, for it breathes a spirit which it is a pleasure to help in spreading, with our hearty thanks for it, in hope you will accept Messrs. Perry Mason & Co's cheque for fifteen dollars ($15.00) in payment for the manuscript. Copies of the paper containing the lines will be sent you when   they appear.

Believe us, Faithfully yours, The Editors. Walt Whitman Esq. 328 Mickle Street Camden. N.J.       see notes | april 10 | 1891
