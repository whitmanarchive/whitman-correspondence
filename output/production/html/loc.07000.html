  (1885) Wednesday Evn'g​ May 13

Aleck dear boy I cannot find "Locusts & Wild Honey" this moment—but let me lend you another of John Burroughs's books—(thought by some to be his best.)—"Birds and Poets"—hope it will please you & your mother and sister.

Your friend W W

Aleck boy here is a copy slip of my little new poem just out in Harpers' Weekly of May 16.
