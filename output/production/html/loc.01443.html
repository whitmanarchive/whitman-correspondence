  Spokane March 16 Walt Whitman Dear Brother

I pray that you may be well long ere this reaches you—Please write me aline​ a line​ that I may have your hand writing. I held a letter once yeares ago that you wrote to George Chany. I have since then be very disirous​ desirous​ to have   some writing from you before you go to your long home.

Fraternally your Mrs. L. Dillard Post Fall Idaho
