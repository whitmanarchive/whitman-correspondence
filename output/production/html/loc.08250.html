Camden New Jersey U S America Nov: 1 '90 Evn'g

Only a word to salute you & Wallace & all the friends—to say that matters go on with me much the same—& to enclose a printed slip, "Old Poets," my latest writing (age's garrulity I guess)—have been out in wheel chair this fine sunny cool afternoon—God bless you all—

Walt Whitman
