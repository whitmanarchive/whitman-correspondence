  Camden Dec: 24 '90

Y'rs rec'd: ab't the catheter &c:—thanks—Am feeling tolerably easy—bowel voidance—clear sunny weather here—

Horace is sending his piece to N E Magazine—I send MS "Some Personal Memoranda" & a poemet to Lippincott's intended for March—I have also sent (did I tell you?) some poetic stuff to Scribner's—McKay has paid me for the 100 big book sets sheets went to England—Enclose Dr J's letter came last evn'g—(good friends good set there in Bolton)—letter this mn'g f'm Ed Wilkins, welcomed—

Christmas at hand—(a sort of general prosperity & "intestinal agitation")—Looks fine out & I sh'l likely get in wheel chair—

God bless you all Walt Whitman
