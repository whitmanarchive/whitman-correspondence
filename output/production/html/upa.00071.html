Herb yours rec'd​ & welcomed—Nothing very new with me here—have enjoyed the past seven weeks hugely—but am quite ready to go back—You must be having first-rate times there—Bee was here to see me Monday—looks well—Eaton is still in Canada, I hear—Some artists here, last evening—(one of the Morans, & wife)—Herb I suppose you get the papers I send you—I am well—great Tom Moore Centenary here in N Y to night—I have been formally invited—but shall not attend—

W W
