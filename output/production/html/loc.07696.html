  Camden Aug: 29 '89

Am writing this just before sunset—feeling pretty fair—been requested by Alden the editor to write a piece for Harper's Monthly, & shall send it off this evn'g—If printed I shall either send it you or inform you—Dick Flynn came last evn'g, & he & Ed: are off to-day in Phila—I believe Dick goes hence Saturday—shall probably send the picture by him —just had to pay nearly $40 for taxes to the banditti who govern our city here —word from Burroughs yesterday—he is well—back in West Park—

Walt Whitman
