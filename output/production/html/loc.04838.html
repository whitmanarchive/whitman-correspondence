  Anderton, nr Chorley Lancashire, England 25. Feb 1892 Dear Walt,

Horace's letter of Feb 12th to hand with your loving message to us all & your special message to George Humphreys, Fred Wild & myself. Our deepest & tenderest love to you in return, & our prayerful good wishes.

Tomorrow night I expect that there will be   a good meeting at Johnston's which I shall attend. I shall have to do most of the talking & it will be about you. And our loving thoughts & blessing will go out to you, with our earnest best wishes.

I send a kiss to you again in token of tenderest love.

Your loving comrade & son Wallace
