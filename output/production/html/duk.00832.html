Camden Feb 22 '87—noon

Nothing further from O'Connor. See pp. 39 and 40 in Dr Buckes book ab't the wife & their hospitality to me—they had two children, a boy died in early childhood—the girl died not long since aged 22 or 3—a fine girl—I knew her quite well—I cant get over what you say ab't Trowbridge—tell me the particulars—I am sitting here in the little front room writing this—

Walt Whitman
