  Van Ness House L. S. Drew & H. N. Clark Managers U. A. Woodbury, Proprietor. Burlington, Vt. Feby 14 1890 Bro Walt.

I think that Han is on the gain,—Wm was in to day—thought she should improve more rapidly—but she has had a protracted, debilitating seige—where the Whitman stamina substantiated her—nothing like it—She had letter from Lou which pleased her very much—caused her to desire a visit to   to George—which would do her more good than tonics—said she would write to George—He might encourage he to come to Burlington, and stay with them some time—said you might talk with George, or Lou about it—she sits up, can make her own bed—and cook for herself, in her way. Ever grateful to you sturdy old friend—favouring us, (myself) when you can with a trifle—

Gratefully— Charlie
