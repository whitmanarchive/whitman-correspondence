Camden May 5 '89

The Frenchman Sarrazin's book came yesterday "Poésie Anglais"—tantalizing me a good deal, as I can just glean enough for a whiff only—Nothing special to write ab't in my affairs—dull here—fine & sunny too—Love to you & N[elly] & prayers for your ease & comfortableness—

Walt Whitman
