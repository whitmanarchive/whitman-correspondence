Camden New Jersey U S America June 26 '87—3 1/2 P M—

Edith & another girl have been to see me to-day—nearly two hours—a nice visit—they are well—appear to be enjoying their trip—we all liked them much—they have been to Niagara—I wrote you a while ago that the books had come, & that I liked the get up of them—I will send very soon some more stuff for the "Democratic Vistas,&c. &c." Vol. Fine summer weather—

Walt Whitman
