  431 Stevens st. cor West. Camden, N. Jersey. Oct. 15. My dear Marvin,

Your letter of 13th has reached me. Thanks to you—& thanks & best remembrances to your wife—for the kind invitation in it. I shall probably accept it for a few days, as I want to visit Washington soon as I am well enough. I should have made my visit the current week, but one of my bad spells has intervened—will write   to you before I come—I still linger along here in the same tedious baffling way—still hope to get well—but still don't get well.

Walt Whitman
