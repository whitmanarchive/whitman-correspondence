  Medical Superintendent's Office. INSANE ASYLUM LONDON ONTARIO 22 Feb 1891

Postcard of 19th from you to hand last evening. Was much relieved to hear that you were easier even if it is not very much so. I trust now that you will keep out of the deep ruts for a while and be able to look after the annex. Have you given up the idea of other folks writing in it? I hope so—Horace & I are busy planning a vol. of W.W. essays and I do not see why we should not carry it thro' at once—that would seem to me far the better course.

The first machine made meter is done & tested—it is perfect. I have "The New Spirit" have had it a year—the "W.W." is mostly good—has some bad shots in it

Best love always R M Bucke
