  see notes Aug 27 1888 Camden, Feb. 24 '75. My dear Linton;

I want you to have printed very nicely for me 1000 impressions of the cut, my head, to go in book. Herewith I send the size of sheet. If convenient I should like to see a proof, fac simile​ facsimile​ , first.

I am still holding out here—don't get well yet—& don't go under yet.

Love to you—Write immediately on receiving this.

This sized sheet—print dark in color as you think they will stand, (I dont​ don't​ like them too weak in color).

  to W. J. Linton Feb 24 '75
