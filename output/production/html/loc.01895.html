 loc.01895.001.jpg OFFICE OF SCOTT & WILLIAMS, BOOK AND JOB PRINTERS, 24 BEEKMAN STREET, New York, Aug 14th 1867 Walt Whitman Esq Dear Sir

We respectfully call your attention to the enclosed statement of a/c showing a ballance​ in our favor of $23.50. Your earliest attention will much oblige

Yours Respectfully Scott & Williams for Kent

P.S.

Any further orders you have will be thankfully received & promptly attended to—pleas​ send a copy of your Book for Mr. Sterne

S&W K
