Camden Aug: 27 '89

Am getting along pretty fairly. Nothing very new or marked—Ups & downs—the trend steadily the latter way as of course is to be expected. I believe I sent you "Poet-Lore" with the notice, & I suppose you have rec'd it before this—send me word when you next write—also what if anything I can do, or get & send—don't be afraid to request me—I hear f'm Dr B[ucke] often—he is well & busy—Ch's Eldridge is in St Francisco, no d[oub]'t as U S Revenue Ag't—I suppose you get the papers all right—the weather changes here cooler to-day—

Walt Whitman
