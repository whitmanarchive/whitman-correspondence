  Camden Feb: 6 '91

Much relieved to know you get over so easily f'm the bad little attack—am well pleas'd that Beemer sh'd have one of the pictures—physically much the same with me—a Californian magazine ("Overland") in an art. in wh' I am alluded to, just rec'd—will send it to you—dark & rainy mn'g but is clearing I think now.

Walt Whitman
