  March 27. Wed. Evn'g.

Wm. has had the best day to-day of any since the attacks last Sat. Has sat up all day, but gone to bed now at 7.30 very tired, & hopes to sleep better. We have dreadful nights & they wear one out. Sent the paper to Dr. Bucke to-day. Wm. read it again, as he wanted to.

Love from us both, N. O'C.
