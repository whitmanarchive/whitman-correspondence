 loc.00879.001.jpg New York May 23 Dear Sir

Enclosed find $20 which I have collected for yr Sanitary distribution. I hope I shall be able to send more from time to time.

I am yrs try W. E. Worthen1 Address care of  
  D. Appleton Ho.

Notes

  • 1. William E. Worthen was an engineer, evidently employed in the Croton Aqueduct Department, New York. Walt Whitman's reply to this letter is not known. [back]
