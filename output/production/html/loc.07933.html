Camden New Jersey U S America Aug: 15 '90

As I write am getting a little uneasy at not hearing f'm Dr J[ohnston]—no doubt he is all right & back there—send me a word immediately on getting this—he did not visit Dr Bucke, Canada (wh' Dr B regrets much.) I send you my last screed in Critic N Y, (have sent other papers too)—Am getting along fairly considering—fair secretions & excretions—Memories & best respects to you & all—

Walt Whitman
