  Ye Painte Shoppe, 1833 SPRUCE STREET PHILADELPHIA. Sunday My dear Mr. Whitman

I enclose the only one of the Herald contributions I have recently seen. The one a week ago today I missed and have not yet got a copy.

Mrs. Louise Chandler Moulton who is in town is very anxious to present herself to you and unless you are not feeling well enough to see anyone, I shall bring her over tomorrow afternoon at about 3. If you are not quite up to it, you must telegraph me as above, "collect" in the forenoon.

With love believe me Yours Talcott Williams   T. Williams   Mrs T. Williams
