431 Stevens street Camden New Jersey U S America Oct 11 '82 Dear Miss Ford

Yours of Sept: 25th rec'd​ —& accordingly I send you (same mail with this—same address) my two volumes—poetic and prose. The price is one pound sterling (which includes the books, postage, & everything)—send to me here by P. O. order—

When you see Edward Carpenter tell him I am well & hearty (considering) & send him my love—

Walt Whitman
