Camden Feb: 7 '88

Nothing new or special, I am ab't the same—quite frequent visitors—weather dark & moist & snowy, not very cold—I believe Rhys is to lecture to-night before the N Y Century Club—a week from to-night before the Phila: Contemporary—no letters have come here for you—I have just sent L of G. to Andrew Carnegie.

Walt Whitman
