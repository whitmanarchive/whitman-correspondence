  Camden noon Aug:8 '89

Feeling pretty well—sitting here in 2d story den—rec'd a letter from Logan Smith, they are all down in Surrey—Haslemere—all well—hope you have got the two copies little pocket bound L of G I sent—letter f'm you yesterday—Ed went over to "Chimes of Normandy" Eng: Opera last evn'g—good—Herbert Gil: here last evn'g—superb weather here, this the third day.

Walt Whitman
