  14 March 91 Kind, dear good Friend and Bro Walt—March 18.

March! March away!—19.

Cold—frozen lake—bitter—sharp relentless wind—South—but no sunny-genial south here—

Han decidedly improving—is having her breakfast now—all alone—as she desires—toast bread—poachd​ poached​ eggs—splendid milk! allways​ always​ stimulates her—to have a letter from you—It would do her—encourage her, if Lou would write to her—send her a trifle—I am—or continue to paint pictures—tho I am straind​ strained​ to purchase canvass​ canvas​ etc. Shall sell them some—if but for a dollar a day—Wonderfull​ Wonderful​ to all how you keep us—immortal like—

Charlie
