Dover, Delaware, April 4, 1875 Dear Friend,

Again I take the time & privlege​ privilege​ of droping​ dropping​ a few lines to tell you that we have not forgotten you & want to hear from you. We have had a son borned​ born​ since we heard from you & We call him Walter Whitman Millis in honer​ honor​ to you for Love for you.

How I would like to see your dear old face once more. Times is very hard here, & money is very scarce. Hoping to hear from you soon.

We remain yours with love
