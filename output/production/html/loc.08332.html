  Century Club 7. W. 43rd St. New York. May 6, 1891 My Dear Sir

I hope you will allow me to come & have a chat with you for the Pall Mall Gazette, for which paper I am one of the chief writers. I could come to you about the beginning of June. I go to see Dr O. W. Holmes, & Mr Russell Lowell by invitation next week.

Faithfully Yours Raymond Blathwayt
