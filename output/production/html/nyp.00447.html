Camden Oct:​ 25 Evn'g​ :

Quite sick the last ten or twelve days—havn't been out of the house—great bodily weakness & "misery" in head & stomach—the doctor says from almost entire stoppage of the action of the liver, other causes—malarial &c—I dont hear any thing particular. Do you? Did they send you the Boston Herald's two criticisms on S. D.—very warm, eulogistic (largely extracts)—I shall be all right in a week or less—

W W
