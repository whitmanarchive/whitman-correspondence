  Tuesday Evn'g Sept: 1 '91— Camden

Nothing very new—sun shining to day & cool here—have got down stairs every day for the last three afternoons wh'​ is quite a feat—sold a big book—rice pudding in old yellow earthen dish (& brot​ up in same) for my supper—visitors (two little boys to–day, dear little fellows)—Am looking for Dr Bucke on his return f'm​ England—Mrs: O'Connor is here visiting a friend—here yesterday—I am half blind & deaf—Love to you & God bless you sister dear

Walt Whitman
