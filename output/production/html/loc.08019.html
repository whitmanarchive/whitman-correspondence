  Camden Noon March 29 '91

Still keep up (but it is a heavy pull part of the time)—No worse. Dr L comes, & is valuable to me—dark glum weather all the time—proofs of "Good-Bye" get slowly on—have sent back to 31st page —there will not be much more than 40 unless I put Sarrazin &c: in an appendix (wh' I believe I sh'l not, as you and H T are decidedly against)—Suppose you rec'd the full proofs of the L of G. 2d annex I sent—am having distress in head to-day—

God bless you all Walt Whitman
