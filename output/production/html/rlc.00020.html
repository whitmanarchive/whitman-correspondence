Camden New Jersey U S America Dec: 24 '89

Still (after a sort) hold possession of the ship—but my grasp growing fainter & my eyes dimmer—Wish to specially write to thank you for kindness—Y'r proof just rec'd by Horace Traubel—To-day a fine sunny day & I have been taken out two hours on a drive—enjoy'd all—the sun most of all—been to a cemetery to select a lot.

Walt Whitman
