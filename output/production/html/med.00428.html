Tuesday May 20th 1 Dear Doctor—

I will be happy to receive a visit from Mrs. Bielby—will be in my room to-morrow, Wednesday, from 10 to 11 ½ forenoon—Will always be happy to have visits from you or Mrs. B.

Walt Whitman
