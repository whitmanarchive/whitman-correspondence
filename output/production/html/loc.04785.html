  Camden Feb: 10 '91 Dear sister

Am sitting here pretty much same—got in a new cord of saw'd​ & split oak wood, so I keep warm enough—sold a book yesterday—Heard f'm​ Ed this mn'g​ —he is well & hearty—Been a long dark rain storm, three days & nights here, but the sun is out this mn'g​ .

—Am at my 2d annex for L​ of G.​ to complete it—God bless you, sister dear, 2 enclosed—

Walt Whitman
