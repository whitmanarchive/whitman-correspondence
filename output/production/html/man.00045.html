  Oakenholt Hall nr​ Flint: England 2nd July 1880 Dear Walt Whitman

I am very grateful for your kindness in sending me so many interesting papers from time to time. The article on Emerson has touched me very nearly. It is great and good—

My object in writing now is merely to thank you for your thoughtfulness & to call your attention to my change of address.

With affection & respect Herbert J. Bathgate
