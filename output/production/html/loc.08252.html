Camden NJ US America Nov: 18 '90.

Y'rs & J W W[allace]'s letters rec'd & welcomed—I am here yet & much the same way—Still the grip & the bladder trouble—am writing a little—just sent off a little thanksgiving poemet for NY paper (ordered)—the Ing[ersoll] lecture is to be printed in book & I will send you one—H Traubel here daily & invaluable & kind—hear f'm Dr B[ucke]—

affectionate regards to you & Wallace & all— Walt Whitman
