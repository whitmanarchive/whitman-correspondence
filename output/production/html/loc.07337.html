  Superintendent's Office Asylum 
 for the Insane Ontario London, Ont., 29 Jan 1890

I have a line from Osler this moment. In answer to mine asking if they would look after you in Johns Hopkins. I enclose the note. I do not understand all of it fully but this much is clear that for about $25. a week (which it seems to me we could easily afford) you could be accommodated and provided with every thing—of course before taking any step we would find out every thing and I would visit Johns Hopkins myself—I will write again tomorrow when I get Osler's further letter promised in this one

R M Bucke
