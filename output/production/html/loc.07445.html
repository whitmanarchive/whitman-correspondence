  MEMORANDUM.  
 From WHITELAW REID, The Tribune Office, New York, May 29 1879 To Walt Whitman Esq. 1309 Fifth Ave near 86th St. Dear Sir—,

Mr. Reid directs me to enclose your cheque on our Cashier, for $45 for the three letters referred to in your note of the 28th.

I am, Respectfully D. Nicholson
