  Camden Dec: 5 '90 My dear JMS

I like y'r plan & feel greatly complimented by it & hope it will be carried out forthwith say in the February number. Of course I will do whatever I can—remember what I said ab't the printerial proportion & look. Send me soon a printed slip of the pieces you have, so I can make a nice flush page.

I send you herewith the MS of personal article (must not have my name signed) Of course if you print it send me good proof—The price w'd be $20 & I reserve the right to print in future book if I wish & shall want 25 numbers of the Mag:

  Personal to J M S
