  Woodbine Upton Park Chester. England. "The Time is short." 19th January 1891 Dear Walt Whitman,

I was much pleased to receive the newspaper you so kindly sent to me with yr. beautiful little poem. Accept my sincere best thanks. I notice you are about to issue a new Book—so some of my Literary Papers here say.

Where shall I be   be able to get it & when?

It is not yet too late to wish you a year of happiness & peace.

With a disciples gratitude & affectionate regard Believe me, Faithfully Edmund J. Baillie
