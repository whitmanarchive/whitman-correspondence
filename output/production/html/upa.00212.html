  152 A. Ln. N.E., Washington, D.C.: Wednesday, Nov. 24th 1880. Dear Sir,

I recieved the two volumes of your works yesterday by mail. I have had a copy of them for some years, but I wished to send one to a friend in Scotland.

Shall your many friends here have the pleasure of seeing you in Washington this winter?

With good wishes, Yours Faithfully, David Hutcheson.
