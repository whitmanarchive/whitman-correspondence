  Camden Wednesday noon April 27 My dear Jeannie

Yours just rec'd here sent on from Boston—Yes, I will give you a page or more of Boston notes—will send them on by mail to-morrow night—so you will get them Friday morning & be able   to send me a proof Saturday afternoon & I will return Sunday night, so you will get it Monday morning—

Walt Whitman
