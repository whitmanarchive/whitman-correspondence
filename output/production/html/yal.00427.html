Camden New Jersey Oct 23

I am well, for me—Have been down at the old Jersey farm by the pond nearly all summer—start again to-morrow—Am writing some—Shall come to N Y to lecture—Al: I got your letter down in the country four days after due—I return the Goethe Club ticket—

W W
