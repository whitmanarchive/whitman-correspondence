  Camden NJ Feb: 16 '89

Am still here pretty well considering but rigorously imprison'd in the sick room—good heart but entirely disabled—printing my final & complete editions all done—The news f'm Wm is bad (I get word from N occasionally)—he is room-fast & weak—sits up part of the time but does not write—I think hourly ab't him—it is gloomy—am uncertain whether you know ab't him or not. I expect Dr B here next Wednesday.

Walt Whitman
