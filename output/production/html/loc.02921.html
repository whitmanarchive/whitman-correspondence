  Sat. '88 Good morning!

I see a Mr Gardner of Paisley Scotland has filched J. W. Wilson's Dr. Bucke's W.W. (with Dowden's tail—[illegible] & is to bring it out at once. Hartmann is figuring as teacher of dramatics (latest). Scrawl from Baxter who is in Tempe, Arizona with Hemenway Exploration Expedition.

aff. son (in the spirit) W.S.K.
