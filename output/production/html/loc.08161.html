  R.M.S. "BRITANNIC." 7 a.m. Wednesday 8th July

We are off, dear Walt, in a few minutes. I was glad to see Warry yesterday—many thanks for the L. of G. tho of course I had a copy with me, would not think of going so far without one.

 

I have this moment received a cablegram from Johnston & Wallace of Bolton to say that they will write me to Queenstown—they are wonderful fellows, I shall enjoy seeing them immensely

Love to you always R M Bucke   see notes July 16 1891
