Camden Jan: 26 late Evn'g [1888]

I have look'd over the MS &c—hardly made any emendations—Shall send it back soon—y'r card dated 24th rec'd—What do you mean ab't E R[hys]?—write more fully & plainly—I am ab't as usual—very cold here—It is most 10 & I am going off to bed—

W W
