  Kents Corner Well Road Hampstead Dec 13th 1880. My dear Walt

Mother has been very ill; but is a little better now: & with grt​ care will I hope be very much better. You will do me a kind service if you will receive this money order for payment of duty I havent the remotest idea how much it is & if you will let me know the difference I will remit it. I should be glad if Mrs Stafford could have the book by Xmas day.

Herbert H Gilchrist
