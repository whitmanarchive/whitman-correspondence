  May 18. 89 Dear Walt:

Look out for Stepniak's remark on you in to-day's Transcript." L. of G. is one of his favorites, he says. How pitiful is Howells' recent depreciation of Walter Scott. Who is Howells I shd like to know, to judge the mighty Walter Scot?

W. S. K. See Notes May 1889
