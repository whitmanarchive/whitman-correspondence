Camden Nov: 29 '90

Yr's & J W W[allace]'s kind letters rec'd—thanks—have sent the L of G.to Rev: Mr: Johnstone—Still ab't the same—catarrhal bad head and abdomen—cold & sunny weather—have been out at mid day an hour in wheel chair—Sad & gloomy days here—death of my brother T. J. W. at St Louis Missouri—

Walt Whitman
