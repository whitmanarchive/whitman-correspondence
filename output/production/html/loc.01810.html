  3. GARDENCOURT TEMPLE.E.C. : London. Jan. 5. 1869. Dear Mr. Whitman,

I cannot find room for the poem wh​ you have been so obliging as to send me, before the April Number of the Review. If that be not too late for you, and if you can make suitable arrangements for publication in the United States so as not to interfere with us in point of time, I shall be very glad. Perhaps you will let me know as early as you can.

With very kind regards— Always your's sincerely John Morley.   note from Mr. Morley Jan. 5 '69. ans. Jan 20 '69 see notes May 27, 88
