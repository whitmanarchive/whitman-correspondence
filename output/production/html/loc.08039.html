Camden May 8 P M '91

Still hold the fort, but it is pretty tough elusive business. No bowel action for a long while. Dr L[ongaker] is sick, has not been here in a fortnight—my grip runs dominant & riot—How are you getting along? Have you had the N[ew] E[ngland] Magazine? I rec'd one day before yesterday, & can send it you—

Walt Whitman
