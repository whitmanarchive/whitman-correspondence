  Camden May 5 P M '91

Bad days—bad condition. Was taken out to Harleigh yesterday, had to be led assisted every movement, to see the tomb—it is well advanced—is a hefty very unornamental affair not easily described—is satisfactory to me & will be to you—some pieces stone weigh 6 or 8 tons—splendid weather—Mrs. D & Warry good to me—Dr L sick & home

W W
