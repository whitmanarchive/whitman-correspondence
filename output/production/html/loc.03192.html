  540 E. 155th In New York Dear W.:

The letter, you sent me, was from your French admirer Sarrazin, who will soon publish an essay on L. o. G. in one of his books. Of the 'Grashalme' I, so far, rec'd only one copy, but expect some more. I hope, the book will be received favorably by the critics.

Yours ty Karl Knortz
