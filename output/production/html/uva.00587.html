Camden July 3 '88—P M

I am sitting up & feeling pretty well—the weather is fine now (after coolish & cloudy) & I have not retrograded—Congratulate yr father & Alys after their safe & favorable arrival wh' I have heard, & sent to Dr Bucke—As I sit early afternoon every thing is quiet & comfortable—I have not yet left my room—

Walt Whitman
