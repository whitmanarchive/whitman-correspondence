Camden N J—U S America March 14 Evn'g '91

First, thanks for y'r affectionate fervid letter of 3d wh' I have read twice & absorb'd—dont expect much of the little 2d annex—it is very brief & most of it you have seen already—then it is bro't out in sickness & g't depression—Sorry I have no cheery or favorable news to send of physique—am up and abt to eat a bite of supper—

Walt Whitman
