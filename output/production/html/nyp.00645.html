Camden Feb: 27 '89

So-so with me—not as well even as usual—Dr B[ucke] is full of the meter business (the invention patented & company forming) —Am sitting here alone by the oak fire—dark & rain & hail all day—

Dr B will probably come on to W[ashington] (some days hence) in the morning & leave the same evening—

Always best sympathy & love to you & N[elly]— W W
