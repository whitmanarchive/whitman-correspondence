  Kirkwood N.J.​ Nov​ 21–77 Friend Walt

I received your letter this a.m. was glad to hear from you. Father is a gereat​ great​ deel​ deal​ better to day, he is going out a little he came out yesterday to see me and Homer kill the pig, but he felt so bad he had to go right back to bed again when he got in. I am not feeling very well nor haven't for a week nearly. I   have the hed ache​ headache​ all the time, and have had it for a week, I caught cold somehow, I don't know how though. I wish you would bring me down a coppy​ copy​ book, Spencerian if you can find it, No 8, and about 6 pens of the same kind. I will be much oblidged​ obliged​ to you if you will. You write and let me know how you are. Received the box all right Monday eve.

Yours Truly, Harry Stafford
