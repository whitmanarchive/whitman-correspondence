Camden N J—U S America Evn'g Aug: 16 '91

Warm yet but not as severe—It has pull'd me down badly, but keep around much as usual—sitting here, sunset—

Aug: 17—Rumor (not confirmed) that Whittier is severely ill—Y'r letter of leaving & return to B[olton] rec'd.—A bad night (insomnia &c:) last—W'd you (or w'd Wallace) care for the W W clay head at 40 Grosvenor r'd London? If so I will send an order to give it you—

Walt Whitman
