 Proprietor. Burlington, Vt. 28 May 1890 Bro Walt,

Han rec'd your last, with 2 dollars. She is too nervous to write—far from being well—I think sometimes that she may not be herself again—she is weak—very—very excitable—I do not want to give up, but I do not see how I can maintain myself—your kind sending helps much however—for which I am gratefull​ grateful​ —

Charlie,
