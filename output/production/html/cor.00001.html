  328 Mickle street Camden New Jersey April 13 '86 My Dear Prof: Carson

Yours rec'd—The copy of John Burroughs's Notes I send to you by same mail with this.—The price is $1 which you may just enclose in envelope & mail to me here—I too enjoy'd the young man's Woodruff's visit to me & talk—& hoped to have seen you—I am ab't the same in health &c—(nothing at all to brag of)—I read publicly my Death of Abraham Lincoln next Thursday in Phila:

Walt Whitman
