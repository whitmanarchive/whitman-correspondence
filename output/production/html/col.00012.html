  328 Mickle Street Camden Oct 12 pm My dear Williams

After I sent your note in answer yesterday—& reading yours again—I have perpetrated this—do I understand you are to have a long varied Blaine melange like in sections—the Presidential canvas business, of course? If so you might find a spot in the course of it all, where this screed may come in—

—If that don't suit you, or is not practicable (though I hope it will be, as I should like it just as well) print it by itself, like the little piece of Friday last.

—I will call about 10. this evening to see proof.

Whitman
