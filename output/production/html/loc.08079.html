Camden N J: Sunset Sept: 10 '91

Perfect weather to-day & hope you & W[allace] have had the benefit of it—nothing very different in my health & feelings—have just eaten my supper with fair zest—Horace here this even'g—Warry just ret'd from a three hours jaunt over in Phil shipping—days shortening markedly—

Love to you all— Walt Whitman
