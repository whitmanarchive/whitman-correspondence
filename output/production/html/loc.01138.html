  5 East 65th Street New York City Evn'g​ Aug​ 3d​

Your postal of 29th rec'd​ —I am here for a few days, after spending a week down on Long Island, mostly at West Hills and Cold Spring, my parents' places of nativity—& my own place. Dr Bucke has been with me—he has return'd​ to Canada—I am about as usual—I go on to B.​ before long, about the book—

W W
