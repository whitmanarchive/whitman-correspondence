  Camden Nov: 4 '91

Bright cool Nov: day—Stoddart (Lippincott's) & a Californian girl just here—a great pot of yellow chrysanthemums—& John Russell Youngs big "Round the World" by express—bad-feeling day with me—head, gastric & bladder—Wallace left NY. this mn'g—Arnold also f'm here—

W W
