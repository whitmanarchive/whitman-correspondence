  Medical Superintendent's Office. INSANE ASYLUM LONDON ONTARIO 20 Sept 1891

One more good day! Warm, bright, full of beauty and cheer! Thanks for "Literary World" with the notice (a good one) of "Goodbye." I have sent Horace a bundle of Photo's of myself all taken by Dr Johnston at Bolton—some groups & some just Wallace & self—all (I think) good. We are having a good time here, good talks, good drives, good meals—lots of splendid vegetables and fruits, corn tomatoes, very fine potatoes, melons, pears and all the rest of it. Tell Horace to give you that Bolton Speech of mine to read and tell me what you think about it!

So long, dear Walt R M Bucke
