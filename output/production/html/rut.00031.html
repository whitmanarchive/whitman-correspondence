Camden Feb: 11 '91 near noon

Bright sunlight out—Am feeling fairly—send you a couple slips of the Dutch piece—it is the best thing of its kind yet—H T[raubel]'s paper will be out in four days & you can have as many as you want or more slips either—Yes Ernest Rhys was married in London early last month—If I get letter or details, I will send you—Dr B[ucke] is recovered fully—is well—I see James Redpath is dead in NY—Y'r letter rec'd—

Walt Whitman
