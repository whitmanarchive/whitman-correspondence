Camden N J—U S America Nov: 3 '91

Sunny cool day—Wallace went hence this mn'g well & in good spirits to take City of Berlin f'm N Y to morrow mn'g—Sir Edwin Arnold & others here yesterday—all went well—A is being recepted here finely—he is evidently one of my warmest & solid friends—I continue ab't same as before—Y'rs rec'd—thanks—

Walt Whitman
