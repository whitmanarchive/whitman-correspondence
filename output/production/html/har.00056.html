431 Stevens Street Camden New Jersey U S America1 March 21 '80

I send today same address as this card, my Vol:​ Two Rivulets (as requested by Mr Rossetti)—Please let me know soon as it reaches you safely2—

Walt Whitman

I have also mailed to you a little photograph—

Notes

  • 1. This letter is addressed: Frederick Locker | 25 Chesham Street | London S W | England. It is postmarked: Camden | Mar | 23 | N.J.; Philad'a, Pa. | Mar | 23 | Paid All. [back]
  • 2. Locker-Lampson acknowledged receipt of the book on April 7 (Thomas Donaldson, Walt Whitman the Man [New York: Francis P. Harper, 1896], 236–237). [back]
