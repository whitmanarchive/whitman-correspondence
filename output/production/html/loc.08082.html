  Camden Evn'g: Jan: 15 '91

Feeling fairly after two very bad days & nights—ate my supper with relish—many visitors to-day—Herb: Gil: Stoddart & five others—Arthur Stedman &c: &c:—Horace T just here—the March No. of Lip: will indeed be Whitmanesque—am sitting here writing by a little tallow candle—clear but a storm preparing
