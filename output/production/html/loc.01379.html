  Camden, NJ America Sunday Evng Sept: 2 '88

Your good letter just rec'd & here I am sending word back—still imprisoned in my sick room—non-rehabilitated yet but middling well for all that—my booklet November Boughs ab't finish'd—& a large vol. comprising all my stuff begun—I am here just at sunset—Love to you all old & young—I sufficiently comfortable

Walt Whitman
