  Printing & Publishing House OF A. SIMPSON & CO., No. 60 Duane Street. New-York, July 3, 1867. Walt Whitman Esq. Dear Sir

While considering the question of publishing "Leaves of Grass" &c we have consulted with several eminent literary men and they have advised us most strongly not to do it.

In view of this fact, though we are favorably impressed, as we were when we first wrote you, with the idea, we deem it injudicious to commit ourselves to its publication at the present time.

With the highest regards Yrs​ very truly A Simpson & Co
