Camden April 22 '89

Decidedly the best news to-day is the saving of the wreck'd Danmark's 750 passengers & crew—out from the very jaws of death!

Fine sunny weather here—Every thing continuing on the same with me—Letter from Dr B[ucke]. He has rec'd the S[tedman] letter & likes it well—I am entirely pleased with the presentation (selections & portrait) S makes of me—

Walt Whitman
