  Camden March 6 PM '90

The sun out & fine this afternoon—but we have had a dark cold storm two days—I keep on ab't same as usual—in-doors these times—my MS returned f'm the Nineteenth Century—Shall find some other market perhaps—(if not, shall print in a little another Annex—for I contemplate such)—have had my mid-day massage—am sitting here at the table in my den—good oak fire—

Walt Whitman
