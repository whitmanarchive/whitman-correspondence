Washington, March 13, 1865 My dear Sir:

Would you do me the favor, if convenient, immediately on receiving this, of enclosing the sketch by Capt. Sims, and directing it, Capt. George W. Whitman, Portland Av. near Myrtle, Brooklyn New York—

How are you getting along in New York and how is your brother, Major Wright?—I send him best regards. I am still here in Washington, and nothing new.

Yours, etc.

Walt Whitman
