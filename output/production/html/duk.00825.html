328 Mickle street Camden N J Oct: 16 '86 P M

Yours of 14th rec'd by midday mail—Good roots (as the N Y boys used to say) to your venture—I havn't heard from O'C[onnor] in quite a while—I fear he is, medically, in a bad way—I am sailing along ab't as usual—have just had my light dinner—Cool & raw weather here—my canary is singing blithely, as I write—

Walt Whitman
