Camden, March 25 '74 To: J. C. Mann Tuft's College Box 55 College Hill, Mass.

Your letter of the 20th (for committee) has reached me here. I accept with pleasure the invitation to deliver a poem to your college & visitors next June. Without any thing very definite at this moment, my idea is of a poem, fitting in not unappropriately​ inappropriately​ to such an occasion, the recital of which would not occupy more than 30 minutes. All the pay I would want would be enough to pay my expenses, transportation &c., probably between 30 and $40, certainly not more than the latter sum. Please acknowledge this & please write me more particulars about your college, where it is, specialties, if any &c. I am stopping here, not entirely well, but able to get around, recovering from an attack of paralysis of over a year ago.

W. W.
