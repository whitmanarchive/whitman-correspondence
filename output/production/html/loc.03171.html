  Trans. Office— Frid Dear Walt

I did not realize that you were so ill. Accept deep sympathy

Nor did I realize that the booklet was so far along Yes, the lines appeared in Youth's Companion—4 lines.

Wifekin sends love & sympathy.

I am quite worn by the hard winter & by work; but am so so.

I must send you a jug of orange wine—three years old—when I get round to it. I see O'Connor's Android has place of honor in Atlantic monthly just out. I must read it.

affec. WS Kennedy
