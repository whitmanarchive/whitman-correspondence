  Camden p m April 12 '90

Grip continued bad as ever. Continued heavy headache—ate my breakfast—am sitting up most of the time—rest very poorly—still calculate on this grip trouble passing over, but not in time for my 15th lecture in Phila:—I have now sent you word by letter or card every day the last week, wretched week for me—Tom Donaldson here last evn'g—

¼ to 5—Headache easier—have just eat a fair supper—weather fine—

Walt Whitman
