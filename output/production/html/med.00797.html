Camden, N.J., April 9, 1887.

Yours rec'd.—Yes, I am (expect to be) going on to N. Y. to deliver my piece April 14th. Dr. and Mrs. Bucke are not coming on—rather think I shall return here next day. I am not very well, but much ab't as usual. Wm. O'C[onnor] is nearly the same as at last acct's—no worse.

Walt Whitman
