  8 Oct. '90 Bro Walt

Studio, now, over the kitchen—head of stairs—Octr 8.—Miserable, cold, damp weather—

Han about the same: has made a couple of visits, near by, to old acquaintances— who congratulated her—on coming out. Hard times, however. The 5 dollars you sent, put by until yesterday—recieved [illegible] dollars from Lou—I added 25 cents—all I had—and had a ton of coal sent up and fixed for winter—wood for the Kitchen stoves, expires in about 2 days—where shall we get a couple of dollars then, to replenish? You write that you wish Han was near You—she would like it—

Charlie
