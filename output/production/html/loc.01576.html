March 22, '68  
 (Went 24th) Ed. Routledge

In my note of January 17 last, sending "Whispers of Heavenly Death," I included a request for thirty copies of the magazine, containing the piece, whenever printed. If agreeable therefore, please request the New York house to send by express, at my expense, thirty copies of the "Broadway" containing the piece, to my address, Attorney Gen's office, here.

I propose, before long, to submit to you another piece, for the magazine, if approved.
