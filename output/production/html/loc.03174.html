  Dear W.W.

I always forget to say that while several times in my MS I [illegible]seem to have ignored yr notes, in reality I [illegible] them all. Where I wrote "omit," I have embodied what you wrote elsewhere. Hope you are well as usual, or rather no worse, dear friend. I tho't yr poem in Critic strong in diction, & Bucke's Jubilate good. I think Howells has something on the big vol. in Feb. Harpers. Just wrote Burroughs 7 deg. above here today Sunday.

W S K
