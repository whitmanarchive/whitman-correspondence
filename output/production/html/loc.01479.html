  Camden Tuesday Evng. Aug: 14—'88 Dear TD—

Am here yet in the sick room—the Doctor thinks it best not to go yet—have had no set back—but remain horribly weak in legs & body—(sometimes wonder whether that would remain for settled)—Thanks for the Catlin—it is an inexhaustable mine

Walt Whitman   Written Aug 11/88 T.D. by Walt Whitman
