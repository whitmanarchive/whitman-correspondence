  Camden Evn'g Oct: 31 '90

Horace has ret'd safely & was here last evn'g—Have signatured the books and wrapt them safely to be express'd back to you—suppose they will go forthwith—grip on me badly—headache & congestion—slim mail with me—have been out to day in wheel chair—clear, colder weather—small miserable local politics & elections Penn: and N.J. now—a fight of crows & kites—("intestinal agitation") y'rs recd—

God bless you all Walt Whitman
