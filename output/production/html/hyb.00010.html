  431 Stevens Street Camden New Jersey Feb 21 Dear Bee

Your letter rec'd​ , & we all read it with interest—my sister sends love—We are all about the same—I am continuing well for me—To day is very clear, but cold & windy—I have been out some two hours enjoying it—cross'd the   river—The Staffords, (with the exception of Mr H who has a spell) are well as usual—Harry comes up to see me occasionally—

—A note from your mother yesterday forwarding the enclosed letter of Rossetti's which she wished me to post to you—So far my rheumatic (neuralgic) attack keeps off—But I am not out of the woods till April sets in—

W.W.
