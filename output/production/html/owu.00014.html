  Camden Evn'g Dec: 13 '89

All goes on the same & fairly. Have been out in the sun & mild temperature a good part of afternoon. Sent on out a little poemet (welcoming Brazilian Republic) to McClure's N Y Syndicate—& rec'd money for it. So Browning is dead—(I have never read much B & don't have any inherent opinion)—How are you & Mrs: K?

Walt Whitman
