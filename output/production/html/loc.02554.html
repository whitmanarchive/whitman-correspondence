  Dec​ 20 '76 My dear Johnston

Enclosed find check for $35 for which send me on immediately as good a gold watch, hunting case, middling showy in appearance & best inside you can give me for that sum. (Let me have it at wholesale price, only paying yourself what you pay for it). I want it for a Christmas present for a young man— Can't you send it by express to-night or tomorrow morning early?

Walt Whitman 431 Stevens st cor West Camden New Jersey

I might as well leave you some margin. If you think of something a little more price or different any how, better, send it along, as it can be changed, paid for, or made right when I come on in January—

I find I have no revenue stamp to put on check & cannot go out to get one—
