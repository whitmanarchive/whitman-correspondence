  Camden June 18 1890

Y'rs of 14th rec'd this mn'g. I continue fairly—go out in wheel chair every day—get along well, all things consider'd—have massage every day—do not write any thing since that Century rejection (do not feel to)—good letters from Col. Ingersoll to me—my nurse is down stairs practising on his fiddle—he gets along well—

Walt Whitman
