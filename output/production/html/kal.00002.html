  Esopus N.Y. April 4, 1876 Dear Walt:

I wrote a letter to the Tribune the other day touching you & your matters & if they publish it I wish you would send me the paper as I do not see the daily except I am away from home. I saw their editorial of Tuesday & was much incensed by it. I presume it is Bayard Taylor's  hand.

I am on the sick list now from quinsy soar throat & am very distressed. I wish you would drop me a line. I wrote to you a week or two ago.

Affectionately John Burroughs
