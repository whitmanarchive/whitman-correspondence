  Bridgevill​ Bridgeville​ Del Jan 20th 1868 Dear Uncle

I take this oppertuneity​ opportunity​ of giveing​ giving​ you this as an excuse for not comeing​ coming​ up to see you again before Leveing​ leaving​ the city. on the same evening I was taken with a very severe Chill​ chill​ and the morning I was so sick that I could not think of staying in the city an hour longer. I come home and have been sick every​ ever​ since. My arm has come out in a sore were​ where​ one of the balls struck it it is so bad that it gives me much pain that I cant rest but the Dr thinks it will   be well again soon Father is very sick at this time.

Uncle if I were to send you an aplication​ application​ for a position in the Interior​ Interior​ Department with recommendation will you please put them in for me and see the Asst.​ Secretary & urge him to give me a posison​ position​ I have some recomdation​ recommendation​ from Curtain of Pa & my Captain. I will stop with the hope of hearing from you soon.

Sister wishes to be remembered to you

Your Humble Servant Wm H. Millis Jr.
