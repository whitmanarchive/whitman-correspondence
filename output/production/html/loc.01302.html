    328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey U S America Aug 24, '86—

Yours of Aug. 8 rec'd—I send (same mail with this, same address) a copy of John Burroughs's book— & can send another. "Leaves of Grass," author's special ed'n—& "Specimen Days" would be £1—s2 (one pound, two shillings [—] the two Vols.)—Sent best by post office order to me here. Before you decide to reprint J B's little book write me—there are several things I sh'd like to post you up on—

Walt Whitman
