Camden N J— Feb: 4 '91

Sh'd like to have (for our own uses to send about this country & to Europe) 100 printed impressions (the whole stuff) of the poems & my own & H Traubel's piece—so that we c'd have them stitched if we like. Of course we will pay the printers. Horace T. will hand you this & will arrange & boss the thing, if you folks are agreeable (as I suppose there is no reason but what you sh'd be.)

Walt Whitman
