  November th dear Walt

i am well and i hope you are the Same old man. Would love to see you once moor​ more​ for it seems an age Since i last met With you down at the pond and a lovely time we had of it to​ too​ old man. i would like to Com​ come​ up Som​ some​ Saterday​ Saturday​ afternoon and Stay all night With you and Com​ come​ home on the Sunday morning train. i love you Walt and Know that my love is returned so i will Close

from your friend Edward P. Cattell
