  Camden Sunday noon Nov: 23 '90.

Enclosed two copies my latest f'm Lippincott's Dec—Nothing very new—Cold weather grip & bladder bother continued—physically uncomfortable enough—sunny today though—steady belly ache—fixed comfortably enough here (surroundings &c)—word f'm Dr B every other day he is all right—Horace T every evn'g (welcome)—Stanley's companion & secretary (Hamilton Aide) has been over to see me—Talcott Williams comes—I sit here by oak fire every thing quiet.

Walt Whitman
