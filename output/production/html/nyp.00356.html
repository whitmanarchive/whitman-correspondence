431 Stevens st.​  
 cor West.  
 Camden, N.​ Jersey, Jan.​ 7.

Am feeling better than during Nov.​ and first part of Dec​ —strength better than for a long time—rec'd​ the Springfield Rep[ublican] this morning—(is it from you or Ch[arley]?)—That ¶ in the T[ribune] was the most flourishing puff yet given me—& from them!—A leaden, heavy day here, with sulky rain & some snow & sleet—I have to stay in, but am feeling comfortable.

WW
