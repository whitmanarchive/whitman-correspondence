  72 East 11th St. N.Y., Sept 26. 1888. My Dear Walt Whitman:

May I hope that you are better than the papers say? I keep you ever in mind as I saw you last at the Westminister nearly two years since.

Mrs Hinton desires me to send her warmest regards. Can you write me word or two in reply.

Always with loving Friendship yours Richard J. Hinton

Your last published poem on Old Age is simply superb

  Hinton
