  Asylum for the Insane, London, 9th May 1883 My dear Walt

I returned home last evening and found plate proofs to the end of book and two post cards waiting me. I have been over the proofs and enclose some requiring corrections if these can still be made (or perhaps you have already made them). I like the book better and better, think it will do—As soon as you are able to fix date of publication let me know. I am up to my eyes in work

R M Bucke
