Camden N J U S America April 7 '91

Y'rs rec'd to-day—thanks—fine sunny spring-like day out—keeping on much the same—no worse I guess—Have you seen my dead friend O'Connor's story "The Bronzoid Android" com[mence]d in April Atlantic monthly?

Am sitting here (listless & stupid as a great log) in my den—take medicine every day—God's blessings on you & Dr. [Johnston]—& my love—

Walt Whitman
