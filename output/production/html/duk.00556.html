  Brooklyn 25 Nov '68 Novem 25 My dear Walter

i received your letter to day wensday and the one on saturday also and was particularly glad to get the letter and the contents as i was very short off funds and as georgey had let matty have considerable before Jeffy came he wasent very flush but the 2 dollars helped me out on saturday and saturday night george gave me some so you see providence has provided) Jeffy is here came last friday matty is better i think she went to new york yesterday and is quite smart much better than she was a few weeks ago Jeff and she talks of coming to washington for a few days the first of next week Jeff will   write to inform you what day they will come) i have had several letters from heyde saying hanna is gaining but her arm by his account seemes to be bad yet) have you wrote to Doctor their yet walter if you receive a letter from him i wish you would send it to me) hellen price was here yesterday to dinner she says they think so strange you havent wrote to them mrs price aint very well but so she s around mr Davis from providence has been to see them hellen said he said your next vacation he hoped you would spent at his hous george has not commenced his house yet but is talking about it) i am pretty smart matty wont bring either of the pets

  good bie walter dear

i write in a hurry as usual
