  Saturday Jan​ 26 1878 Dear friend

I write again fore​ for​ you to come down we all want to see you so much. George is not able to do any thing & he wants you to come to talk to him & Cheer him up we shall send to the Station for you Tuesday 29th besure​ be sure​ & come on the 4 pm train

yours with love S. M. Stafford

do not disappoint us fore we shall send [damage] you Tuesday

    from Mrs Stafford | Jan 26 '78
