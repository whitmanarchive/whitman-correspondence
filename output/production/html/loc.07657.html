  Camden Sunday Evn'g June 2 '89

All goes well—the feeling pretty good Friday evn'g continues. Suppose you rec'd the pocket-book b'd L of G—I sent you yesterday three or four papers with lengthy reports of dinner &c—Every body says it was a mark'd success—It is well toward sunset & I am going out for an hour in the wheel chair, (wh' proves a great comfort)—fine sunny weather—

Walt Whitman
