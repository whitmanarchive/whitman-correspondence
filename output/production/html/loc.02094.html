  Adel Grange, nr Leeds. May 12. 1891. Dear Mr. Whitman

My sister Bessie & I both thank you very warmly for the present you sent us of your book. Edward Carpenter sent it on to us—we send you our warmest greetings & best wishes for   your birthday—we never forget it—& always wish you all good.

Your's​ Yours​ very sincerely Isabella O. Ford.
