  Camden Aug:27 '89

Am pretty comfortable & easy upon the whole—breafast, toast, honey & tea—have just written to Mrs. O'C—also an "autogiographic note" for Horace's dinner book, (a page, fine type)—bowel voidance this forenoon (first during a week)—sit here alone, ab't the same—the weather markedly changed color to-day—the Japanee​ Japanese​ Hartman call'd yesterday—have been idly reading & scribbling a little to-day—one of my let ups—

Walt Whitman

Y'rs of 25th comes—shall be glad to see Dick Flynn—
