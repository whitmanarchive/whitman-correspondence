  Anderton, near Chorley. Lancashire, England 22. March 1892 Dear Walt,

I send you a line or two as a brief token of our constant thoughts of you and of our dearest love.

We cherish always the faith & hope you have taught us, and, with strong cheer & acceptance of fate, send you our tenderest blessing and love.

And, here is a kiss long & tender X from

  your loving Wallace       see notes april 2 1892
