  Editorial•Department The Century•Magazine Union•Square•New York New York Walt Whitman Esq Camden N.J. My dear Mr. Whitman:

Your postal has just come & I write to ask you for particulars before making the request of Mr. Cox.

   

Will you kindly let me know who it is wants the negative & for what purpose.

I was sorry not to be able to grasp your hand on your birthday

Yours very truly, William Carey     see notes June 19 1889 I wrote to W.C. 6/20/89
