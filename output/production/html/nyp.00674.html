Camden April 14 '89

Fine sunny coolish day—Ab't same as before with me—(fancy less rub-a-dub in my brain from the "cold in the head" & constipation coalition)—Hope to hear (& good news) f'm you to-night or to-morrow—Have you rec'd mine, & Stedman to me enclosed, I sent you via Kennedy? Have just written to Dr B—

Best love to you and N— Walt Whitman
