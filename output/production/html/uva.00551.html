328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey May 3, '87

Your letter rec'd & welcomed as always—My visit to N Y was a hasty flash only—I am more & more wretchedly physically disabled, & feel better off here in my own den—the "Anne Gilchrist" book is a wonderfully well done Vol. & interesting very to me because I knew & loved Mrs. G—but I doubt whether it contains much (or any thing) for you—I can loan you my copy if you wish—I will certainly keep you posted ab't myself, or any literary movement or change or happening of my work—

Walt Whitman
