  Camden Nov: 6 PM '89

All fairly well with me—Sunny bright cool weather—y'rs rec'd—let the MS: lay by then & mellow & round & be added to (& be yet rounded if fate ordains)—settle on the few matters you w'd exploit & bend to them & enrich & fortify them & prune (perhaps) the rest, & sort 'em out—I hear f'm Buck​ Bucke​ often, he is well & busy—Was out yesterday (after three weeks' embargo) in my wheel chair, too cold.

Best respects to Mrs: K Walt Whitman
