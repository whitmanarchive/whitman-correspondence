Arrived home all right, 24th, at dusk, over the "shore line"—I like that route better than the old one—no dust that day, and fine view of the water, half the time.

The package came safe to hand on Friday, containing my 20 purchased L. of G. and 20 to give away at discretion. I shall send you a tally of the latter as I [cut away]
