Harry, I shall come down on Wednesday in the 4 p m train (as I said)—Nothing new—I am well—I had a good day yesterday (Sunday) perfect day—went over to late breakfast to the Steamer Whillden, Arch Street wharf—every thing jolly and plentiful & sailor-like—there four hours—then in the evening to Col:​ Johnston's family—I have got your blue flannel shirts for you—

W W

—love to your father & mother—
