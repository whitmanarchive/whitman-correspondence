Camden July 18 '91

Bad days & nights with me—neuralgic sick head ache in addition to other ails—Keep up & was out yesterday to my tomb in Harleigh Cemetery (will send you a sort of photo soon)—Dr Bucke has arrived in Eng: & is with the friends at Bolton (cablegram yesterday)—the enc'd: letters are lately f'm Bolton—Sarrazin came back to Paris very sick, but is grown better— H Traubel goes on to Wash'n to see Mrs: O'Connor & others anent of a book cont'g O'C's splendid life-saving sketches (f'm wrecks)—hot weather here five days—am feeling it badly, but getting along better than you w'd think—

Love to you & frau— Walt Whitman
