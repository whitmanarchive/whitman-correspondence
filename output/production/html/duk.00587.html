  '69 ? Decembe 22 Walter dear

i got your letter yesterday all safe i am obliged to you walter dear but i can doo without the order till next week if its just as conveinent for you to send it by wensday i would be glad but if it aint the week after will doo George sent me a letter that he wont be home till the last of next week he says he has to work hard but after next week he will have it easier) we have   had a very rainy day here to day i havent made any preperations for the holidays nor i dont think i shall as you nor george wont be home george may be here new years i have been very lame but to day i can seem to get around better but i am afraid to doo much for fear i will get bad again) you must try to come home for a week or so if the new man gets in i see an account of it in the papers but i thought it must be judge strong of long island

 

walter you may send me a galaxy if you can just as well as not) mr and mrs Bruse has gone to washington he may probably call to see you if he does it will be to the office) i hope they will find you well and happy walter dear love to mr and mrs bouroughs and the Oconers also nomor at present

your mother L W
