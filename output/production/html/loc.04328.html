  200— | A 328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey March 27 '89

yr's of 22d rec'd & welcomed. Am still laid up in the sick room—havn't been out in most a year. (Get along though better than you would suppose)—Come and see me if you make y'r trip—Give my love to Jo Hyer & any other inquiring friends. Jeff was here a little while ago, he is in St Louis

Walt Whitman
