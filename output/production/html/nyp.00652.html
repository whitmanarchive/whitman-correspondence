Camden March 7 '89 6:50 P M

Have come thro' the monotonous day much the same as usual—sold two big books to-day—Dr B[ucke] in for an hour, busy yet with the meter—every thing moving slowly, but no serious impediment—

Sitting here alone by gas light—dinner an hour or so ago—breakfast at 9½ a m—mostly free from serious pain—

Walt Whitman
