Hampden, Ohio, March 1, 1875 Kind sir,

I received your card was glad to here​ hear​ from a soldiers friend in time of need and if you are the friend that took care of me I am glad to here​ hear​ from you I can not place you as I did not learn your name but havent forgot the kindness I recived​ received​ while in the Army​ Armory​ Square Hospital I would like to see you

I draw a small pencion​ pension​ am owing to york St[ate] grafting in a bout​ about​ 3 weeks

I guess you are the friend that wrote a ltter​ letter​ for me when I first came to the hospital I am glad to here​ hear​ from eny one​ anyone​ write again

my respects and good wishes
