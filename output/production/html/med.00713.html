328 Mickle Street Camden Oct 11 '84 My dear friend

Thank you for your kind & warm invitation to write a Blaine poem—but I shall not be able to accept it—

Thank you heartily too for the pay (more than I asked & more than it is worth) for the little Red Jacket bit—but it has not yet come to hand—most likely will come soon—I am about as usual—only very lame.

Walt Whitman

I enclose my last screed in the Critic—
