Camden Thursday night Nov: 1 '88

Had fine weather to-day. Much as usual with me—An elderly gentleman, an Iowan just ret'g from Eng[land], told me at a dining party at Rossetti's a Frenchman told him of a review of L of G. ab't three or four months ago in Paris—the gent. said he could give me the date & name of magazine & if so I will tell you—Your letter came last evn'g—Have had beef & onions for my dinner—Ate with relish—

Walt Whitman
