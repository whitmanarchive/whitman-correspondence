Camden Evn'g Nov: 22 '88

Not much difference—if anything duller & some depression to-day. Clear weather & unmistakably cold. I sit crouch'd by the fire—No word from you for three days—An Englishman, Lewis Fry, MP. for Bristol, call'd, bringing two fine tall daughters. I am just writing to Mary Costelloe.

Walt Whitman
