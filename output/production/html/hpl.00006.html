Camden Thursday Evn'g Oct: 4 '88

Still here in my sick room—everything much the same—Book printing &c slowly proceeding—the sheets now in the bindery—Shall send you the little Nov: Boughs soon—a good visit f'm H Garland —Cool & pleasant—I have a wood fire—Horace is here every day—continues invaluable.

Walt Whitman
