  328 Mickle Street Monday 2 PM

I have just learned that a young man whom I sent to speak for me in my sympathy for you & deep sorrow for Mr Kelley's death, did not call early last evening—So I write this line—I am too feeble to get around much, but you can command any thing I, or Mrs: Davis, can do or furnish—

I had not much acquaintence with your husband—but enough to know that he had a beautiful soul—

Walt Whitman
