  1871 August 22

tuesday evening 6 oclock and no letter from walt and nobody else as to Jeff he dont never write to me in these days i have written to him but dont receive any letter from him or matty well walt i gess i shall get one from you to morrow you must write walt when you will come if you can tell so long before hand   well walt who do you think has been here to day mrs Hinds charleys widow she and her oldest daughter have come on here for a short time probably to see something about disposing of his pictures her daughter dident come here as she was not very well but a lady by the name of mrs judson came with her poor woman i felt great sympathy for her if i had had 5 doller in my possession in the world i would have given it to her   i dont know as she is so very needey but i felt as if i wanted to assist her a little she says it looks very dark bfore her but she thinks she shall remain in new haven for the present you must write to her walt and to townsen she said townsend has a colulm and a half in the new haven paper concerning you and they thought so strange they dident hear from you
