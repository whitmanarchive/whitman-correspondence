Camden Sept 17—Evn'g

All salubrious—Sheets go to the binder Tuesday—I will try to send a book so you will get it Saturday—After you read it, forward the "Modern Thought" (I send by same mail with this) to Dr Bucke. They are now on their fourth Phila: ed'n L of G—a furious article in N. Y. "American Queen" of yesterday—

W W
