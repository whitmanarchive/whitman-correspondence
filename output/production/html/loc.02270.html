  Art Department. HARPER & BROTHERS, FRANKLIN SQUARE, NEW YORK. July 25, 1885. Dear Sir:

We wish to publish your portrait in Harper's Weekly. Will you please say where we can get a good recent photograph? We would prefer one without the hat.

Yours truly, Charles Parsons Sup't​ Art Dep't,​ for E.D.W. Walt Whitman, Esqre.
