  Camden New Jersey July 4 '87 Dear Doctor—

By request of C W Eldridge I sent a copy of author's ed'n Leaves of Grass June 21—and now a second copy same—write me a line please to say whether they reach you safely—I send my love to you & Mrs. C and to the family, your girls & boy—Is Wm O'C still there—Many an anxious & loving thought is wafted thither on his account—

Walt Whitman
