328 Mickle Street — June 16 '90 Camden New Jersey—U S America

Yours of June 4 rec'd & welcomed—There is no other ed'n of Specimen Days (that I know) but the one I believe you have—the "Celebrities" pamphlet rec'd safely with thanks—

I am keeping on fairly—have been out in wheel chair to the river side (Delaware) to-day—pleasant weather here—

Walt Whitman
