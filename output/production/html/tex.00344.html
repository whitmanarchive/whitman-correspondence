Camden, N. Jersey—U. S. America— March 23

Yours of March 5 recd. with enc.—Books will be sent hence by Baldwin's Express (office in London, H. Starr & Co. 22 Moorgate st)—will probably arrive there somewhere ab't 10th or 12th April.

I sent a set (L of G and Two Riv. Two Vols. in one package) to you by mail March 15—& another set two Vols. same date, to Mrs. G[ilchrist]—but have since learn'd that such packages are overweight, & fear they may not have been forwarded from N. Y.

I wrote you letter March 17—I sent a single Vol. (Two Riv) by mail, March 20—I continue about the same.

W.W.
