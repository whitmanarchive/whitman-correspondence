  Camden Sept: 30 '89

Y'r letters & the papers rec'd—best thanks—I am much as usual—sit here in the old den in the arm chair, altogether disabled—John Burroughs has been here—& return'd to his farm, West Park NY—is well—dark & rainy here continued long. Mrs: O'C must now be there in B[oston] again—A unitarian minister to see me yesterday they all come here

Walt Whitman
