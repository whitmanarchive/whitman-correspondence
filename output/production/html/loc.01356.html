    328 Mickle street—Camden New Jersey—U S. America Dec. 13, '86—

Here I am, still, dear friend, & nothing new or special—the last week I was quite ill again, but am on the mend yesterday and to-day—Your good father comes to see me often, & Logan paid me a nice visit yesterday—Your letter of Nov. 12 has been read & re-read, & quite gone the rounds—much admired—I send you "My Book & I," in print—

Walt Whitman
