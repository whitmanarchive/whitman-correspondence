  Belmont Mass. Dear W. W.

How about the article on Ars Poetica? Have you thought about it? rec'd it? lost it? I will send stamps for return if you find that, on the whole, nothing can be done with it in Philadelphia. Heard from Dr Bucke recently. He asked what had become of my article. Paper rec'd (Camden) Thanks

aff W.S. Kennedy.
