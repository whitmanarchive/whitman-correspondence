Yours of 20th recd.

I shall be glad if you will send the copy this week, as I sail for Europe in a fortnight, and would like to come to some understanding in the matter before leaving.

Yours truly J. R. Osgood Walt Whitman Esq  loc_nk.00271_large.jpg
