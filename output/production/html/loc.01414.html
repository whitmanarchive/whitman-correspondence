  Walt Whitman to the Editors of The Critic (s. issue May 30, 1885) 328 Mickle street Camden New Jersey June 1 1885. My friends

I wish to deeply & gratefully thank the author of "Victor Hugo" in Critic of May 30, for the spirit & words of the allusions to me—& thank you also—Oblige me by sending this note to the writer of the V H article—

Walt Whitman
