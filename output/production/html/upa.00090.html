  Camden N J— March 11 '91

Y'r letter came & is welcomed. I am poorly yet—nothing very new—Harry S was here yesterday—the folks are well as usual—Geo: is getting along well—Harry has not found any place yet—Did you get the March Lippincott? I am sitting in the old chair in my Mickle st. den writing this—fine weather—

Walt Whitman
