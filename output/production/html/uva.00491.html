328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey July 28 '86 My dear Redpath

Yours of 26th rec'd—All right & no harm done—But I mortally hate to have any thing with my name signed go to press without my seeing proof—Also I wanted the slips—Yes I will furnish you the (ab't) seven page article you request, & soon—My health much as usual—better this summer so far than last—

Walt Whitman
