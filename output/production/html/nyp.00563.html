  [tornaway] Office. INSANE ASYULUM  
  LONDON  
  ONTARIO 7 Dec 18 90

Yours of 3th came to hand friday evening but hardly have had time to breathe the last few days—lectured two hours yesterday and lecture two hours tomorrow—but thank goodness the lectures will soon be through with. I have sent for the Critic of 25 Nov. Yes, Dr Johnston sent me a copy of the little pamphlet and I read it at sight—never laid it down till I finished it.
