Camden March 1 '89

The situation ab't the same, weather sunny & fine—Dr B[ucke] expects to go on to W[ashingto]n in the 8.20 train to morrow, Saturday, & see you midday or soon after—Horace Traubel, a young friend, & invaluable help (printing &c) is going with him, to return in the latter afternoon train. I am sitting here by the fire comfortable.

Walt Whitman
