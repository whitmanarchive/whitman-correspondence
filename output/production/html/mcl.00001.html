  Department of Justice Washington Jan 3 1872 Dear Sirs:

Wishing to close up matters, I solicit, as early as convenient, an answer to my letter of about two weeks since, (in response to yours of a day or two previous) in relation to my books left with you on sale (as per your receipt of Nov. 3d, 1870)

Yours resp'lly​ Walt Whitman
