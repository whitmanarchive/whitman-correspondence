Camden Evn'g—April 1 '90

Y'r card rec'd—thanks—fine sunny day & clear evn'g, after snow-storm &c—I have the grip at last & quite badly—am sitting here alone in my den—nothing very new—my eyes failing—Expect to give (& wish to) my "Death of Abraham Lincoln" memorandum April 15 in Phila:—shall have a little poemet in May Century—Did I tell you to look at (London) Universal Review Feb. 15 for piece ab't me by Sarrazin? Love to Mrs K—

W W
