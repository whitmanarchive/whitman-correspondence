  Medical Superintendent's Office. INSANE ASYLUM LONDON ONTARIO 1 July 1891

I have the proof of the "Birthday" piece. It is immense. I like it wonderfully & feel sure it will do us a lot of good. Think it will be one of the most popular things yet and assist the study of W.W. & L. of G. greatly.

Beautiful day here and all well with us. Sunday I leave & Wednesday morning sail. Write me a line Monday, dear Walt, and address it. "White Star S.S. Brittanic N. Y.["] I will send you a word the last thing as I sail out to sea.

All looks well with meters and everything. I have heard from Eakins—the picture is mine

Love R M Bucke   see notes July 3 1891
