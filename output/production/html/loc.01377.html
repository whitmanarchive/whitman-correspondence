  Camden NJ US America Tuesday Evn'g Oct 23 '88

Still imprison'd in the sick room—but keep up spirits & occupy myself (or fancy I do)—Your kind good letter rec'd—so glad to hear f'm you, & all the particulars—H Gilchrist seems to be thriving & happy—I hear often from Dr Bucke—I will send you forthwith my little Nov: Boughs wh' comes out in two or three days—The big Vol. (Complete Works) soon—

W W
