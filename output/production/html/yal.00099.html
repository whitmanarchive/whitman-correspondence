Annapolis, Feb. 19th, 1865 Mrs. Louisa Whitman: Madam:

I enclose the memorandum your son gave me Feb 14th. If I can tell you anything further, myself, about him I shall be happy to do so.

I am, very respectfully, William Cook  
  Capt 9th U[nited].S[tates]. C[olored].I[nfantry].

92 W. 10 St. 
  New York

 

George W. Whitman  
 Capt. 51st Regt, N.Y.Vols. Prisoner of war at Danville Va in tip top health and spirits.

Mother Address. Mrs Louisa Whitman, Portland Ave near Myrtle Ave, Brooklyn N.Y.
