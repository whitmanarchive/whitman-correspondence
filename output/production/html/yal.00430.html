Camden Monday Evng​ Feb 26 '83

At the breakfast table this morning we all agreed—my sister most markedly—that your piece in the March Century is the best you have written—I think so, after two readings. Don't you go back however on any positions (naturalistic & critical) you have taken in former essays & books—they are all just right & needed—

Dr Bucke's book is in the printer's hands—I am well as usual—Best love & remembrances to you & all—

Walt W
