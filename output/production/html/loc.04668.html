 Camden New Jersey March 6 '83 My dear Stoddart

I have rec'd​ your letter enclosing Mr Kelly's—Why yes I can give Mr K the sitting or two he desires—If he could come on here any time within the next two weeks, I would sit either in the forenoon say 10 to 12—or afternoon say 2 to 4—wish him to send me word a day or two beforehand.

Walt Whitman
