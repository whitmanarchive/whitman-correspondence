Camden N J U S America Jan: 27 '91

Y'rs & J W W[allace]'s letters rec'd & welcomed—also copy of J A Symonds' good letter—thanks for all & to W. for delineation of Fred Wild—& to him & all, my loving wishes & regards. Rev. of Rev's. rec'd, thanks—I continue rather poorly. End uncertain. Have sent copies of Ingersoll's little book, one to you, one to J W W—Fine sunny weather—Am sitting here in my den as usual—Show this to Wallace—

Walt Whitman
