  Camden Aug: 31 Evn'g '88

Horace Traubel bro't me the nice basket pears—& I have been eating—& Mrs. D has preserved a lot—best thanks—Tho' I don't get worse again I don't improve in strength, vim, &c at all & hardly anticipate—My Nov: Boughs will be finished—& I shall have a big Vol. of all my stuff one Vol.—

Walt Whitman
