Camden, New Jersey—U S America Oct:​ 8 '82​

Your handsome "Buster og Masker" has safely reach'd​ me—best thanks—I will soon send you a copy of my prose "Specimen Days"—Dr Bucke's book is not printed yet—My volumes are now published in Philadelphia & are selling fairly—I rec'd.​ & acknowledg'd​ g'd Thorsteinsson's poems—I am well as usual—

Walt Whitman
