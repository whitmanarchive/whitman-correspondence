  Camden— June 21—noon

All ab't​ the same with me—the mare was quite lame, sprain of right hind foot, was the reason I didn't come down yesterday—I miss'd the jaunt & was grum & dull all day—I got a letter from Dr. B​ this morning—he had spent a day & took dinner with Herbert Gilchrist & Edward Carpenter—all well—

—Delightful weather here as I write by the open window,—but I am disabled worse than ever & can't get out—can hardly get across the room—tell George if he wishes it I will get a 7 hat in place of that 6 7/8

W W
