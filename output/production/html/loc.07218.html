  Superintendent's Office. 
 ASYLUM FOR THE INSANE 
 LONDON. 
  ONTARIO 
 London, Ont., 4 Aug 1888

I have your card of 2d and am glad to hear both from yourself and Traubel that you hold your own. I am anxious for more proof—you will recollect that the last p. I have is 104—all the rest of the book will be new to me. I do not understand your plans about publishing, you say you may not publish for a while "for reasons." I think myself a good idea would be to print a hundred or two hundred copies on good (and large) paper, bind them nicely and sell yourself for $5. or even $10. with autograph, by & by publish through McKay or another. All well here, will soon write again, been extra busy lately

RM Bucke     See notes Aug 6, 1888
