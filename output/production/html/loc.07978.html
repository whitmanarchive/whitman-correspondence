  early P M June 26 '91

Hot wave again—am keeping up pretty well—Suppose you rec'd Dr J's facsimile of my letter June 1 to him—isn't it curiously perfect?—The report of the birth anni: spree it seems is to go in the August Lippincott you can easily get it in Eng: & will probably so appear (why so deferr'd doth not appear)—Enc'd y'r introductory to Tennyson—give him my best regards & love—& of course same to Bolton friends—& indeed to all inquiring friends the other side—(But I shall probably write ag'n before next Saturday)—very dry burning hot weather to-day—

best remembrances & thanks & love as always, dear friend. Walt Whitman   June 26
