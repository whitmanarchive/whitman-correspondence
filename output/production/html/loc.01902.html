  My dear Mr Whitman

Please write Your Autograph & Enclose in the Accompanying Envelope I appreciate   the Many [illegible][deletion] favors Asked of You feel desire Your Autograph So Much to Add to My Already Good Collection that I trouble you—Please   oblige one of Your Greatest Admirers.

Sincerely A T. Smith Baltimore June 6th 1871.   AT Smith
