328 Mickle street Camden New Jersey April 11, '88 Dear friend Helen Price

Yours rec'd—Yes, I will sit to Warren Davis the painter—w'd like to have it over with in five or six sittings—In May w'd suit me, as far as I see at present—W'd probably accept any time that might suit W D who had better write to me a little in advance—

I am still living here & comfortable & in good spirits enough but probably near the end of my rope—badly paralyzed & do not go out at all, except by being toted—Am real glad to hear from you once more—Wish you had told ab't y'r brother & sister, & the latter's children.

Best love & remembrances to you & them—I hear from Dr Bucke often—he is well—Still Superintendant in Canada—

Walt Whitman
