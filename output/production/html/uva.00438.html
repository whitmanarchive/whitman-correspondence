431 Stevens street Camden New Jersey March 21 '82 My friends

I believe you have in MS one or two clusters of my Notes—yours—they are paid for—I think I would like to look over them & touch them up to date (perhaps, or not, I could tell on seeing them)—I wish you would send the MS therefore immediately here—& I will at once make the improvement-changes, (if any)—& return to you—

Walt Whitman
