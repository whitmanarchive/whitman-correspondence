  Altnacraig Oban June 9: 80 Dear Sir

I have just heard from our general P. O​ that the clerk here was mistaken in her directions   as to how to send a P.O.O​ to you. I now enclose order for £1. for "Leaves of Grass" which please direct to "Miss   Macdonald, c/o Professor Blackie, Oban, Scotland"

& oblige yours gratefully Katie ES Macdonald.
