Camden New Jersey Dec: 27 '82

Thanks for the Molière—the Vols:​ —(vivacious & jaunty—& entirely new to me & not too deep)—will surely lighten many hours in a way most opportune & desirable—

I am well as usual—have been out in Germantown on a few days' visit—have taken long rides & explorations along the Wissahickon, Indian Rock, & all about that region—Happy New Year—

Walt Whitman
