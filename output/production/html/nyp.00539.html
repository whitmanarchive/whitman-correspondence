328 Mickle st. Camden Monday Evn'g

I am ab't as usual—All right, heart & spirits—I send Springfield Republican with piece, more because I want to send something than because of any moment in it—Send on to Dr B[ucke] when you have read. I write a little—sort o' sundown sonnets—have some nice visitors—Sometimes foreigners—two or three American girls now & then—great comfort to me—

W W
