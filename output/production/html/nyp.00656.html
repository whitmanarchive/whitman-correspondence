Camden Evn'g: March 11 '89

Disappointed in not getting any word f'm you to-day—sit here as usual alone—often thinking—Dr [Bucke] was in for half an hour—ab't a continuation every way with me—I sent a big book to Stedman to-day—O I hope & pray to get good news from you to-morrow—

Walt Whitman
