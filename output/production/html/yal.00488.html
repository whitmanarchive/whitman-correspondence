 Newark, N. J., June 23d 1890 Mr. Walt Whitman Camden, N. J Dear Sir

Will you please let me know who is to publish your new book, or if it is sold by private subscription only.

I notice in the paper that you are writing a small annex to it. I should like to secure a copy of it.

Yours Very Truly Chas. B. Campbell.
