328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey August 4 1884 Dear Sir

In answer to your letter & request a few days ago Yes I will gladly write for the Century an article on the Hospitals & Hospital Nursing of the Secession War, such as outlined by you—I will set about it immediately—

I enclose a short sketch & reminiscence of Father Taylor, with authentic portrait, (given me by his daughter). The price is $50—If you use it I would want to see proof, & would like to reserve the right of printing it in future book—Say after the lapse of a year—Respects to Watson Gilder—

Walt Whitman
