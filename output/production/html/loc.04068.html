  Elliottstown Effingham Co: Ills:— August 11th /77 Dear Walt

Thinking you would like to Here​ Hear​ from me I would write you a few lines in as much as Im​ I'm​ well and Hope this will find you the same

Business is very dull Here now and crops is ruined for the want of rain. all of my worment​ torment​ of of mind and toil of body is of no avil​ avail​ Im​ I'm​ feafuly​ fearfully​ in want now and when my crops is geathered​ gathered​ it wont​ won't​ be much better as I see   O I musent​ mustn't​ say Im​ I'm​ in want Because I Have got plenty to eat and some close​ clothes​

But not much money now I dont​ don't​ nead​ need​ as much

But the fuather​ future​ looks dark But may come out Better than I amagn​ imagine​

I will Buy some Hogs on a credit and feed my unsailable​ unsellable​ corn to them and may come out all right in due time I received those papers many thanks)

yours truly Jos. C. Baldwin

write soon
