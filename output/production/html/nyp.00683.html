Camden May 3 '89

Matters going on much the same with me—N[elly]'s card came yesterday—Coolish weather—am sitting here by the oak fire alone—trying to interest myself with the morning papers & Harper's Weekly, &c—My young friend Horace Traubel comes in (as every day) & manages all the printing affairs as I wish & direct—Best love to you & N—

Walt Whitman
