WashingtonSunday  
  Forenoon  
 April 28. My dear Mr. Curtis,

Thanks for your kind contribution & note. The help I seek is for Louis Fitzgerald Tasistro—he has paralysis—& has had a miserable winter, cold & hungry—(I have myself been absent great part of the winter)—But he is now up & comparatively better—your kind $5 will truly aid in ameliorating his condition—

Walt Whitman
