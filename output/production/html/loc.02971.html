  March 21st 1889.

Another bad night, vomiting, & very poor day to-day, weak & poorly. I did not sleep till after 3 A. M. Wm. not much till 2 A. M. At this moment he is taking a nap & I hope will wake up better. Your papers & Mary's. came. Very sorry Dr. Bucke's business fell through. We had great hopes for him & his scheme. Trust to send better news soon.

Love from Wm. & me. N.
