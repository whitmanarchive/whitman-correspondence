  Boston Sept:​ 14 Dear Hank

Yours of 11th just rec'd​ —I enclose you a note to Secretary of War, at a venture—If you go on, you get to see the Secretary. It wouldn't be any use to send it by mail—(as you requested)—I shall be through here in nine or ten days—Keep well yet—Shall stop a week or so in New York—then home to Camden for two or three weeks—then to Canada for some time.

Your Walt
