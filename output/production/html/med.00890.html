Camden New Jersey June 30 1890

Thanks for the fine photo: wh' has come safely—I am as well as usual—had blackberries & bread & tea for my breakfast—a hot spell of weather here—have just had a bath—Sh'd like to send a copy of L of G. or something (or two books) to Trans[cript] editor or publisher—who?

Walt Whitman
