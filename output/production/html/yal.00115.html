Tom here is a New York paper with an acc't of the great Cricket Match between the Canadians and Americans—I thought you might like to read

I find I have no photo: here like the one I gave Tom Bradley, but I have some home, & when I go back to Camden, I will send you one sure.

W W  yal.00115.004_large.jpg  yal.00115.005_large.jpg  yal.00115.003_large.jpg

Notes

  • 1. According to the New York Times, the Canadians defeated an American cricket team on October 11. October 12 was on Wednesday in 1881. [back]
