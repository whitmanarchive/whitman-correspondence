  Camden N J Dec. 18 '81 My dear Ben: Ticknor

Thank you for your (& O'Reilley's) suggestion about Swinburne—I cannot consent however to solicit him to write a notice—& must decline to do so—But I have dispatched to him by mail an autograph copy of the new edition, & a little bundle of slips of the new pieces & some notices—without however any letter from me)

—I have sent to David Bogue a list of some forty or more special friends of L. of G.​ (& author) in Great Britain & on the continent & have requested him to inform them—by little printed circular or otherwise—that he is the London pub. & agent of the book—

Walt Whitman

☞The 15 copies for editors have not yet reach'd me—perhaps they will come to-morrow—have they been sent?
