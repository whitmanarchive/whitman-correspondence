  dear walt

i got your letter tuesday my hand is some better to day lou returned home yesterday she has been gone since last saturday week she has had a very good time indeed thinks the visit has done her good i had a rather hard time of it i was so very lame at times i couldent shut my hand my finger were so swoln but we got along and had pretty good things to eat i cooked with my left hand and edd helped me if i hadent been so dreadfull lame and my hand pained me so bad i shouldent   have minded if we could have kept house longer lou and george are very clever but i think they are a very saving couple what they want to save so much for i cant see as they have no young ones but maybe its all right george is so changed in regard to being saving but i cant get used to being so ecomoical george has got a new gold wach chain lou has been saving the money up to get it for a long time so she made it out with edds board money it was 46 dollars

good bie my dear walt
