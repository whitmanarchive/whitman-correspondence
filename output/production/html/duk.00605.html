  30 Sept 1871 Saturday morning My dear walt

i thought i must send you a line to tell you i have got all the letters and the order came very good and the book by georg sand and all the papers and best of all the green porters has left early this morning for home george and loo came last night friday night quite late and have gone over to the institute to day Jeff has gone to boston look for him home to knight or to morrow   he went fishing wensday and caught lots of very large blue fish he brought home 2 it was first rate one of the men from st louis was here with Jeff matt must be home by this time O walt i am so thankful to you for your kindness to me in sending me money at all times good bie walter dear)

i have had a letter from mrs hind she thank you for your donation and has got the poem and wants you to send one to townsend) tom rome is married to a scotch girl
