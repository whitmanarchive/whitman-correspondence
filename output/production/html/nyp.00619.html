Camden P M Nov: 13 '88

A remarkably fine sunny day, & I went & sat in the warm bright bask of it from 12 to 1—Not much different in my condition—what there is, bends favorably. I am still imprison'd in my sick room—Please send the "Open Court" (in the bundle) to Dr Bucke—Am comfortable & in good spirits—few visitors lately—

Walt Whitman
