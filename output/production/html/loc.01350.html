    328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey— U S America June 1 '86—

Best love & greeting to you, & to Mr C—also to Dr Bucke— Best love to your father, mother & Logan I to-day enter on my 68th year I have rec'd Dr's good letter, with your good note enclosed—Nothing new with me—I am ab't as usual—fine sun and air to-day no very warm weather here yet—Am sitting here by the open window—great bunches of roses, pinks & mignonette near me

W W
