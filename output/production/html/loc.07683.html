  Camden P M July 25 '89

Dull & quiet—Slightly more ill than usual—half cloudy & warmish—Kennedy's book is at sea again as you will learn by enclosed—Herbert Gilc comes frequently—often strangers, visitors, sometimes queer ones—

I get out in the wheel chair—was out last evening till dark—acts as sort o' pacifyer—The Staffords are well—

Walt Whitman
