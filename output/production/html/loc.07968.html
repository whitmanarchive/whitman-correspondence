  Camden N J—U S America July 21 '91

Hot weather cont'd—depress'd cond'n with me—Horace ret'd f'm Wash'n yesterday—Mrs: O'C[onner] is still in office, & ab't same (is not well)—they talk'd the Life Saving Book into plan & shape—hear copiously f'm the Bolton friends—am sitting here as usual—

aff: regards to Mrs & Mr C[ostelloe] & the Smiths—took my breakfast with zest this forenoon, (the best sign I can send)— Walt Whitman
