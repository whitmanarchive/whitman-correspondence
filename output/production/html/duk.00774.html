Camden May 31 '83

The publisher having placed a few advance copies in paper of Dr Bucke's WW at my disposal I send you one—The book will be pub:​ here & in London in ab't​ ten days—May-be you could find a market (in some remunerative quarter) for some early notices of same—yours of 27th rec'd​ —

I don't think you appreciate the importance of that article of yours, (statement, position, signal for advance, &c) in the Critic. It entails on you deep responsibilities, & great wariness & determination in keeping it up. I mean exactly what I said in my last.

W W
