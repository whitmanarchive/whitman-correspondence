  October the 16/1868 Walter Whitman dear friend

hearing you was in the city i have taken the liberty of addressing you a few lines i am the wife of Henry Reynolds and my son is called after you, my husband went out West about a year and a half ago since which time we have not heard from him. we are very much in need, and your name sake is a nice boy, between 13 and 14 years old, and i thought perhaps you might take an interest in him. we would be very glad to have you call and see us at 302c East 26 street as soon as possible or drop me a line

yours Respectfully Mrs Reynolds     Mrs. Reynolds.
