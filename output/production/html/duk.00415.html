Han is not quite so well—rather ambitious and tried to do as ordinarily to day—yet I think she is stronger. Weather is much against her—Chilly—sheivering​ shivering​ —does not see any person yet. Visitors stay too long and talk too much, whch is very distressing—I rather discourage such—indeed I am positive at times—she appreciates any thing  coming from you most affectionately—keeps your letter in hand.

I wish she was near Lou and George—I am at times much discouraged and too nervous to wait upon her—my apprehensions professionaly​ professionally​ render me often eccentric and confused—

I shall be obliged perhaps to get an assistant, yet I hope not.

Mention to George—My anual​ annual​ interest 15 dollars only is due in November—I have 10 dollars toward it—I contrive somehow—
