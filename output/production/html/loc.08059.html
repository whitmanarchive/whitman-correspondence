Camden N J—U S America Evn'g June 6 '91

Will write even if only a line or two—Y'rs & W[allace]'s letters just rec'd—am getting along, but sick enough to day (yesterday easier)—just rec'd letter f'm H T[raubel] in Canada—cool weather (after almost hot)—Probably Dr B[ucke] will be in Eng: soon & come & make you all a short visit—

Love to you & all— Walt Whitman
