  Camden New Jersey U S America Aug: 12

Yr's recd—thanks—it is delightful to hear f'm you & thro' you f'm all—Probably it will be better to do up the twelve books (perhaps others) in a parcel & send by express to 44 Grosvenor Road (as I do not know they c'd go that way to H.) The money $58.80 has been sent me by ALS. Phila. Has been long hot weather but to-day & yesterday fine & cool.—I am as well as usual & writing a little—Best love to you & y'r dear father & M & A. Write whenever you can

Walt Whitman
