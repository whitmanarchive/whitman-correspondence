  John Phillips Street New Brunswick, N.J. July 16, 1891 Walt Whitman, Esq.,— Dear Sir:—

Your advertising circular which you send me gives me much of the information I want & I thank you for it. However my pocket-book just at present is in such a condition that I will have to content myself with a cheaper edition. If it is not troubling you too much, can you not recommend to me some cheaper edition of your complete poems, sold for $2 or $3.20

Yours very truly, John Phillips Street
