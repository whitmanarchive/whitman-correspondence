  Camden Sunday noon Apr: 12 '91

Cloudy & damp yet—am feeling a shade better this forenoon—bowel actions (more or less,—generally less, but I believe decided at that) each of the three past days—wh' is of course a great gain,—Tom Harned was here last evn'g—he is busy & prosperous (very possibly going off on business to San Francisco, for a few days)—Horace here as usual—proof will soon be done—enclosed a MS from J W W of Bolton, Eng:

God bless you all— Walt Whitman
