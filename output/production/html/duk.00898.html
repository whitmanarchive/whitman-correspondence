Camden Feb: 1 '88 PM

I remain ab't the same as usual—Dr Bucke was here most of yesterday—staid over in Phila: on his way to Florida with an invalid friend—Dr B to return in ab't two weeks—Shall return your MS. in two or three days or so (by express to Belmont, unless you wish otherwise)—I am writing a little for the N Y Herald personal column—E R[hys] is not here yet—weather more endurable—

W W
