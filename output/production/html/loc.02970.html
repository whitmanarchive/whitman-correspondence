  March 20. 1889.

I am sorry that I can't give you a good report of Wm. but he has had two bad nights, & a bad day, vomiting, & very weak. Night before last I hardly slept any, & as a consequence am not able to use my eyes next day. Tho' we have a man now, he does not get a chance to do much if I am about, as I am preferred. I wish I could do all. I do nearly all. I hope you are better. All cards welcome

N. O'C.
