February 22, 1868. Messrs. Routledge, (J. L. Blamire)  
  Gentlemen:

Your letter reached me this morning, enclosing draft for Fifty Dollars, gold coin, deposit in Bank of New York—of which amount, paid me, for poem, (with reservation to myself of right to print in future book,) you will please consider this the receipt.

Please accept my respects to editor & house—with thanks to yourself for promptness.
