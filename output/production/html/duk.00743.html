Camden New Jersey U S America Nov​ 27

Your card of Nov​ 13 rec'd​ —have to-day mailed Mr Vines' books—Your card of a week or ten days previous rec'd​ —Many & sincere thanks—

I still keep pretty well, & every thing goes on with me much as usual—Geo:​ Stafford has been very ill with hemorrhages from the stomach (hematemesis) but is over it, & out, though feeble—the rest well—the Gilchrists are all well—Mrs G is here with us in Camden today to dinner—We often speak of you—

W W
