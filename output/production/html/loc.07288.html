  7 P.M. Superintendent's Office. ASYLUM FOR THE INSANE LONDON. ONTARIO London, Ont., 25 Dec 1888

Your card of 23d to hand by this morning mail—glad to hear that you are holding your grip—We have had a quiet Xmas—we have eaten 122 turkeys—80 geese and over 400 lbs of plum pudding—regular English style! For my own part I have just had a glorious dinner of roast turkey and plum pudding and feel well! My great anxiety now is to put meter matters in such shape that I may get to Phila and see yourself and Traubel and see with my own eyes how things are   with you.

Must close this letter and go upstairs to the amusement room where we have Xmas trees for the patients and then dancing

Love and good luck to you RM Bucke
