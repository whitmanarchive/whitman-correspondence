Camden July 9 PM

Yours of 7th rec'd with R[hys]'s card. Tr[anscript] not rec'd—A good letter from E[rnest] R[hys] to-day—J[ohn] N[ewton] J[ohnson] is certainly crazy—a cross between Zdenko (in Consuelo) & something more intellectual & infernal—Very hot weather here, continued—I am feeling it badly—yet not so badly as you might fancy—I am careful, & Mrs Davis is very good and cute—As I write it is clouded over & begins to rain—H[erbert] G[ilchrist] is still here painting—Morse here—

Walt Whitman
