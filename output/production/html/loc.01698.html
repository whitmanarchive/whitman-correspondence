Department of Justice, Washington. Sept. 14, 1871 My dear John Swinton:

I have rec'd your note & enclosures. I have several things to write or talk to you about—but at present I only enclose these two slips—& I wish you would see that one is offered to Evening Post, & the other to any paper you think best—perhaps either Commercial Adv[ertiser] or the Standard.

I am well as usual.

W. W.
