  JOHN H. JOHNSTON. ALBERT EDW. JOHNSTON. J.H. JOHNSTON & CO., DIAMOND MERCHANTS AND JEWELERS, 17 UNION SQUARE, CORNER BROADWAY & 15TH STREET. ESTABLISHED 1844. All Communications should be addressed to the firm. TELEPHONE CALL: "916 - 18TH STREET." 305 E 14 St. New York, Nov 30 1891 Dear Walt:

Last Saturday night I was at the Dinner given by the Lotus Club to Mons. Giacosa—the Shakspear of Italy—whose Play on Wed. night at the Standard Theater Sarah Bernhard​ Bernhardt​ is to celebrate—

During the evening I had a good long half hour's talk with Bronson Howard and he surprised me by telling me how much he thought of & read L of G and said he wished he had a good copy easy to hold and handle—and I recommended the 2 vol edition & he thanked me for offering   to write you for them—

If you will send them to Bronson Howard New Rochelle N.Y.

I will guarantee cash payment.

4 weeks housed up—Up and at it now, but tire out easy, but am better every day—

Ever yours JHJ
