Camden Feb: 26 '89

Fine sunshiny cold day—Dr Bucke is here, got in to-day all right—crowded with the meter company & capital & manufactoring matter—wants to come over to W[ashington] & see you, & probably will—but no day yet fixed—Rather miserable with me, constipation, cold in the head & now a bad spleen trouble (enlargement &c)—Have rec'd the German trans: L of G. (200 pp)— Shall I send you one?

Walt Whitman
