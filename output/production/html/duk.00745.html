Camden New Jersey U S America Oct​ 5

I have to-day sent by mail post paid, the Volumes to Messrs Thompson, Templeton, Teall and Haweis, (seven Vols in all)—Many thanks to you—I am well, for me—I am just going over to the G[ilchrist]s to spend the evening—H[erbert] has return'd​ —

W W
