  Moses King Corporation. Boston, Mass., Nov. 14 1891 Walt Whitman, Esq. Camden, N.J. Dear Sir:

I have taken great pleasure in sending to you as one of the most notable men this country has produced a complimentary of "King's Handbook of the United States"; a little book that aims to give an outline of the glorious things of this country; but as you will readily see, it fails to do anything like justice to the vast subject although it is the result of the best efforts of 2½ years' of the most laborious and painstaking work.

Kindly let me know of its safe arrival.—

With great esteem, I am, Sincerely Yours Moses King
