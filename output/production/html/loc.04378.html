  Anderton, nr Chorley Lancashire, England 22 August 1891 My dear Walt Whitman,

I am prevented again from writing to you as I intended. As you will already know I am coming to Camden per SS. British Prince, sailing from Liverpool next Wednesday August 26th—I have had a busy time this week, & this afternoon my time is again taken up.

So I content   myself with the briefest line, to convey my continued love & good wishes.

I will try to write for next mail.

Love to all your household.

Yours affectionately J.W. Wallace.
