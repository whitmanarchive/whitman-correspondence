Camden April 11 '89

Splendid sunny perfect weather here—I sit with my window open—friendly notices from Chicago—Am busying myself with a special L of G. ed'n (to be trimm'd close & bound pocket book style) to include Sands at 70 and "Backward Glance"—Am quite miserable from the join'd cold & constipation ruling me now over a week—A good letter just from Dr B—

Walt Whitman
