 State Library C. L. Ehrenfeld,  
 Librarian. Harrisburg Oct. 25 1880 Walt Whitman Esq. Dear Sir:—

Please send me a copy of the Centennial edition of your works for this Library, with bill, and I will remit you the amt.​ at once.

Yours truly, C.L. Ehrenfeld
