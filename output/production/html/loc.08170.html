  Medical Superintendent's Office. INSANE ASYLUM LONDON ONTARIO 11 Sept. 1891

Wallace and I reached here sound and well about 8 o'clock last evening. The Asylum band was out in front of the house and they played quite a while to welcome me home.—All is in good shape here and the folk all well—the health of the Asylum has been excellent during my 2 month absence. I have a pile of work in front of me which it will take me weeks to wade thro' but I feel well and able to accomplish the whole job. Wallace will like it here and he will have a good visit and rest. Mrs Bucke sends her love to you. I will not write much of a letter now but will drop a line from day to day and whatever turns up you shall have it fresh.

Always with love R M Bucke     see notes Sept 14 1891
