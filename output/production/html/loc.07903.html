Camden N J—U S America March 29 noon '91

Still up—no worse I guess but bad enough—thanks to you & J W W[allace] for kind messages, papers & books—bad weather here all the time—head distress to-day—the R[eview] of R[eview]s came—my proofs of "Good-Bye" getting on slowly, 3-4ths done—Have just sent off two big books to Cambridge Eng. God bless you & to W. & the friends—

Walt Whitman
