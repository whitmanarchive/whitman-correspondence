Camden New Jersey U S America Aug: 20 '87

Nothing special to write—I suppose you have lately rec'd some papers from me—We appear to be thro' the long & fierce spell of hot weather—have had it very agreeable now for nearly a fortnight—John Burroughs has been here for a couple of days—he is well—nothing new in my literary affairs—H[erbert] G[ilchrist] is here & is painting leisurely—he is well—I hear frequently f'm Dr Bucke and Kennedy—I drove out twelve miles & back yesterday—

Walt Whitman
