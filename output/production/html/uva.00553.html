Camden NJ US America June 25 '87—Saturday—near noon

It is a perfect day—sunny—cool enough, & I am feeling pretty well & will write you a line—How are you getting along? & how is the baby? & how is Alys & all?—

I have written two little poems lately & sold them & got the money for them—will send them to you when printed. Am better than I was—Am sculpted & portraited lately—(I like 'em)—Love—

Walt Whitman
