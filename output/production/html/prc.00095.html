 cor West. Camden, N. JerseyJanuary 30.  
  76 Jeanette Gilder Dear Madam,

I though[t] I would send you a copy, (see other side) of a note I have just forwarded to the Herald.

I also enclose a slip better describing "the situation", (which I wish my friends to bear in mind more than they do.)

I remain about the same. Rec'd​ your kind note, with piece. Hope I may yet meet you personally—& your brother, (whom I have heard of lately by my friend John Burroughs.)

I also send you a newspaper same mail with this.

Walt Whitman
