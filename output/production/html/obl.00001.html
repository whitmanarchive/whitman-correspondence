328 Mickle St. Camden New Jersey May 11—noon—

I am still here & nothing very new or different—I suppose you rec'd the Press with a brief report of my Lincoln lecture in Phila: April 15—I go out every day with my mare & rig—sometimes to Phila.—Alice comes often, was here Saturday.

Has Dr Bucke arrived?—& you too I haven't heard from you in a long time—I am sitting here in the little front room down stairs—

Walt Whitman
