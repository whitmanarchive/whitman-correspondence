Camden noon Feb: 24 '89

Sitting here by the oak fire dawdling over the Sunday's Tribune and the Phil: Press—my physical condition rather worse—a depression the last three days & nights—nothing very new—sunny & very cold here—some strangely favorable notices ab't my books lately, (Paris, London & Chicago)—I expect Dr B[ucke] here Tuesday evn'g—

Best love to you and N[elly]— Walt Whitman
