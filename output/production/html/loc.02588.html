  PUBLIC SCHOOL No. 13,  
 A. G. KNAPP, PRINCIPAL. Rochester, N. Y. March 25, 1883 Walt Whitman My old time friend

Do you ever think of the boy that you found sick in the winter of '62 in Ward A of Judiciary Sqr.​ Hospital in Washington? If so, in memory of old Washington experiences together will you send me a volume of your "Leaves of Grass" with your name written in it?

I shall be very glad to hear from you

Cordially Albert G. Knapp
