Camden June 23 '88

I send the 20 proof pages of the "Sands at Seventy" as you & Nelly may care to see them entire in "November Boughs"—After four or five days I wish you w'd send these 20 pp. to mail to Dr. Bucke—

Saturday Night—Nearly free all day from the prostration weakness—better though partial—a thunder storm & rain this afternoon—Love—

Walt Whitman
