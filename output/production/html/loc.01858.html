  New-York Tribune. New York, Dec 22 1874 Dear Mr Whitman:

I was sorry the article about the Camden School seemed to you unkind in its reference to your health. I shall have a paragraph within a day or two, which will I think relieve you of the idea that we had any such intention.

I sincerely hope you are getting better, and will soon be out of the woods.

Very truly yours, Whitelaw Reid. Walt Whitman, Esq 431 Stevens St corner West Camden, N.J.     Whitelaw Reid see notes July 10 1888
