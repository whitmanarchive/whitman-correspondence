Camden, Aug: 6 Evening

Nothing specially new—I am well—pleasant weather—the 2d Phila ed'n was delay'd till Friday last—commenced coming in at noon—by noon next day (yesterday) 500 had been sold—Specimen Days is jogging along—neither very fast, or slow—You will get a copy of the Phila. ed'n L of G to-morrow or next day, as I have requested one sent you—by what I can learn there is no Florio Montaigne to be had for love or money—Have been in all day finishing up copy of S. D. for the printers—Out now to p.o.

W W
