Camden N J U S America Jan: 16 '89

I was too fast & all wrong abt the drafts. The first one was duly rec'd (I was very ill at the time) & cash'd—Best thanks. The second, the duplicate, I have destroy'd—Remain here laid up in the old sick room, but sort of comfortable—just had a letter f'm the Misses Ford—Remembrances & love to you—

Walt Whitman
