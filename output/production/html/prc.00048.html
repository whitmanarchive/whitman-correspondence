431 Stevens st.​ June 14—11 a m Dear friend

I am afraid to venture out much in the heat of the day (as part of my trouble of the head is sun affection, & susceptibility)—so I give up the Congress visit to-day.

We will see about it, either next Wednesday, or indeed most any other (cloudy) day—as I want to go aboard—& you shall go, if you wish to. Love to mother, father & Jack.

Walt Whitman
