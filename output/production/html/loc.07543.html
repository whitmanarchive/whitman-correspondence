Camden P M Jan: 3 '89

Yr's of 1st comes—day fine—feeling fairly continued—sit here by the stove as I write—slightly sweating—send copies of the corrected letter by this mail—Have written (perhaps I told you) another poemet & send it off to-night or to morrow to the Century—(shall send you a printed copy, & of the first one also)—It is near sunset as I close & all moves fairly—

Walt Whitman
