  Camden Dec: 8 '90

Snow storm today—Horace returns f'm N Y—poorly enough but sitting up as usual—bowel action yesterday—head uncomfortable & aching—am getting in 100 new bound big books, (ab't 100 left in sheets big)—Sit here as usual—send you paper with article & pict: (failure—impression poor—bad) of the cemetery place—suspicion of belly ache, but not strong—am trying to read & write—

Lord be with you— W W
