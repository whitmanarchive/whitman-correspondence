431 Stevens st  
 Camden New Jersey Sept 2 '76 Scribner, Armstrong & Co: Dear Sirs,

I have forwarded you by mail to-day, in compliance with your order of August 9, two copies of Two Rivulets. The price is $10, which please remit to me here.

Walt Whitman
