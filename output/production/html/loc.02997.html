  April '89

Yes, I sent on the letter of Stedman & O'C. (& yrs). Stedman's letter was extremely interesting, impetuous, egotistic, vain, extravagant, affectionate & warm—quite characteristic. His Lib. of Am Lit. I can't like very much, somehow. Good for cross‑road school-houses, I suppose. Thank you very much for Stedman's letter. A copious green‑silver warmish rain today. Worked to‑day on Greek type & proofs.

as always yr frd.— W.S.K.
