Camden Feb: 19 '89

Am sitting as usual by the oak fire alone rather monotonous here after long confinement—sharp cold to-day—Dr B[ucke] may be a day or so later in coming—I expect him now 21st or 22d—Have rec'd a long criticism (markedly favorable) in London Pall Mall Gaz: Jan 25—& another shorter Echo London, Jan: 26—Pretty fair with me, but "cold in the head" obstinate—

Best love to you and N[elly] Walt Whitman
