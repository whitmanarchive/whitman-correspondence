  431 Stevens Street Camden New Jersey June 16 '81 Dear Sirs

I have been down in the Jersey woods the last ten days—just returned—have finished my copy which is now ready to put in hand—all plain sailing & all complete—When do you want to begin? & will it go ahead pretty fast when actually begun?—I have spoken about the type—ought to be new or substantially new long primer—I sent on the proof page for a little change, last night on my return—I have pretty well decided to come on to Boston & see it through—

Walt Whitman
