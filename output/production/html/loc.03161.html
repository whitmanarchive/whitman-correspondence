  4th, July, '91 Dear W.W.,

In July, 3 Transcript— Brunswick's letter has an impudentish(?) bit abt [illegible] (I can't like [illegible] Gilder & I have [illegible] (I think it is) in "Liter. Notes" a notice of [illegible] Miss E P. Gould's "Gems" I had an agreeable call fr. her at office. Her deep violet eyes are fine. She is a noble woman, evidently.

Nice call (highly valued) from Chares Eldridge! Sent you my love by him.

W.S.K.
