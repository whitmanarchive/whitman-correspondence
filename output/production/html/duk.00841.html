328 Mickle Street 
 Camden N J April 19 '87

Am here in my little old shanty again, & every thing ab't as usual —Stood it very well in N Y—it was a good break f'm my monotonous days here, but if I had stayed long, I sh'd have been killed with kindness & attention—I rec'd $250—Y'rs just come. Thanks—

Walt Whitman
