Camden N J—U S America P M July 14 '91

Matters essentially ab't same with me—hot wave last 40 hours—but am getting along tolerably so far in it—good letters this mn'g f'm Bolton friends—O'D[onovan] comes every day (what the sculp'g will eventuate in the result will tell)—I eat blackberries—no Dr L[ongaker] now for four days—miss y'r letters here—so does H T[raubel]—sit here by window as usual—

Walt Whitman
