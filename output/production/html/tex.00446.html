328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey U S America Aug: 3 '85 Dear Edward Carpenter

Yours of June 9th with the draft for £50 has reached me & I have the money. Hearty & affectionate thanks to you, dear friend, and to Bessie and Isabella Ford—

I have suffered from the great heat here—have had two or three bad fits of vertigo (thermal fever the doctor calls it)—but shall soon almost certainly be around as usual—

Walt Whitman
