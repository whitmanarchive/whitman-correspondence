Camden New Jersey

Tho't I w'd send a line to show we do not forget you—but have no particular word to write—suppose you rec'd the letters & papers I sent—Have heard f'm y'r aunts H[annah] and M[ary] lately—both are poorly in health but H is getting around—nothing new with me—as I write it is late at night Dec: 31 '90—a storm out.

Jan: 1, '91—Well it has come—a dark stormy morning here—but of course it will clear & brighten up—

Walt Whitman
