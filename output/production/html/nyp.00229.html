 Washington D.C. July 13th 1864 Mr. Walt Whitman  
 Dear Friend

I have once more returned to this place and am improving in health. I should like very much to see you. I am very anxious to hear something of the whereabouts of my Capt I have written several times and as yet have recd no answer. Hoping to see, or hear from you, soon. I remain as ever,

Yours with respect Aaron Smith  nyp.00229.002.jpg  nyp.00229.003.jpg
