431 Stevens street Camden New Jersey Nov:​ 17 My dear friend

I do myself the real pleasure of presenting you with a set of my books—which are sent by same mail with this—I have inscribed Mrs G's name with yours on the fly leaf, & please show her this as a testimony of my remembrance & affection to you both—

Walt Whitman

I am well for me—have been down two weeks in the Jersey woods—return'd last evening—
