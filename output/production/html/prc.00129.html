Camden July 7 '91 Dear sister

The same as before with me—am getting along fairly—My friend Dr Bucke starts for Europe, SS Britannic f'm​ N Y​ to morrow—to be gone ab't​ two months. I got over the 4th favorably—am sitting here by the window as usual—have had a good supper—My nurse has gone on to N Y​ to see Dr B​ off—Hope this will find you comfortable, sister dear, & best love to you—

Walt Whitman

2 enc'd​ —
