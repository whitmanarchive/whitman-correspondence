  Nov. 7 1½ p m Mr: and Mrs: Harned Dear friends

I send the two tickets for the lecture—Also a little book for Anna. Also a programme of Donnelly's "Cipher" Book—curious to look over.

—Also your Sunday Tribune wh' I pilfered last night—also a couple of Critics—enjoyed a happy three hours last night & am all the better for it to-day—

W W
