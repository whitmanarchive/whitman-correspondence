Camden NJ U S America Sept: 8 '90

Y'r kind letter rec'd & I have enc'd it in my letter to Dr Bucke to–day—I continue well as usual of late—had melon & rye bread for my breakfast—fair appetite—have just written a card to Dr J[ohnston]—his photo's rec'd. good—I am getting some sharp notices [in p]rint here lately—one I hear of (I have not seen it yet) in Sept. Atlantic Mag: by Dr Holmes—(the physician you doubtless know, wants to achieve a good job, more than to get the love of the patient)—

Walt Whitman
