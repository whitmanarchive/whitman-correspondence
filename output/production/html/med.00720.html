Your book has come so nice and fresh like a new pot-cheese in a clean napkin—I have read the first piece—"Look Out"—all through and thought it fully equal to anything in the past, and looked over the rest of the pages—doesn't seem to me to deserve the depreciatory tone in which you speak of it.
