  Richard E. Labar, Real Estate, National Exchange Bank Building Waukesha, Wis., June 4 18890. My dear Mr Whitman

An earnest hand–shake on your birthday & continued presence among us! I notice a brief mention in the papers of a tribute by friends, including the Colonel, & will much enjoy a full account of it, if you have a paper handy. All goes well here—

Affectionately, Dick—
