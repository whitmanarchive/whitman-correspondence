  Camden early P M Feb: 23 '91

Sunny bright day—still bound up & bad but a slight turn for better—(have just bo't & used a good enema pipe & seems helpful)—Sent off the copy for "Good Bye" pp: 3 to 42 to printer, but he sends me word he can only put one man on it—(dont expect the proof complete for ten days)—my piece is adv: for March N A Rev: (it will be very scrappy)—a little delay in March Lip: but yrs will go to-morrow I guess yr letter rec'd—

Walt Whitman
