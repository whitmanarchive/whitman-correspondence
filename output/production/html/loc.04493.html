  328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey Sept: 22 '87 Dear Sir

Yours rec'd with the $5 in advance for "November Boughs"—The book is not printed yet, but is contemplated before long. Thank you for the papers that come occasionally, always acceptable & new—I do not know what "letter" you allude to in a late "Tribune"

I send some scraps, MS. and autographs.

Thanks & good bye for the present—

Walt Whitman
