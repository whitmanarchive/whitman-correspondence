Camden1 June 18 '83

Please send copy of Critic of June 9 to Dr R M Bucke, London, Ontario, Canada—also (if not already sent) copy of Critic of June 16, to same. Dr B. is a subscriber to the C. but tells me in a note rec'd​ this morning, he has not rec'd​ the paper of June 9 (?2 or has his subscription run out?)—I know he will be deeply gratified & grateful for your notice of his book in that paper—

Walt Whitman
