  30 Jan 73 My dear darling walt

I have just got your letter i am glad my dear walt you are as well as you are i know its bad enoughf to be confined in your room and unable to walk but i am glad to hear your friends is so kind i thought of peter i knew if it was in his power to be with you he would and cherefully doo everything that he could for you) i could wish you was here walter dear but as it cant be so i trust you will be restored to your usual health and your dear old mother i am about as i was when you was here the cold weather dont affect me so very much) good bie walter dear remember me to peter

write just as you are walter

dont say you are better than you are

god bless you my son

thursday afternoon 30th
