431 Stevens Street Camden New Jersey / US America 1

Yours rec'd​ —Many thanks—shall be happy to supply you with the Book—the best way is by mail—the price is £2 sent me here by p. o. order—it is entirely safe and easy—

Walt Whitman

Thanks for your offer of "the Poetry of the Period"—I have not seen it—

Notes

  • 1. This note was sent to an Englishman to inform him of the price of the 1876 edition. [back]
