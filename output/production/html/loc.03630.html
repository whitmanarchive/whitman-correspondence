 loc.03630.001_large.jpg SOUTH HEATH,  
 HAMPSTEAD. N.W. London, Nov 23rd/83 Dear Sir

Will you also send me "Leaves of Grass" the same edition as "Specimen Days" that I may have your works complete. I have not  loc.03630.002_large.jpg yet seen it but have read several of the poems—"Lines on a Locomotive" I cut out of a newspaper years ago before I knew your name thinking it very forcible & grand. Trusting that you still enjoy life &  loc.03630.003_large.jpg that as sometimes happens the paralysis is decreasing & that you are surrounded by kind friends; & surely you must be for are not you the friend of every man!

I feel the confidence of me of those friends that you will find no presumption in my writing thus, & again  loc.03630.004_large.jpg thanking you for the assurance of that brotherly feeling

Believe me Sincerely yours Emma Riley
