  Brevoort House, New York. Sep. 27th Dear Mr. Whitman:

I was only in Philadelphia for a few hours, but I propose to return there for some days the end of next month or the beginning of November

 

It will give me real pleasure to make your aquaintence​ acquaintance​ having been, I think, one of the first to welcome you into our great old world literature.

I remain yr​ very tly​ L​ Houghton
