Camden Sunday Evn'g July 17 '87

Heat, heat, heat, night & day—I find Evn'g a great relief—have pass'd great part of to-day lying on the lounge, with a big palm-leaf fan—have read Fullerton in the Record and Mrs. E. in Herald—best thanks to both—I suppose O'C[onnor] is in Wash'n, very poorly, but have not got word thence of his arrival—I am just going to my supper (blackberries)—

Walt Whitman
