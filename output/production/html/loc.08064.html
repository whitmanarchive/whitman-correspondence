Camden N J— Sept: 15 '91

Glad you have same perfect weather we here—y'rs 13th just rec'd & glad to get it—Love to you & J W W[allace]—am getting along fairly—some nice oysters for my b'kfast—Warry is cook as Mrs. D[avis] has gone visiting for the day—

Walt Whitman
