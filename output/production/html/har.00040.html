 har.00040.001_large.jpg 328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey1 April 19 '90 To the Editor or Superintendent:2

The printed slip seems to me more or less flat & erroneous—at a venture I send you in the MS sheet what I think would be a better brief biographical note for the book—wh' sheet I hope you will substitute & cancel the other

yr's respectfully Walt Whitman  har.00040.002_large.jpg
