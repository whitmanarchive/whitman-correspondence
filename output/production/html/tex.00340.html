  dear walt

i dont want to worry you but i thought i would send you hanna s letter it is rather a strangely connected letter but god only knows what will be the end of her troubles i have got one from him one of his ranting ones i cant tell what an awful letter it is) i wish she could be got here if matty and jeff does go back she could take that room till spring) The erysiplus generally affects the head so i think probably she will be quite smart) as she gets strenght doo you think she gets your letters walter perhaps she does i was thinking maybee it would be as well to send her one to the care of Dr. Thayer as i suppose he has a box at the post office but doo as you think best walter dear) i am pretty well matty is about as usual Jeffy will come in a few weeks

dontbe worried no more than you can help walter i know the nature of the disease george would get something in his head and worry as she does about the letter if heyd was kind to her she would get well

i wish you had went there walter when you was home

Dear Mother

Burlington
