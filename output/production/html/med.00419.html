To the Attorney Gen's Office, for Mr. French, Pardon Clerk.1

Please unlock the case where my books & pamphlets are in Mr. French's room & send me by bearer 6 copies "Democratic Vistas."

Notes

  • 1.

    Transcript.

    This appears to be a note written while Whitman was still in Washington; therefore, the year 1873 seems plausible, since before his illness Whitman would have gone for the books himself.
