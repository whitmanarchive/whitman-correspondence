  Belmont June 8 / 88 Dear Walt Whitman;—

Mr Harned has written me of yr​ recent attack of illness. It was at first quite alarming. But thank God it was no worse. I wont weary you by saying more than to express my heartfelt sympathy & thankfulness & good wishes. I sent Mr Harned's letter to John B., & suggested his sending it to O'Connor. Mrs Fairchild asks affectionately after you. She is just going to England.

W.S. Kennedy
