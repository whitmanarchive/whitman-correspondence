  [cut away]SE, ASTOR PLACE, [cut away]NEW YORK, N. Y. 45 RUE VIVIENNE, PARIS. 142 DEARBORN STREET, CHICAGO, ILL. The Illustrated American. New York, March 9th 1890 Walt Whitman, Esq. Camden, N. J. Dear Sir,

One of our artists has just completed an excellent large portrait of yourself, which we hope to publish in an early issue. We should like a few lines to verse to go with it, and if you could supply us with this we should feel extremely obliged.

Yours truly, Maurice M. Minton
