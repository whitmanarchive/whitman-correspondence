  Camden NJ— March 25 '91

Am still worrying it out poorly enough—no worse however—the Doctor comes every 2d day—I am satisfied with him—the printing of "Good-Bye" gets along slowly—I read proofs—I believe I told you the 20 pp: poetic stuff (end of L of G) was done & cast—I ask'd you if the youth's comp: had printed "Ship Ahoy" & have not rec'd any answer—

best respects to frau— Walt Whitman
