 Trenton, June 21 Hon. Walt Whitman: Dear Sir;

As I am collecting the autographs of distinguished persons I would like to add your signature to my collection—if you will be so kind as to send it to me.

Yours truly, Henry W. Armstrong P.O. Box 312 Trenton, N. Jersey
