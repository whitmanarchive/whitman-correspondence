 Trenton, June 21 Hon. Walt Whitman: Dear Sir;

As I am collecting the autographs of distinguished persons I would like to add your signature to my collection—if you will be so kind as to send it to me.

Yours truly, Henry W. Armstrong P.O. Box 312 Trenton, N. Jersey  loc_tb.00025.jpg

Correspondent:
Little is known about Henry W. Armstrong. According to the New Jersey State Census records of 1885 and 1895, Armstrong lived in Trenton, New Jersey, with his wife Katherine M. Armstong.
