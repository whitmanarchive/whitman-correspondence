  Camden Sept:​ 14 '91 Dear sister,

Getting along fairly—eat my two meals every day—one of my greatest botherations is the dimming of eye sight—incipient blindness—a long stretch of perfect sunny weather here, pleasantly cool—I went out on a two hours drive to what we call "Pea Shore" Saturday afternoon—receive some good letters f'm​ England—am sitting here early afternoon in big chair alone by window as usual—have lots of papers & magazines—best love to you dear Han—

W W
