  OFFICE OF THE 
  Camden & Atlantic Railroad Co. Camden, N.J., Jany 25 1877 D.M. Zimmerman, 
  Secretary and Treasurer Walt Whitman Esq Dear Sir

I would like to have a little article on Lake Side Park (at Kirkwood) for publication over your name, if you do not object. The enclosed by yourself is good, and if it was in mss I could get it published in one of the Philada Papers; & others would copy. We also propose to collect such articles and have printed in circular form.

I hope I do not ask you to take too much trouble

Yours truly DM ZimmermanTreas
