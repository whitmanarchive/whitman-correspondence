  CHARLES P. SOMERBY, BOOKSELLER, PUBLISHER AND IMPORTER OF Radical, Scientific, Positivistic and Oriental Literature, No. 139 EIGHTH STREET, (Few Doors East of Broadway,) New York, Sept. 25, 1875. Walt Whitman: Dear Sir:

Mr. Somerby left for Boston last night, before the arrival of yours of 24th inst.​ He will get your letter Monday morning. We should forward to him, if there was certainty of reaching him.

Yours, etc., C. P. Somerby, Per Wa[illegible]     Sept. 26 '75. C. P. Somerby
