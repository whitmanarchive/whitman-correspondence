 man_ej.00059_large.jpg Dear Sir

I am one of many in this country who look for different preaching & teaching from that of past generations, who thank you that you have led the van in a glorious charge against materialism & asceticism. It is high time that, with our boasted culture,  man_ej.00191_large.jpgwe should be simpler & truer than we are; that having learned of God from X (& from many others weaker than He but stronger than we) these many generations we should now be holier men & women than we are.

Will you send me a copy of your "Leaves of  man_ej.00192_large.jpgGrass" (I enclose P.O.O. for £1), address it to "Miss Macdonald, c/o Professor Blackie, Oban, Scotland" & oblige

yrs very heartily Katie E.S. Macdonald Altnacraig, Oban June 5: 80  man_ej.00061_large.jpg

I find it is not usual to enclose the receipt of the money order I have sent you. But you will find I have sent the money all right
