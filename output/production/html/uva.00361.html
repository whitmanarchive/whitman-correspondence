  Washington 7½ o clock  
 Evening 23rd  
 Sept '68 Dear Walt

As i​ I​ send this I am getting ready to go to the Theatre to see the Black Crook nothing new today but the Weather​ weather​ Very​ very​ Wet​ wet​ & Stormy​ stormy​ & i​ I​ am afraid to Venture​ venture​ to work until the Storm​ storm​ is past just received your letter of the 20th about ten minutes ago i​ I​ cant Explain​ explain​ the Pleasure​ pleasure​ i​ I​ experience from your letters

Farewell my good & true Friend, Pete the Great   23 Sept '68 Dear Walt  
 Pete
