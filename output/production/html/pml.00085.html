328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey— April 20, '84—Evn'g

Yours of April 5th just recd, & very much welcomed—Some how it is a special comfort—I have not been well as usual for two months but am now better & shall soon be ab't as of late years—I have moved into a little old shanty of my own—(See new address above)—am much more contented—am writing this impromptu at P.O. so you will get it at once. Will write more fully soon—best love—

W W
