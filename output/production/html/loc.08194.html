  Camden N J— Sept: 18 '91

J W W[allace]'s and y'r letters rec'd—thanks—full report of Bolton mt'g Aug: 24 rec'd f'm friends Shorrock and Dixon ("hold your horses, doctor")—the report with a little pruning, I think ought to go in the book—am still held here indoors—long stretch of fine sunny weather cont'd—warm to–day, sweating me a little—good—Dr Longaker here yesterday—nothing specially different—had a fair night last—bowel movement yest:—

Walt Whitman
