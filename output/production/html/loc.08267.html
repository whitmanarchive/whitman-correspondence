  Camden NJ— Oct: 20 '91

J W W still here well & enjoying everything—y'r letters rec'd & welcomed.—Have you written to Costelloe to send the clay W W bust (if not engaged) to Dr J. Bolton?—I have mention'd it to J. Bad day with me—bladder &c: Horace T. well—the latest of L of G. not yet—I expect McKay here to-day—

W W
