Camden early P M March 5 '91

Y'rs of 3d rec'd—No I am not worse—bad enough, the most obstinate & long continued bound up spell yet—but I fancy a slight shade leaning to the better—you must be this moment at the highest of the tempest gale there—we have got the international copyright law pass'd here—my piece is in March N[orth] A[merican] Rev[iew].

I send you slips—

W W
