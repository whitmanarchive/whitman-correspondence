Camden Wednesday PM July 11 '88

No special change for good or worse—Bowel motion last even'g, not copious but definite (by syringe)—Warm weather to-day, pleasantish—I am trying to get the E[lias] H[icks] paper presentable—but hard work—but I keep at it obstinately (my poor brain is just an old worn out horse—you tug & tug & tug)—All are good to me—I send more proof—all alone this afternoon—

Walt Whitman
