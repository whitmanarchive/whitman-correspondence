  The Magazine of Poetry 
 C.W. Moulton, 
 Editor DICTATED Buffalo, N.Y. November 28th 1888 Mr. Walt Whitman Camden, N.J. Dear Sir:—

Sometime since I wrote you concerning a selection from your poems which I wished to publish in the first number of the Magazine of Poetry. Dr. Buckle of London, Ont., has kindly furnished a biographical notice of yourself, and with your permission, I would like to make the selections from your poems. I enclose letter from your publisher in which Mr. McKay expresses his approval of our new magazine.

Trusting to hear from you at your early convenience, I remain,

Yours very respectfully, C. W. Moulton (L.M) answered Dec 1 '88 by HLT.   See notes Nov 30, '88.
