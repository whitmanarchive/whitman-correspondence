  Sept. 1. Delgany. 
 Ireland.

Yesterday 1stinstalment of proof sheets of German L. of G. reached me — thought you might be glad to hear this. Please send a line to Dr. Knortz if you know his present address, & ask him to tell me if proofs have been sent to him also. I should like him to see them before final printing.

Yours T.W. Rolleston.
