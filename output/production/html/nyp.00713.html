Camden NJ— Nov: 12 '91

The book came all right this mn'g—seems to me a good piece of typographic work, type, paper, press work, & binding—pleases my book & printer eyes—And how are you getting along & satisfied there? (Lots of friends more than you know of are asking)—I myself still hold out—lots of [illegible]aches & sufferings—but mainly fair spirits—sitting this moment in big chair with g't wolfskin spread back—Traubel here last evn'g—he & wife well—Dr Bucke well—

God bless you Walt Whitman
