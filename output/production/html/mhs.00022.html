  Camden Sept: 7 p m

I think every hour of the day, (& night too when awake) of Hattie—& of how it must be there with you & Jess—but keep on much the same, with my doings & affairs—

—I know well enough, dear brother, how gloomy & blank all must be to you & Jess.

—I wish I could do something to help—

W W
