431 Stevens st.​  
 cor West.  
 Camden,  
 N.J. Oct.​ 2.1

Have had just a slightly more encouraging week—though slightly—strength gets a little more reliable (has been very poor,) will try to send you some definite word soon about coming—

WW

Notes

  • 1.

    This piece of correspondence is addressed, "Pete Doyle, | M street South, bet 4½ & 6th | Washington, D.C. " It is postmarked: "Camden | Oct | 2 | N.J."

    The projected visit to Washington makes the year almost positive.
