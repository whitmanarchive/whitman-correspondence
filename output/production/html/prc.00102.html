328 Mickle st: Camden N J forenoon Dear T D

Yours of yesterday rec'd​ announcing the 3 o'clock P M arrangement. Opera House Chestnut above 10th—for 15th inst. I will be ready & on hand. When you come over, Sunday or any other day soon, come in the forenoon or before 3 in afternoon or in even'g​ as convenient

Walt Whitman
