  Camden Sept: 21 PM '90

Have got at the O'C bit for preface this forenoon & finished it & will put it in type & send you an impression say in three days. Feeling fairly—Grip and bladder trouble yet—Markedly colder weather to-day—sent letter yesterday to J H Johnston to be handed to Col: Ingersoll—

the Lord be with you— Walt Whitman
