Camden Sept 25 (noon)

I shouldn't advise trying the C[ritic]. Let it go for the present. I have rec'd​ a cheery letter from C W E[ldridge]—says he has hung out his shingle as lawyer—but two or three lines in it squint at going into publishing yet—John Burroughs is at Ocean Grove N.J. temporarily for sanitary purposes. A bright, sunny glorious day here as I write—

W W
