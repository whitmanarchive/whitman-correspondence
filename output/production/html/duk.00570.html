  Dear Walt

I write a few lines with Georges letter i received it last wensday and should have sent it before but i have not received the money he writes of going to send i think there must be something wrong about it if i dont get it to day perhaps he was orderd away before he could express it i thought maybee i should have to show the letter to the express but i will send it) mat says she gesses he is going to get married because he said use what i wanted and put the rest in the bank I shall put all i can doo without in certainly but if he could see Andrew i know he would say mother give him some and let him go in the country if it will doo him good I went down there the other day but O walt how poverty stricken every thing looked it made me feel bad all night and so dirty every thing he thinks of going to rockland lake county next monday i hope it will doo him good we are quite well i feel middling well i am fatter than i was when you went away but i dont feel any better for it i think sometimes not as well i will write again soon i have had anothe letter from Heyd he says han is gradualy getting better but is not able to come home

miss hattie has torn this letter as usual
