  Germantown, Pa. Jan. 4, 1889. Mr. Walt Whitman:

My dear Sir, I take the liberty of sending you in the same mail with this, a little pamphlet—upon literary work, which I thought might prove interesting to you.

Should it prove so, my object will then be attained.

Believe me with the greatest respect

very sincerely yours Edwin C. Jellett
