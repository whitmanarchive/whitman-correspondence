Camden Feb: 16 '88

Card rec'd—thanks—every word is welcom'd & appreciated

—All ab't the same with me as of late—severely cold here—

—Dr Bucke is in Florida to return in ten days—An old Quaker has paid me a visit to-day (I am yet writing my Elias Hicks paper)

Best love to you & William W W
