  431 Stevens street Camden Nov 21 My dear Herbert

I had a safe little jaunt home yesterday afternoon—What a long-drawn-out storm—cloudy yet this morning.

Upon talking more fully with my sister about the colored woman Rosy, I am convinced she would not do—so I think we will give up any further thought of her as a help to you—

J T Nettleship's address is 233 Stanhope street, Regents' Park N W—The criticism in the Examiner was written by J H McCarthy. I am feeling pretty well (for me) this morning—Affectionate regards to all—

Walt Whitman
