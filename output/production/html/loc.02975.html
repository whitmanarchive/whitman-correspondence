  1015 O St. Ap'l. 24,

Wm. has been very sick & weak since I wrote you; able to sit up but little & "throwing up" at all times of day & night. He is at this moment, 11 A. M., sleeping,— after a fairly good night. Your package of papers came to-day & postal card yesterday. Hope you are able to enjoy the fine weather.

E. M. O'C.
