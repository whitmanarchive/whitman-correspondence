Camden Feb: 21 '88 P M

I am perhaps essentially the same—plus the feebleness & lameness & clouded heavy almost constant half-pain feeling of head & brain. Much milder weather here & I shall try to get out in phaeton if I can—I have rec'd the London Adv. Feb. 16—Two ladies have just called & chatted. (Mrs Talcott Williams one of them)—I enclose Ernest Rhys's letter, just rec'd—also two letters for you—I believe they still print my little bits in the personal col. NY Herald, but do not see the paper—Do you see it there? I enjoy all you say of the balm & flowers of Florida—

Walt Whitman
