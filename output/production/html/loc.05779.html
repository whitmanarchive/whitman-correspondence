 loc_no.00020_large.jpg Buffalo NY.1 Decr 14, 1880. My dear Mr. Whitman

I am trying my best to make up for the loss of my collection of Autographs a year or two since

Will you oblige me with yours and anything you can spare either Literary, Musical or dramatic and confer a favour on

My dear Sir Yours respy​ Wm Davidge

PS. My address in Brooklyn is 132 Pacific St. I shall be here all this  loc_no.00019_large.jpg  loc_ej.01014.jpg week. Rochester N.Y next week
