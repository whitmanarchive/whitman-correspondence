(Private) when a great general dies, you print his obituary, & his record, by its inspiration sometimes does as much good as his deeds.—I think the 51st New York Veterans, lately captured almost entire, while bravely fighting, deserves some such mention. The statements in the abstract I send are all facts. You will see I have avoided any thing like puffing, but given an abstract only.
