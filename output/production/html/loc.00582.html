  Do not for God's sake breathe a word of this to any one even after the papers report it.  
   
  Boston  
   
  December 5, 1860  
  Dear Father,

We go by the board tomorrow or next day. Please return the check to Mr. Honeybun care of Thayer & Eldridge. We have fixed your account so that creditors cannot trouble you & so that you owe us but a nominal sum just for appearance sake.—

Our friends would help us but from the condition of our affairs & the prospect of bad business for six or twelve months to come advise us to stop immediately, wind up & begin again.—

Yours Truly T&E
