  Brooklyn 10.25/91. Dear Friend Walter,

I recid your kind note of the 13th: with papers &c, & welcome they was.

I was surprised & greived​ grieved​ to hear of my old friend Jeffs death. He was a grand good boy, & I well remember the many long walks he & I had together.—   He was a good true friend & often at my own fireside I speak of him of one of the lads I knew who had true Sterling qualities of manhood in him. Peace to his memory. I did hope to meet him again & talk of old times I shall meet him on the "Other Shore" I trust.

With a heart full of love to you my dear friend,

I am Yours truly Sam. G. Stanley. 323 Macon St.     Sam G. Stanley.
