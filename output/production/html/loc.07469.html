Camden P M Dec: 30 '88

Remarkably fine day—I hold ab't the same balance as before—sit here monotonously, try to interest myself reading, but don't succeed—the good Harned has been here bringing to-day's Tribune & a bottle of fine sherry—the baby flourishes, & Mrs: H was out-doors yesterday. Happy New Year—

Walt Whitman
