  New Year. Jany 1. 1890 Bro Walt.

Blustring day—but Han is certainly improving—gaining strength.

How opportune your 5 dollars and George's came, to replnish​ replenish​ the coal bin—Every little helps, too.

Business now will be dull—not unusual for me—an artist has no business, as the world runs—. Han is not yet well enough to work, but will be.

Charlie
