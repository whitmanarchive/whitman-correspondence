  A.F.S. Baltimore June 6th 1891 My Dear Walt Whitman

Please write your autograph & enclose in the accompanying envelope I appreciate   the many & [torn away] favors asked of you but desire your autograph so much to add to my already good collection that I trouble you—Please   oblige one of your greatest admirers.

Sincerely A. F. Smith Baltimore June 6th 1891.   A F Smith
