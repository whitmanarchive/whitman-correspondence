Camden Oct:​ 30 Evn'g​

Have been quite ill the last two weeks—jaundice & mark'd​ bodily prostration & lassitude—But I am better, & have just been out a few steps—the doctor comes every day, (old school) & has certainly done me good. I suppose you rec'd​ "Specimen Days" I sent (two copies)—

W W
