  328 Mickle street Camden New Jersey U S America March 20 1886 My dear Ernest Rhys

Thank you for the little volumes—three copies have reached me—they look very well, & I am pleased with them, & with prefatory notice—I am here, ab't the same as of late years—Keep up pretty good spirits & buoyancy—that makes the best of it all—Give my respects to the Walter Scott folk—& dont forget to write to me soon, to let me know how the Vol. goes—I much hope to reach the working men, & guilds of the British Islands—especially the young fellows—& trust the W. S. vol. will forward that object.

Walt Whitman
