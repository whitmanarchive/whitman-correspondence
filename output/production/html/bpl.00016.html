  Camden July 5 '87

I shall get a good 4 or 5 room shanty by the water or in the woods

Yes I sent you the card I ought to have sent to Baxter—Can you send it to him—this one also?—To day, Tuesday, we have the great relief of a cloudy, showery, fairly coolish day & I feel better—previously we had had a week of great heat & glaring sun & it had sapped me pretty well down—

Walt Whitman
