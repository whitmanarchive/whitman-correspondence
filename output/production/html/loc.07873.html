Camden New Jersey U S America Evn'g: Dec: 23 '90

Y'r letter welcomed—Feel pretty fair (considering)—was out in wheel chair yesterday, but cold—sit here today in big chair with old wolf-skin spread on back—some visitors—write & read (or rather go thro' the motions)—once in a while something or somebody that cheers me—(what an art that is!)—Often think of you all there—

Walt Whitman
