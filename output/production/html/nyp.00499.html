Camden N J Sept 
  17

I wish you would send me (if you have it) the piece in the N Y Evening Post of a while since—the subject of your letter—rejected by the P. & by N Y Times also—Which piece I will return you—Marvin spent Saturday afternoon last with me, & has posted me up about many things. (You have an immense accepter & champion in M.)

W W
