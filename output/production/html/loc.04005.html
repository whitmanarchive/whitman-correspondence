  Camden Feb: 6 '89

Am here yet, dear Hank, in the same place &c, after passing a good hard shaking (& not out of it yet)—but somewhat better—Have written & sent your folks at Glendale a longish letter wh' I want you to have, as it is meant as much for you—I have finished all my books &c. and feel better.

Best love— Walt Whitman
