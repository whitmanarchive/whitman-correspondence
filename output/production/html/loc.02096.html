  Camden NJ—US America Sept: 27 '91

Y'rs of 8th came safe with pay for the books, (best thanks)—I send copy of the complete works, (for Maurice)—& copies unbound of the little "Good–Bye" with more—Others will follow, especially a new fuller version of L of G. now in press

—I am physically an almost complete wreck (right arm & mentality & fair spirits left)—Respects & love to you & y'rs

Walt Whitman
