Camden N J—U S America June 27 '91

Perfect day sunny right temperature—am getting along fairly—Suppose you have rec'd the printed slips (proof) the birth day business, f'm next August Lippincott—(slips sent you yesterday)—forward you now a budget pictures curios—where duplicates, let J W W[allace] have one each duplicate—the photo mark'd in * corner back was the favorite of O'Connor & his wife at Washington, as a remarkably good likeness war times, (where the pict: was taken ab't '64)—the one in hat (curl'd) was the favorite of dear Mrs: Gilchrist—have sent proof slips of birth day acc't to Symonds & to Dr B[ucke]—(the best bits are S's, Conway's & Dr B's)—Warry is over to Phila: to-day—H T[raubel] with friends & photo: cam[era] are going out to the tomb, the cemetery. (I believe tomb finish'd)—soon as I get good pic't. will send you.

Walt Whitman
