SS   328 Mickle street Camden New Jersey May 21 '85 Dear Sir

As I have lost the address of the young man who call'd upon me yesterday from your paper, & to whom I promised some pictures and memoranda, I send them directly to you—If I can furnish you with any thing else, or any information I shall be happy to do so—

Walt Whitman
