I notice that Charlie Pfaff is dead. "Brunswick" of the Transcript (who is Mrs Jeanette Gilder) has a word in his memory in Transcript. I am going to stop & see you in July. Love to Dr. Bucke. Bluebirds (a pair) building in my box. Also robin & golden woodpecker nesting in lane. What a burst of spring! Am cutting lawn, planting & sowing loving & hating, working & eating & sleeping as usual, always a brave cheery heart Thank you for yr postal bulletin. Your Transcripts you & I pay for by our contributions, that is the agreement.

So long. W. S. K.
