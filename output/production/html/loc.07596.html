Camden April 11 '89

Y'r cheery little letter has just come—have just finished & sent to the printer a little two page preface (so to call it) that has to be eked out in the Backward Glance for 70th y'r ed'n L of G—

Am having bad hours (& now seven or eight days) with the damnable cold & constipation leagued—

W W
