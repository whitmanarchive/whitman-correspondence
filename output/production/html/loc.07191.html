U S America—Camden New Jersey May 29 '87— 328 Mickle Street

Your good affectionate letter, with the welcome carte pictures & the handsome birthday present, safely rec'd this morning—& thank you for all most sincerely—I am living here comfortably enough, but a paralytic bodily—As I write I sit by the open window of my room, the birds singing & summer bursting—Again thanks—

Walt Whitman
