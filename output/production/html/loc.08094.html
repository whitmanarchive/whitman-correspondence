    Camden Sept: 20 '91

These are the title & backing pages of the forthcoming really complete & last L of G.—Ab't same with me—appetite, nights & bowel action fair to middling—get out (very moderately) in wheel'd chair—Y'r & Wallace's letters rec'd—Thanks—fine weather cont'd—warm—am sitting here immobile as ever (or rather more) but mainly cheery—might be much worse (perhaps will)—

Love to you & all— WW
