  R.M.S."MAJESTIC." 300 miles east of N.Y. Tuesday afternoon 1 Sept '91

As I shall soon see you, dear Walt, I shall not write much now. We expect to get landed tomorrow forenoon and I hope to reach Camden Friday evening or Saturday at latest. I want to  see Mr Dane about meter matters in N.Y. and attend to a couple of other small matters there.

We have had a good passage—at present it is very hot and I fear we shall have a hot time in N.Y. I am well and trust to find you no worse than when I left

So long—With love R M Bucke   see notes Sept. 3 1891
