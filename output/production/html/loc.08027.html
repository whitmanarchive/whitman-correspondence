  Camden Apr: 11 (2 P M) '91

Getting along ab't same—wet dark glum weather—bowel action—proofs moving slowly—will be ab't 60 pp: altogether—quite many small personal notes & reminiscences—letter f'm Dr J., Bolton Eng:—yr letters rec'd always welcome—have just rec'd a bunch of spicy arbutus, very nice—hear f'm W S K. Boston—he is hard at work & well

Walt Whitman
