 Dec 8, '68 J. T. Fields, Dear Sir:

Your letter has come to hand, with the check for $100, as payment in full for the piece "Proud Music of the Sea-Storm"—leaving me, however, the right to print it in future book. Please when ready send me proof, which I will return forthwith. Please send me, by express, 30 copies of the number, when ready, to my address here.

With thanks & best respects,
