  Camden New Jersey US America Saturday Evn'g Dec: 7 '89—

Finest sort of weather, sun, skies &c here days & nights—I was out last evn'g to supper and stopt my chair to have a good long look at the skies & full moon—Alys has been here to-day with a young woman chum—on their way to Milville—Nothing to write about but feel pretty well & tho't I w'd write. The news (the papers full) is of death of Jeff Davis—that ends it—or seems to—

Love & respects to father & all, not forgetting the girls. Walt Whitman
