  14 Aug. '91 C Studio 21 Pearl St 12 Augt Bro Walt.

Your note to Han with 2 dollars is recd gratefully—

Mr Brigham has not been to see her for several days—He is with his aged father—now very sick—out of town—and therefore could not write to you as you desire —

Gratefully ever and forever yours, Charlie
