  30 Sept 1869 thursday afternoon my dear

i have to write again you know i said in my letter if your letter came this week all right maybee i wouldent write well walter the letter and order came all safe but i have been to the post office and the order had not come so of course i couldent get the money

 

i was going yesterday but just as i was going i had quite an increase in my family mary and Louisa and minny all come bag and baggage they shut up their houses and come to brooklyn yesterday i had 5 ladies to tea) walter if you get this friday i wish you would send me 2 dollars george sent me 2 dollars i rather look for him saturday if he dont come i have hardly got any money

 

the young man to the office said he would write to you
