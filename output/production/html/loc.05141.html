  Sept. 24 29 Lange Str. 
 Dresden. '82

Your two postcards just recd. Nothing further as yet, but packages sometimes take a day or two longer to get to me. Sorry you didn't receive the piece of translation—sent it with a letter a good time ago & was rather wondering I hadn't heard from you about it. Am working away at it with full steam up & feel that it looks promising. Will defer lecture till it is nearer completion. Rees Welsh & Co all hail! May your shadows never grow less! Will get prose book as soon as it appears. Will write soon as pictures arrive. Many thanks.

TWR.
