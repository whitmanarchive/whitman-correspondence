  431 Stevens Street Camden New Jersey U S America Dec 10 '78 My dear Josiah Child

Yours of Nov: 23d duly came to hand with Trübner & Co's remittance of $47.55, & statement of acc't​ —Thanks—I send by same mail with this the Photos: to Messrs: Ham Smith and Brooks, as requested—

Won't you also accept (for your Christmas trifle) a large & small Photo: which also goes—(Should the mat on the larger Photo: not please your eye, It does mine though—just as it is—you can remove it for a different sized one—or remove it altogether)—

The New Quarterly Magazine also came safely—Grateful thanks—I still keep around well as usual

Walt Whitman
