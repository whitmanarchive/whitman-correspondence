Camden NJ—US America Oct: 27 '91

J W W[allace] has ret'd f'm a pleasant visit to the Staffords (Glendale, Camden Co: N J)—is well, has just gone over to Philadelphia—O'Connor's book "Three Tales" pub'd by Houghton, Mifflin & Co: Boston, is out—I am having a bad spell—(sinking & congestion.) All yr's recd—

Walt Whitman
