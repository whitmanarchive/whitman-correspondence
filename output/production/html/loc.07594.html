  Camden April 7 '89

Y'r letters arrived & welcome—My card yesterday f'm Mrs: O'C speaks of our dear friend as having a good night previous—& sitting up next forenoon—Nothing new with me—Horace, Tom, Mrs: H. and Mr Morehouse have just call'd—My cold in the head still on—cloudy & raw weather—Am sitting here in the big chair alone—have had two moderate sweating spells past two days—

Walt Whitman
