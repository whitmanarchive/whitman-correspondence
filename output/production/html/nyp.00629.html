Camden Tuesday Evn'g Feb: 12 '89

Soon as convenient send me a word or two, as I am thinking & worried—Send the Transcript to Dr B[ucke] (the one I mail to-night same as this)—the last date fixed by Dr B starting hither was 18th (next Monday)—Ab't as usual with me—(but my head dizzy with a "cold" in it)—

Best love as always to you and W[illiam]— Walt Whitman
