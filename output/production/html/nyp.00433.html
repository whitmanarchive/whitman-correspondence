431 Stevens Street Camden New Jersey Oct 31—p m Dear Baxter

I have seen your fervid and stirring criticism—respond with thanks & love—if convenient mail me three or four copies here (see above)—please mail one to E C Stedman 71 West 54th Street New York City—one to Dr R. M. Bucke, London, Ontario, Canada—and one to John Burroughs, Esopus-on-Hudson, New York—

I write in N Y, but the above is my main address, & I return there forthwith—I keep well as usual—

Walt Whitman
