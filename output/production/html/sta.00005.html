Camden, New Jersey, U. S. A. October 25th, 1877.

I send to-day to same address as this card, one L​ of G​ , one T[wo] R[ivulets], and four copies Dem[ocratic] Vistas.

I am pretty well and getting along as usual. I saw the G[ilchrist]s last evening. They are well.

If you want another copy or two, send me word, and write when you can.

W. W.
