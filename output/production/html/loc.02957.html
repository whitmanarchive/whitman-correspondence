I send you the Transcript with my notice of November Boughs—hastily pencil-scrawled bet​ jobs on my proof desk. I have really no time to myself now—exc​ a sleepy hour o' evening. The same Transcript contains a good long piece by young Sadakichi Hartmann.

Yr​ card just rec'd.​ Thank you. Be sure to tell me abt O'C. when you hear. I asked Traubel to tell you that Wilson (Glaswegian) had written me abt​ my book.

cordially yrs​ W.S.Kennedy.   See notes Oct 19, 1888
