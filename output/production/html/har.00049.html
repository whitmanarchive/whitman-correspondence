  Camden NJ Oct: 14 '83

Rec'd​ your letter—I send three photo's by same mail with this—is the profile one something like what you want?—I don't know but I have already sent you the front face with hand up, as I designed one for you—but as I am not certain I send this—I have just returned from two weeks down along the Jersey sea beach, & am well as usual—always glad to hear from you—hope we shall be together again one of these days—give my best love to Mrs. F​ and to B.O'R​ —

W W
