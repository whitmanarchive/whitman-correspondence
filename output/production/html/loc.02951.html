  Walt Whitman Camden, New Jersey, March 13. 1888.

Thanks for card. I will send paper to Dr. Bucke. Since March 1st William has been treated every A. M. at 7 with "massage," to-day was the thirteenth treatment. He is very hopeful of benefit from it. As yet no change. To-day cold very.

Love from W. & N.
