  P. Reinhalter & Co. Granite Monuments Vaults and Statuary Work. Works at West Quincy, Mass. Office 18 South Broad Street. Philadelphia, Pa. April 27' 1891 Walt Whitman Esq. Camden N.J. Dear Sir

Having taken advantage of the good weather this last few weeks gave us a chance in making a very good start on your work at the Harleigh Cemetery, we have the work over half done, now we would like very much to have you come out and see how we are progressing providing your time and your health permitts​ permit​ you to do so. Mr. Moore will call for you and take you out at any time if you be kind enough to drop us a note to the effect or if you wish to have us call at your house, we certainly wish you to see it hoping to hear from you soon we remain

Yours very truly P. Reinhalter & Co. J. E. R.
