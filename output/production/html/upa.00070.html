  Esopus April 29

All goes well—enjoyed my journey up the river that afternoon & evening—10½ when I got in—Every thing soothes, comforts, invigorates me here—the hills, rocks, sky, river, nearer & more to me than ever—

All well here—the baby superb—yesterday we spent at Po'keepsie & in Vassar College—I return to N Y​ Friday or Saturday—

W W
