Camden March 6 '89

Bright & sunny & just right temperature to-day—Dr B[ucke] has been in for half an hour—the prospects of the meter inauguration & practically being made & marketed are good—one thing & another delaying as was to be expected—but I guess it will all finále in an extensive accepted & actual & prosperous thing—So mote it be—Dr gave his piece (abt L of G &c) last evn'g to the "Ethical Society" Phila: marked success—Not physical comfortable to-day, bad head & trouble with the spleen—still here in the big arm chair with the big wolfskin back—

Walt Whitman
