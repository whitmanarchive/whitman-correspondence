328 Mickle Street Camden Oct: 13 My dear Williams

I should like the little Presidential canvass poem to go in the paper of Sunday Oct 26—I think that time would be opportune—Send me word (by note) if that suits you—& if so I will send it two or three days beforehand.

As I am writing to you I enclose the rec't for the Red Jacket bit—

Walt Whitman
