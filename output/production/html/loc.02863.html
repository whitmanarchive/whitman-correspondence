  St Louis March 1th 1870 Dear Uncle

Mama is away and we are getting along very well with out her I want to see mama and papa very much I thought when she went away I could never get along with out her but the time passed away and she will soon be back.

I got the book you sent me and I am very much oblighed​ obliged​ for it papa is coming home friday

I go to school and I study Geography Spelling arithmetic   and, Reading when mama comes back I will have more to write

Mama wrote me that she was going to see you and if she is there tell her to stay as long as long as she can that we are geting​ getting​ along very well with out her and as this is all I have to say good by from your loving neice​ niece​

Hattie Whitman
