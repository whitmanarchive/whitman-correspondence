  Camden night— Feb: 3 '91

A little uneasy at not having any word f'm you to day—letter f'm Kennedy sending back proof of his Dutch piece—he is well & getting along same as usual—letter f'm J W Wallace Eng: — the Strand the new mag—not equal to the leading American mag's—uncomfortable day—gastric worst—Sit here as usual reading & writing—Hope this letter will find you much better

W W
