  Camden P M Jan. 27 '88

Quite froze up here, but ab't as usual, tho' a trifle less well than common this winter—a bad obstinate cold in the head among the rest—I don't get out at all—I am writing a little to order—got up late to-day—had chocolate & buckwheat cakes with quince jelly for my breakfast—am sitting here by a good fire—How are you all?—I wish Ed or Jo would stop & tell me—

W W
