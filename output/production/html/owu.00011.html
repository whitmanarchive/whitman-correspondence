  Camden p m May 13 '89

yr's rec'd to-day—our friend has no doubt been buried by this time—his death hour was peaceful—I think he must have been unconscious for a good while previous—the face & all look'd peaceful & beautiful in death—Traubel has sent a short piece ab't him to the Critic—I will send you (or word of) all I hear or get.

—I have been out to-day noon in wheel chair to the river shore as secluded as I c'd find & staid over half an hour—

W W   p 161 Ksch | p 63 Kennedy
