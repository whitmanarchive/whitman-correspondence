  Camden April 20 '91

Fine weather here & Warry thinks I ought to get out doors, even if but a bit—& I may as it is not noon yet—Nothing very new or different—(of course I skip all the many bad points & botherings as you have plenty enough y'r​ own)—Lou was here Saturday—all well as usual— my 2nd annex proofs are substantially done wh'​ is a g't​ triumph & relief—Dearest Han I will send you one as soon as I get—I am sitting here alone expecting the doctor—$2 enclosed—

God bless you sister dear

Walt Whitman
