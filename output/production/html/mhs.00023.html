  Camden Sept: 8 Dear Brother & Dear Jess

Our hearts out here are with you more than you probably think at this gloomy time. I hope & trust you both bear up under it, & that the "God help us" of your telegraphic message will be fulfilled—I find myself better than toward the conclusion of former summers—am still living here at 328 Mickle st—& comfortable.

—I am anxious to hear whether Lou got there all right—am sitting here down stairs by the open window as I write—warm here for two days.

Walt Whitman
