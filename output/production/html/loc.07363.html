328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey Sunday noon Feb 14 '87

Still here in the land of the living—in pretty good heart most of the time, & comfortable enough, but horribly crippled & banged up—Spirit moved me to write you a line & send my love to Alma and Al and all—I am just going out for an hour's midday drive.

Walt Whitman
