  88 Lyon St. Ottawa 30 April 1883 Sir,

I have just finished reading, with intense pleasure, your "Leaves of Grass." Would you forgive my suggesting, as a sufficient reply to your adverse critics, the     the insertion of the following motto in your future editions. It is from the "Heauton Timorumenos" Act 1. Sc.​ 1. line 28. "Homo sum: humani nil a me alienum puto."

Yours gratefully Craig McGinnis Mr Walt Whitman, &c. &c.
