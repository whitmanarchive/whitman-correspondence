Camden March 14 '89

N[elly]'s card came this mn'g & has given me comfort all day with its favorable tidings—sent it to Dr B[ucke] (whom I have not seen for two days)—Miss Eliz: P Gould of Boston call'd to-day—quite pleasant. (Some few of my most determined friends & understanders appear to be in Boston)—Rather dull with me to-day—

W W
