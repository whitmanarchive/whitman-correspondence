  Camden 3 P M Oct: 24 '91

Feeling fairly (no mark'd bad pressure)—This is f'm John Russell Young—declined with thanks—sent word to Frank Carpenter I w'd sit—Jeannette Gilder N Y. & three charming girls just here—I consider J G, & Jo: & the Critic, old & real & valuable friends—(have never halted or wavered)—the English "nibbling" pub'rs are Heinemann & Balestier (& it w'd seem J G Lovell N Y) & I have written to Forman, asking him to prospect & negotiate with them—have given him absolute power—J W W[allace] is here—to go down with the Staffords to-morrow—shall presently make my supper of oysters & a bit of pumpkin pie—

Walt Whitman
