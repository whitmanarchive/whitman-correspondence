Camden Aug: 22, '90

Thanks for Wednesday's Herald—& indeed for all papers &c:—the calamus lozenges come [on] an occasion (rec'd quite a while ago)—Am well as usual—eat &c: heartily—hot weather here—plenty wet—am anchor'd helpless here all day but get along fairly—fortunately have a placid, quiet, even solitary thread quite strong in the weft of my disposition—

Walt Whitman
