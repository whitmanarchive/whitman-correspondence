July 31 '91

Y'rs of 22d has come (I half think the one sent immediately preceding has not come). With Dr [Bucke']s own letter I get a pretty full acc't of the Bolton visit. I am getting on abt same as ever. Y'rs and dear J. W. W[allace]'s letters help to cheer me up. I send best regards to Geo. Humphries (if you say so I will send him a copy of L. of G.) Have you rec'd the tomb photos? Its best investment—vines creepers &c.—are yet to come. Cooler and more endurable—a g't let off on me. Love to you & J. W. W. and all.
