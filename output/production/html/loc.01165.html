  Camden Oct 6 '88 Dear JB

I send you O'Connor's just rec'd, as I know you want to hear—Pretty much "the same subject continued" yet with me—I am still imprisoned in my sick room—good spirits & ab't the same mentality as ever—great blessings & remains & privileges—but after that is said pretty much every thing wreck'd else—But I must not get into complaining—nor do I fall there—Will send you Nov. Boughs, for it is done—In a month the big book—Best love—Send O'C's letter to Dr B—

Walt Whitman     10\6\88
