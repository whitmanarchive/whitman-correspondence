Washington, June 8, 1871.

I have received your kind note, dear, dear friend—I shall be with you all again next Sunday evening without fail, soon after 7.

Nelly, I have procured from New York one of the Brady Photos, (the one with the hand up, which you liked) and am having it framed, to present to you. Understand that, like the new year's Bible, the Photo is a gift, with my best love, to you & William—to pass on to dear Jeannie.

Walt Whitman
