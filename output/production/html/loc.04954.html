  March 1, 1892 Brooklyn, N.Y. 185 Sterling Place, Dear Cousin Walter,

As the Press gives us very frequent reports of your health &c we have refrained writing you. Knowing you would like to hear from us & reatives​ relatives​ we write you. Margaretta has had cough 4 or 5 weeks, some better now. I am not well, old bowel trouble. No relative here seriously ill. Have not heard from the Townsend Tribe lately. We sincerely sympathise with you and with the knowledge of your retention of intelect​ intellect​ . We join in our best Love to you, neither being able to meet you in person.

Margaretta & William A. Avery.
