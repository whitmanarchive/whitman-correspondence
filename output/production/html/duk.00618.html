  My dear Walt

i received your letter yesterday we got the papers you send walter dear i am glad you gain a little the weather here is very disagreable dark and dreary just so i feel at times dark and dreary i suppose every body has theire good feelings and bad ones) i have been writing to Jeff and speaking about matty makes me always feel so down hearted she was so good to me i cant never get reconciled to her loss never shall i find her kindness from daughter or daughter inlaw but she s gone poor soul i was glad you got a letter from Jeff   i wish he would write to me i suppose he is very busy with his extra work george too is on seems to be full of business mr Lane has empowered him to oversee his work at florence and glowcester so i dont think george will suffer for the want of employment if he dident get the brooklyn work starr dident put in a bill for the brooklyn work so its done away at easton

Lou s aunt is here has been here sometime and will stay i suppose somtime lou likes to have her here very much i suppose so we drift along walter dear some is wanted and some aint but we shall all get to our final resting place some day)

good bie dear walt we will all hope for the best  

this aint a very cheerfull letter i dont feel much like writing a good letter so dont send this to han (i am pretty well

write as often as you can dear walt
