Camden April 22 '89

The best news to-day is the saving of those 750 Danmark voyagers, wh' quite gives additional glow to this fine weather—Y'r letter and Mrs: O'C[onnor]'s rec'd—

I continue much the same—rec'd letter from Alys Smith, from Eng: from Mary's house—all ab't well—the new ed'n gets on slowly but fairly. Harned's fine baby here yesterday—splendid child—Ed has gone out for a stretch.

Walt Whitman
