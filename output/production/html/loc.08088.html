  Camden early P M Jan: 26 '91

Heavy wind & sleet storm yesterday evn'g—fine & sunny as I write—nothing emphatic or "toppish" with me—bad sulky physical cond'n mostly gastric—buckwheat cakes, honey & tea for breakfast—constipation—lansinating pains, extreme left breast—sit up here in chair—Horace T in NY—back this evn'g—good letters f'm England, Australia &c:

God bless you all— Walt Whitman

Y'rs of 24th rec'd
