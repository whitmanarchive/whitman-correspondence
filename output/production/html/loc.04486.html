  Friday. A.M. My dear Mr. Whitman

The Asherah, the little club of which I spoke to you, goes tomorrow Saturday May 21 to Gloucester. We shall go down in a tug, leaving Spruce at wharf at 5 P.M. We will call for you at any Camden wharf, if you will go down on the tug or will you meet us? If this reaches you Friday, a letter will reach me, but I think you   had better telegraph to the office any way. I hope you can go by tug or trace—one way or the other. You will like the boys, they will adore you and it will do you and us good all around

Yours Talcott Williams     T. Williams
