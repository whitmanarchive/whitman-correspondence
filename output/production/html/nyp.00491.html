Camden June 18

The date of publication here has been further postponed to the 20th (to give the London pub'n precedence)—All goes well—A week of splendid weather here—See the Critic of June 16—"Walt Whitman in Russia"—Yours of 15th rec'd​ .—You have just rec'd​ —havnt you?—a copy of the regular bound ed'n​ —Don't you like the looks of it?
