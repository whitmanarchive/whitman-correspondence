1929 north 22d st​ Phila​ June 22

Am here having a good time—Carpenter returns to Europe in Saturday's steamer—the G[ilchrist]s all well—my folks in Camden all doing well—Marvin comes here (to the G's) on 6th of July—Shall come and visit you & S[ula] this summer—

W.W.
