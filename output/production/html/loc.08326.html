Camden Evn'g: Sept: 25 '91

Y'r good letters all rec'd & welcomed—I am getting along easier than yesterday—am satisfied with my finale ed'n—(nothing new but putting things in their right & consecutive positions)—Warry is over to Phila. to see a friend sailor off for California—Good luck to you in y'r Canadian & hitherward jaunts—

Walt Whitman
