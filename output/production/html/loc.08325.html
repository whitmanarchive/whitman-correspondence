Camden New Jersey U S America Dec: 19 Evn'g '90

Y'r circular (ab't Art meeting) rec'd this mn'g—this & all letters & papers &c: welcomed—the celluloid plate promises a good impression—(it is now over in the photo–printers' in Phila: to have some printed for me)—Am poorly enough to–day, head, gastric & bladder trouble all bad. I suppose you rec'd Engineering Record of N Y. with obituary of my dear brother Jeff at St Louis—Love to you and Wallace—best respects to all the friends—sunset just shining out here—

Walt Whitman
