Dear H—

This comes here, by absurd fault of address—So I forward it to you—all goes on about the same with me—I have rec'd a handsome present of horse & light wagon—was down to Glendale all day last Sunday—all well—Ruth home, with her baby, Harry home—trouble with throat—Mr and Mrs Stafford well—

Love to you and your dear mother— W W

Sept 22
