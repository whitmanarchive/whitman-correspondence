  Dear Friend—

You know how I left you at the station to-day. I have thought of it and cannot get it off my mind, so I have come up to ask your forgiveness. I know how I have served you on many occasions before. I know that it is my falt​ fault​ and not yours. Can you forgive me and take me back and love me the same I will try by the grace of God to do better. I cannot give you up, and it makes me feel so bad to think how we have spent the last day or two; and all for my temper. I will have to controol it or it will send me to the states prison or some other bad place. Cant you take me back and love me the same.

Your lovin but bad tempered Harry.
