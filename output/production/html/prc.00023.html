Brooklyn September 13, 1869. My friends,1

I shall return to Washington either on Wednesday next, 15th or Thursday, 16th—Shall probably come in the train which gets in at 10:15 p.m.

Walt Whitman

Notes

  • 1. Probably associates in the Attorney General's office. [back]
