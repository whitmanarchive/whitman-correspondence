  134 East 38th St New York April 24, 1876 My dear Walt—

Please send 3 sets on account of my list of 5. Let 1 set be directed to me, and the others to John Russell Young, Herald office, who has written me to get a set for him, and with whom you will please communication about the mater​ matter​ .

As you may be interested in his note, I enclose it for you to look at—after which, please return.

Yours John Swinton (over)  

PS. I have just replied to Young, telling him you will forward him the set, and receive his subscription therefor.

JS.
