431 Stevens Street Camden New Jersey Aug 1 Dear Sir

Yours of July 29 rec'd—I have some copies of John Burroughs's Notes on WW as Poet & Person, 2d edition, under my control—the price is $1 a copy, sent by mail. Of my own works, complete edition, the enclosed circular will give you the particulars.

Walt Whitman
