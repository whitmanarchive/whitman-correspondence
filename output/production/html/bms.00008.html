Camden New Jersey U S America Aug: 25 '89

Y'rs f'm Wales came yesterday & was welcome (I have sent it to Dr Bucke, as he likes such)—Nothing very significant—am still here in my den in Mickle street—Keep pretty good heart, but alternations of bad & less bad health (plenty of the former)—the summer is waning here, & I have pass'd along & through it better than was anticipated—Write often as you can—Gilchrist, Kennedy, Dr B. & Traubel well—

Walt Whitman
