  Camden June 22 Evn'g Dear young friend

Yours rec'd to-day, & glad to hear from you—I have wanted to come down for quite a while, but business has delayed me. I am pretty well—& all goes well enough to be thankful for in my affairs.—Only a short word this time but I will be down soon & tell you all the news—After I write this I am going out on the river for an hour or two—

W W
