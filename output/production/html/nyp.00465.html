Camden N J Aug: 27

All going on well with me—the type-setting of "Specimen Days" will be all done next week & the book out in ten days afterwards—A Volume ab't same size, same sort of type, binding &c as L of G—same price—as I write, (Sunday afternoon) up in my 3d story room, heavy clouds, the rain falling in torrents—R[ees] W[elsh] & Co: are paying out their 3d edition—no signs of any trouble yet—

W W
