I write a word to you in haste. Three weeks since I returned from near Ithaca, whither I went after leaving Jamaica, I was completely disappointed in my expeditions there.—I am now in the city, but as Mr. Meeks by whom I send this is in hast I can write no more than to say that in the course of a few day I intend paying a visit to you all at Jamaica.

Walter Whitman Thursday afternoon Oct 21   Abm P. Leech | Jamaica L I
