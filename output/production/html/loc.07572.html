  Camden 4 PM Jan: 27 '89

Quiet & comfortable & ab't the same—y'rs came yesterday—expect to see you now before long—have the letters come via Kennedy—& the French magazine with Sarrazin's piece?—Of the latter I wish you w'd give me the running am't in translation (no hurry)—Cloudy & rainy day—

Walt Whitman
