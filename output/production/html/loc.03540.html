  Camden New Jersey U S America Jan. 22 '84—

Yours of New Year's day rec'd, with K's translations—I send you a little paper—Yes, I know Edward Carpenter—I am well as usual—A severe winter here—have had fine sleigh-rides, & enjoyed them—or some days on the river, the Delaware, on our powerful steam ferry-boat, pressing & crashing through the heavy ice.

Walt Whitman
