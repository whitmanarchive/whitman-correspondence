Camden Feb. 14 '88

I send the MS. package by express to-day, prepaid, address'd same as this card. Dr B[ucke] is at St Augustine, Florida. Rhys is here—(lectures to-night before the Contemporary Club, Phila)—I continue much as usual—Sunny & comfortable weather here to-day—Your circ. appears in N Y. Times & in Post here—

W W
