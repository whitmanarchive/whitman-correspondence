  Walt,

Here is one soul, born in the year of meteors '59, who, most unutterably has, in these last 3½ years, received the shock of thy influence.

Welcome to Britain!—especially to North Britain!—most especially to the foreground of my own certain centre of identity: we will cross orbits.

Unmistakably yours, J.J. Brown. 160 Cathcart Rd; Glasgow, Scotland. Walt Whitman. United States. America —The Great Camerado is here! The Lover True for whom we pine, He is here!!   J.J. Brown.     J.J. Brown.
