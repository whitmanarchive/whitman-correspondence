  Camden New Jersey

Am writing this late evening Dec: 31, '90, & tho't I w'd send you a line—We all have deep sympathy with you in y'r painful dislocation & sleeplessness—all continues as before here—Horace is at work at the bank all night—a splendid etching "Milton Visiting Galileo" f'm Johnston—

Jan: 1, '91—Well it arrives here in the shape of a dark stormy morning—but it will soon clear—

Walt Whitman
