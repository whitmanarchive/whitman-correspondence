  Camden April 14 '91

Am getting fairly on, considering—weather almost warm—Warry comes in advising me to try & get out a few minutes in the wheel chair (he to push it)—& I havn't​ give it up yet—But I am very feeble & listless yet & shall probably not—Dr Longaker here yesterday—he encourages me—roast apple for my breakfast, (a little 7 y'r​ old boy, friend of mine, bro't​ me two yesterday.)—

Love to you sister dear—$2 enc'd​

Walt Whitman
