  Belmont June 8 '90 Sund. eve 5 ½ o'c. My dear Friend,

I am very curious to get a fuller idea of Ingersoll's dinner speech. Thank you for the papers you sent. I suppose no verbatim record was taken?

Just been out whaling (oil soap) the rose lice. The mystery of life & solar systems lies around me, yet on I go in the petty round of petty daily duties. Am getting ready for my Western jaunt on July 7th. Saw item abt yr will.

With the full-perfumed love of my soul, I close, W S Kennedy
