Camden NJ—US America Sept: 13 '88

Still kept in my sick room & the summer season is ab't over. I sit up all day, but feebleness & inability to get about possess me enitrely.

My little 140 page November Boughs & the big 900 page Vol. Complete are nearly done. I will send them to you when ready—Gilchrist is here—

Walt Whitman
