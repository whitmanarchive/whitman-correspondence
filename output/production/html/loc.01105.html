  120 East 26th St. N.Y. Jan 12—'92 Dear Walt Whitman:

The '92 edition of Leaves of Grass which you thoughtfully sent us has arived and is an additional reminder of you, who have been so much in our thoughts of late. I will not weary you with a long letter, but say Don't give up the ship although "the prize is won"

Thanking you for the book and with hearty love from both my wife and myself—

Faithfully yours H. D. Bush       see notes Jan 13 1892 from H.D. Bush 120 East 26th St. N.Y.
