  EDITORIAL DEPARTMENT THE CENTURY MAGAZINE UNION SQUARE NEW YORK R. W. GILDER, EDITOR. R. U. JOHNSON, ASSOCIATE EDITOR. C. C. BUEL, ASSISTANT EDITOR. My dear Mr Whitman:

I have sent you by express today a new etching which I think you will like.

It has occurred to me that you might sign these as you did the photographs years ago & in case of sale derive an income from them. I will look after the   financial details & when I have any proceeds I will promptly remit them.

The etcher asks you to accept the five etchings he has signed & if you want more to let him know through me. His signature will indicate where yours should go.

Yours sincerely William Carey 5 December 1891
