  Camden Monday 9 AM My Dear Walt

As I feared the "Repub" is not bursting with Liberality—as you see by the little "ence" Bowles $8—does not seem to me capable of division but I am ready to do whatever you say, in the Beaises. I send you a bottle of the best "Gibson", this City affords. Mrs Scovel bids me say she is reading the the Gilchrist article but will return it Tuesday.

Thine Cordially James Matlack Scovel
