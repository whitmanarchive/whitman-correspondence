  Camden evn'g​ March 23 '91

Am uneasy ab't you sister dear fear you are worse than usual. Nothing new or different with me—am getting along fairly considering—the doctor here yesterday—I like him—fine sunny afternoon—wrote & sent off a piece yesterday to N Y​ paper—Am sitting here as usual in big chair have a wood fire—roast apple for my supper—best love to you Han dear 2 enclosed—
