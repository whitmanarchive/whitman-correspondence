  Camden Evn'g​ March 4 '91

Best love to you sister dear—I still keep up, & have eat my supper, but am very poorly—have just had a piece printed & paid for by magazine (N A Review March) & I enclose you 5 (give 2 to C)—Cold here, but sun out to-day. Mrs. Davis is very good—& in fact all are. I keep a stout fire—am printing my 2d annex & busy with proofs &c:—Expect to have some printed slips of the N​ A​ Rev​ : piece & when so will send you one.

God bless you, dear H Walt Whitman
