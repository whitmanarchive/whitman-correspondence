  Camden Dec: 22 1890 Dear sister Han

Merry Christmas to you first thing, from my heart and soul—Nothing very new with me—fine sunny to-day, (just after noon) and I want to get out if possible in wheel chair—Send you enclosed 10—you might give one of the 5s to C—haven't heard any thing further f'm​ Jessie f'm​ St Louis since—Y'rs​ recd​ —Love to you, sister dear—& God bless you—

Y'r​ affectionate brother Walt Whitman
