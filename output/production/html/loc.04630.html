  ans'd little pamphlet enclosed 'The Democratic Movement in Literature' 123 High Street. Musselburgh 13th November 1886 Dear Sir,

Will you accept the enclosed from one who is an admirer of your work? It is sent with the hope that it please you to know how highly in Scotland that work is appreciated.

Yours faithfully James Wilkie Walt Whitman Camden New Jersey       Wilkie Jas
