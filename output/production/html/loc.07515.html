Camden PM Jan 18 '88

Yours of 16th (? or 15th) just rec'd—I am certainly no worse in re the late physical ailments—easier more likely —ate my dinner with relish—(cold beef, potatoes & onions)—Eakins has been today painting my portrait—it is altogether different from any preceding—plain, materialistic, very strong & powerful ("A poor, old, blind, despised & dying King")—

W W
