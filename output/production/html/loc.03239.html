  Herald Office New York Feb 2 1891 My Dear Sir

May we venture to hope that you will feel moved to say a word or two for the Herald's Symposium on the anthropological and ethical question of the "Perfect Man"—or, What are the cardinal points to be insisted upon for the all around development of the coming American?

Whatever you may tell us will be of real value to the cause of science.

Very Sincerely Wallace Wood
