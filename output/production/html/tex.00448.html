328 Mickle St: Camden Nov: 9 '85 Dear T D

Yours rec'd with L[awrence] B[arrett]'s check & the ferry pass—Many & best thanks—I will come over & see you all soon.

I am in good spirits, & somewhat better, but fearfully lame & disabled yet—go out with the horse & buggy every afternoon—

W W
