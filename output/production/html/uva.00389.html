According to your letter just rec'd​ I forward the two Vols.​ L of G and T R by same mail with this. Please send me the pay $7 here by mail2

Notes

  • 1. This postal card is addressed: A Williams & Co: | Booksellers | 283 Washington Street | Boston Mass:. It is postmarked: Camden | Nov | 1 | N.J. [back]
