  Camden New Jersey Evn'g May 27 '91

Y'rs rec'd, welcomed (two to day) of course come down but not put y'rself out badly—things tho' bad enough are not essentially worse—a bowel movement to-day, first in a week—a splendid (very short, very sweet) autograph word f'm Tennyson—yes that is essentially the controling more or less invisible clef to "Good-Bye," but needs much expatiation & patient waiting for—

W W
