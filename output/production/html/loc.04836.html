  Anderton, nr Chorley Lancashire, England 18. Feb. 1892. Dear Walt,

Your letter of Feb 6th & 7th to Johnston to hand today. We have just spent the evening together—first at his house, then in a 4 mile walk in the country here—Johnston returning by train. Snow on ground, air clear &     frosty, stars large & lustrous—Venus & Sirius especially so.

We intend to have exact fac-similes of your letter lithographed & to send them to all your friends.

How deeply your letter has stirred us I cannot tell you. Our hearts best love to you for ever.

God bless you. Wallace
