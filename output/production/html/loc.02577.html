  Telephone Call: 916 21st Street. New York, Sept 25 18 90 Dear Walt:

I inclose you a letter which I wrote you last week and directed wrong as you will see—

Two letters from Traubel this morning, convince me that everything considered Phil. is the place, and I think you good folks had better go right ahead and I know it will be a great success,

Excuse haste Sincerely yours J H Johnston

Yes—I do appreciate what you lost when there was no reporter to take down Ingersoll's  speech. Bertha never tires of talking of it, and Ingersoll has spoken to me of it so often that I know what it must have been. He is a very wonderful man.

J.H.J.   See notes Sept 26 '90
