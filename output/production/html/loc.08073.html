June 25 '91— Camden N J—America

Am feeling fairly considering—first I must tell you do not bother ab't July Lippincotts, as the birth day acc't is not printed, for some reasons, (probably over crowd of matter)—Y'rs of 11th & 17th rec'd with the yellow fac similes wh' are capital—(with them, & such matters, give them out & do as you feel to—I stand by the fac simile)—Wallace's dear letter also rec'd—ab't the p.o. order you sent, I consider it overpaying—I shall retain it, but don't do any thing like that again—I sh'l send the picts (& more books too)—

Love to you & all— Walt Whitman
