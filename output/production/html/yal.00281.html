  328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey June 20 '87

Thank you my friend for the delicious chocolate—I have it for my breakfast frequently, & enjoy it—Please accept a copy of my little book "Specimen Days" London ed'n. which I send for the young folks.

Walt Whitman

It is just possible I have sent you a note of acknowledgment before—but I think not as I have been ill. (I suppose Pearsall Smith was the cause of your sending it to me.)
