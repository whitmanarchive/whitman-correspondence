Camden N J—U S America March 19 Evn'g '91

Nothing worse at any rate—the Dr. has just been here & his talk & atmosphere are cheering. The book "Holland" rec'd—thanks—have just had my supper, some stew'd mutton & rice—It is 6 but quite light yet—Loving remembrances to J W W[allace]

Walt Whitman
