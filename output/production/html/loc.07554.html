Camden Evn'g: Dec: 4 '88

Yours of 2d rec'd—all goes along—a bad week past—(a combination of prostrate gland trouble & diabetes coming to the front)—Shall tell you more when Osler's mate gives his verdict to-morrow—Had a better night this last than for a week—Fine weather here, day & night superb—God bless you all—

Walt Whitman
