  Camden pm Feb: 11 '91

Y'rs rec'd last evn'g—fine sunny day—am sitting here as usual—have been preparing a list of addresses for the Lip: people to send March No. to—am feeling fairly—head congestion—enc: the Dutch piece—you can have some H T's paper cont'g it—(will be out in four or five days)—as many of it, or Lip: as you want—poor Jas Redpath is just dead in N Y—I knew him well—The papers here are talking largely of the Canadian situation—

Walt Whitman
