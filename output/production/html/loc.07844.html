Camden New Jersey Evn'g Oct: 3 1890

Y'rs asking me to write piece for Review &c: rec'd—Yes, (and thanks)—Just now my chosen theme and head-line w'd be "Old Poets—(and other things.)" It takes in the subject you mention too. If that sh'd suit I w'd send you in a week—

Walt Whitman

I must apologize to (I think) Mr Bryce anent of his letter & request quite long ago—I was critically ill at the time & the letter got neglected—
