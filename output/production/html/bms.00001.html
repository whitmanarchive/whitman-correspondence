328 Mickle Street—Camden New Jersey U S America —Oct. 13 1886

First I ought to apologize for not answering your letters before—I am always glad to get them—Nothing specially new or different with me—I am willing you should print "Specimen Days" in your series—let W[alter] S[cott] send me what he thinks he can afford, & I shall want 10 copies of the book. I should advise you to leave out the Appendix—If you want it further cut down, let me furnish you with a prepared copy—

Walt Whitman
