  Superintendent's Office. 
 ASYLUM FOR THE INSANE 
 LONDON. 
  ONTARIO 
 London, Ont., 11 Aug 1888

I have your card of 9th Harkness has been here a couple of days we put in the time quietly and pleasantly together chatting, driving, &c &c. We have been friends now 30 years. He will stay till middle of next week. Weather keeps pleasant—we have had plenty of rain & the trees & grass are very green. Mrs Bucke still away and the house quiet—not to say dull. We have whist in the evening often and we all enjoy it—No news.

Love to you RM Bucke     See notes Aug 13, 1888
