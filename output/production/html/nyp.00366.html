Tuesday, 9th Nov.​  
 11 a.m. Dear Nelly,

I am in Washington, stopping at Mr. & Mrs. Nash's, L st. south east, Navy Yard—am middling well, for me—shall be in pretty sure up to 10 a.m. & from 2 to 3 p.m. Am so fixed that it is very convenient for my friends to call—Love to you.

Walt W
