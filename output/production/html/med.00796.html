Camden March 29 Evn'g

Somewhat under a cloud in physical condition & feelings these days—but will pass over I reckon—I am billed to give the "Death of Lincoln" lecture in New York, April 14—Same splurge here before the Unitarian Society April 5—A good letter lately from Rhys—Nothing further ab't O'Connor—

Walt Whitman
