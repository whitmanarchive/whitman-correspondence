Camden NJ—U S America Dec: 1 '91

Y'rs rec'd—also J W W[allace]'s—thanks—(I can see you all with y'r home-reception to dear W. & the jolly time—so mote it be)—Dr B[ucke] & H T[raubel] well—sunny & cold weather—bad & depress'd physical condition night & day—no hour without suffering—get a new book "Modern Authors, by Arthur Lynch, pub'd London, Ward & Downey, 12 York St. Covent Garden."

W W
