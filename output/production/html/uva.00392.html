  Camden Jan 25 Dear John Burroughs

I havn't been able to think of any thing worth while in the way of a name—to my notion Locusts and Wild Honey is the best proposed—(the Speckled Trout piece suggested to me whether the fish couldn't afford a name for one of your books, for a change)—

—Nothing new with me—I am well, for me—I send you a Phila: paper with a letter—Cold winter here—

Walt     1/25/'79 | Locusts & Wild Honey (1879)
