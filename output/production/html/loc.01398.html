  Camden NJ US America Nov: 18 '90—

Seems a long time since I have heard f'm or written to you—Am still here (still holding out after a fashion)—am writing a little, mentality—(sort o') & good right arm—I sent you my "Old Poets" essay, & shall send bits when printed—

Love to the childer & all Walt Whitman
