  sent 2 papers Aug 20, 1875 Aug 16th 1875 Dear Uncle Walt

I received your Postal Card. but I was away to work when my wife received it.

I have been 75 to 100 miles West of here working in the Harvest fields

She ought to have answered it before this

I am sorry that you do keep so miserable and feabel​ feeble​

I do hope by the time this reaches you that you are better

 

I hardly know what to write to you But I will say that we are well as common

I am going to California in November if the Lord will permit that my life may be spared

Walt I wish that I was abel​ able​ to come and take care of you how happy I would be to see your Dear face once more

God bless you Ruben Farwell Nankin Po​ Wayne Co​ Mich​

Wright​ Write​ soon Dear Uncle Walt

 

address Nankin Wayne Co Michigan
