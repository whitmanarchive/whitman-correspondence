  Camden New Jersey—U S America March 30 evn'g '91

Nothing special to write ab't—but tho't I w'd forward you a line—Still keep the fort (sort o') & have had a glum winter—but signs of spring opening—have the good photo you sent me on my wall here, & y'r last Essays handy—so you see you are not forgotten—am reading printer's proofs of very little 2d annex concluding L of G.—shall send you soon as printed—

God bless you— Walt Whitman
