London Ontario Canada July 24 p m

Am all right again for me—was sick ab't​ three weeks—at times pretty bad—was well taken care of here—the best of friends both Dr and Mrs B[ucke] (as human as I ever met, both)—Monday morning next I start on a long Lake & St Lawrence river trip, 900 miles (mostly by steamer, comfortable, I reckon)—gone nearly three weeks, then back here—May write you from Quebec. Your papers come—

W W
