Ho! Help God! The Quaker Theater to the rescue

Oh God! Thou who made this World, (And worlds wider) And all the little people in it, Forgive the littlest of the lot When thy run up to take thy part. Thou needst deference,—Thou must be cared for. Such men as Ingersoll and Whitman Thy masters are:— So think the Little ones.— Forgive them, God, for thinking Thou a Pygmy.
