  Camden Nov:​ 26 '91

Feeling bad enough these days but made quite a good breakfast stew'd​ chicken—Mrs: D made a great kettle of nice doughnuts yesterday, but I havn't​ tackled them yet (I wish I c'd​ send some to you)—bladder trouble & head bring me much suffering—(This is quite a hospital Mickle St around here)—sell some of my books—the Eng:​ proposal wanted American copyright too wh'​ will probably make the whole thing fall through—Love to you sister dear—$2 enc'd​

W W
