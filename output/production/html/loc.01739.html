  1873 Dear Pete,

I have been very unwell—but am better again—at least at the present moment.

I am stopping at Mr. Ashton's, 1202 K st. next door to the southwest cor. of K and 12th—Come up & see me.—I wrote you a line two days ago, to Milburn's—Did you not get it?

Walt.
