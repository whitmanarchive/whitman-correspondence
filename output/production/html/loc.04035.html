431 Stevens Street Camden New Jersey Nov 27 My dear Reid

If convenient please send me the pay for the Gathering the Corn article ($10) published Oct: 241—

All goes about as usual with me—

Walt Whitman

Notes

  • 1. See Whitman's letter to Reid of September 21, 1878. Whitman received payment on December 6 (Commonplace Book, Charles E. Feinberg Collection of the Papers of Walt Whitman, 1839–1919, Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.). [back]
