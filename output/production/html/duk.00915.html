Camden Evn'g Feb: 25 '89

Yours of 22d rec'd—I send the little German trans: from Zurich (At y'r leisure, give me a sort of English abstract of Knortz's and Rolleston's prefaces in front)—I am quite unwell—Dr B[ucke] will probably be here to-morrow evn'g—I have rec'd a letter f'm Sarrazin Paris—he has the big "complete" book—his book (with L of G. &c article) will be out soon & he will send me one, wh' I will lend you—I suppose you rec'd the Dr B trans: of S I sent—

Walt Whitman
