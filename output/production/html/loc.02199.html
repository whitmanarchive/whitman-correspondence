  1708 Chestnut Street 
 Phila. Pa. U.S.A. Saturday morning, 1st December 1888 My dear Walt,

I want to introduce to you my friend Edward Pease of London (or more properly Newcastle)—he will be able to tell you about your numerous friends in Newcastle & London.

When my dear mother was initiating "The Free Will Offering" in London Edward Pease was the first to render advice & practical assistance, all of wch​ he did in a very charming way. Our friend is of well known Quaker stocke.— He is going stay some little time in Philadelphia. I hope to come over & see you soon—

With all love— Herbert Gilchrist         H H Gilchrist
