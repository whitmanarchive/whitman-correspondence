Camden March 15 '88

I continue on ab't as usual. O'C[onnor] is taking massage treatment—your last Transcript went from me to him, & so to Dr Bucke—I still write bits for the Herald—they pay me quite well—& then it is a sort of spur or fillip—A fearful four-day spell of cold, snow & gale here, but I have not felt it—the sun is shining as I write—

Walt Whitman
