328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey US America Aug: 2 '86

A pleasant forenoon as I write, here by the open window. I remain ab't as usual—a little pull'd down by the heat perhaps. Your letter came, & was welcome, as always—Alys's "circular" came, & now one from Romsey arrived this morn'g—I had a letter from Dr B[ucke] two days ago— all well—

Walt Whitman
