Jan 27 noon

Feeble & weak & restless but not without favorable points —appetite holds out—eat two meals every day—bowel movement every day (rather strange after such a long interregnum)—McK[ay] was here—paid me $283—I enc' two adv't slips—to me the 1892 ed'n supersedes them all by far—adv. intended for N Y Trib[une]—God bless you—

W W
