  Studio June 2 91 Our beloved brother Walt

Han has not be as well—Hears that you are not so well. grieves that she cannot write to you with her own hands—appreciates you intensely—

And yet Walt she can come down stairs, get her own breakfast—and care for her own room. her method and spirit remains—perservering—.

I assist—do washing—ever have—raise water from the well—purchase all she requires or calls for.

I have written to Geo and Lou—but without avail—

Lou could make her a visit, without spending as much money, as she does on tea parties—as she has written—

Charlie
