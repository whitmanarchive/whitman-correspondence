  328 Mickle street Camden New Jersey June 23 '85 Dear friend

I have no idea of going abroad—couldn't do it anyhow—as I am very lame & find it difficult to get about here, even small distances—Otherwise I should have been on to see Alma and you—for my wishes have been with you both—My general health however is very fair.  I am getting along here better than you think for—for very little suffices me—

As I write it is beautiful weather—temperature perfect—Love to Alma & all—

Walt Whitman
