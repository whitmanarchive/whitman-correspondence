  Saturday My dear Walt

i have received severall papers this week but no letter that has been written this week i got one wrote last friday a week ago yesterday but i hope you are right well as they say here lou has had quite a run of company this week mostly girls from the place where she used to work all have to be taken up in my room i stayed down in the kichen part of the time their discourse was not interesting to me but i got through it every body that comes has to be taken up here i dont like it sometimes

 

there is no fire any where else to take any one but let it slide i have had a letter from helen price very good one she says mrs davis wont return till next spring and then katy is hinds is going to urope the quen that josepheine sailed in has arrived so i hope Jo is all safe so far) george has a prospect of getting the brooklyn work he went on there last monday to see and adams gave him encouragement to think he would get it i hope of course he will but to hear the talk about money you would think their means was limited) lou says the rent is more than it costs for provisions) write walter dear the first of the week
