  Meltonsville Ala Aug 5 1876

Walt Whitman 431 Stevens St Cor West Camden New Jersey

 

If all people could derive a practical Theory of Living, from the incident noted 20 years ago, of the European Nobleman, who being imprisoned solitarily in a dark dungeon; conquered his tedium, by continually throwing away and recovering a lot of brass pins! How often restlessness produces criminality! How fortunate to leave to be content with doing nearly nothing—with [illegible] My soul, for twenty [illegible] purturbability!"— [illegible] remain gentlemanly [illegible]
