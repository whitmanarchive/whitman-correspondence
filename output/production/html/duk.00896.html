Camden Evn'g Jan 24 '88

The MS-package has just come, all right, freight p'd—I am perhaps better than three weeks ago—the cold pinches me—very cold here for over a week—What of Rhys?—Did he get the five letters I sent?—I have been invited (by letter of J G B[ennett]) to write for the N Y Herald—

W W
