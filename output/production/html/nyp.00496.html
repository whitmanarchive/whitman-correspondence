Camden Aug 29

Have finished my Germantown visit & am back here. I did not send Dr B[ucke]'s book to Mrs. Ritter—have indeed not sent copies to any except my sisters and neices​ . Mrs. Gilchrist, however, has a copy—John Burroughs also has—Glad you sent one to Tucker—he is a good friend—I have not heard any thing more of Rolleston's German translation. Dr Karl Knortz, cor: Morris av. & 155th st. New York City, has translated many of my poems in German, & published them. Do you know the Nation is made up of Evening Post matter?

W W
