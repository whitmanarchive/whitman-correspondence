 N. Jersey, April 23, 1874. My dear niece,

I received your nice good letter—and also Hattie's a week or two before—& have been very much pleased with them. I want you both to continue. I am not much different in my health—no worse. All the rest here are well. This little cut picture was one I intended to send last Christmas, but it got lost in my papers—so I enclose it now, for fun—

Best love to you, dear child, & to my dear Hattie too, from Uncle Walt—

I will write to Hattie soon—
