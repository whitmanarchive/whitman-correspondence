  Camden Sunday Evn'g Aug: 12 '88

Nothing very new or different—I am still in my sick room—Sit up most of the day—write some & keep pretty good spirits—but am as weak & feeble as ever. (Sometimes I opine will remain so.) I think Herbert is coming to America soon—may be this month. It is raining all day like fury—coolish—

Walt Whitman
