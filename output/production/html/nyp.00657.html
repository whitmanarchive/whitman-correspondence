Camden Evn'g March 12 '89

Sunny & fine here—& ab't the same as usual with me—several orders more for the big book—hope you are over & resuscitated & comfort-cheering—but able again from the attacks of six days ago—have an idea you have a good doctor (Dr Hood) wh' is a great point—Lawyer Harned & Dr [Bucke], & Horace are just off to a swell supper.

W W
