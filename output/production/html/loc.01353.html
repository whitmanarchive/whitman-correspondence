  Camden New Jersey U S. America Sept 27 '86

Yours from Etretat rec'd & welcomed warmly—also the picture—Logan and Alys have arrived—I have not seen them, but shall soon no doubt—I remain much as usual.—I drove out a long jaunt Sunday—Yes, dear M, I have no objection to your writing or collating the "reminiscences" you speak of—actual occurrences, anecdotes, conversations, delineations, personalities, plus—criticisms minus—

W W
