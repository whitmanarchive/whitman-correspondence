  Camden March 29 Evn'g

Harry was here with me to-day—He went to the hosp. to have his throat drest, & then to the RR station, to see J and D off.—He is doing very well indeed.

—I shall not be down next Sunday, but may the next, April 10, if it is pleasant—I am only middling.

Walt Whitman
