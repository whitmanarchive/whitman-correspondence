  Get Mr. Travers [?] to mail | a copy & send | it in closed envelope | the other was lost in the mail | JR # 3 447-1928 328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey Dec: 15 '85—noon My dear J R

I have rec'd the pay ($33) for the "Lincoln" article from Mr Rice, & sent a receipt for it—

—Your letter (12th) says you have sent the proof of "Lincoln" article same mail—I have rec'd no proof.—It has evidently miscarried, or something. So please send me another—None was enclosed in the letter—

—I am ab't as usual—Come & see me whenever you can.

Walt Whitman
