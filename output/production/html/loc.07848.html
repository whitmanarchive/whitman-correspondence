  Camden Sunday noon Oct: 26 '90

Fine sunny day—ab't right as usual—had a fair night—letter f'm Niagara came—the oculist Dr Thomas came late yesterday & examined & will furnish me with suitable glasses (a satisfactory friendly visit)—I send you to-day's Press with half correct half fraudulent report of the little Lafayette talk (good transcript of the Murger poemet)—Warry and Mrs: D are off to Phil. to-day—shall try to get out in wheel chair this afternoon. (Don't know for certain)—This is the 4th I have sent since y'r departure.

God bless you both & all— Walt Whitman
