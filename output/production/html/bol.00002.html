  328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey U S America Aug: 3d '85 Dear Bessie and Isabella Ford

I will at least write a line to say I have safely received your and Edward Carpenter's affectionate generous gift of £50. It has come very opportunely & I thank you all—I have been prostrated lately by sun-stroke, but am well recovering from it—

God bless you, dear friends, Walt Whitman
