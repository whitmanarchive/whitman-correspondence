  Superintendent's Office 
 ASYLUM FOR THE INSANE 
 LONDON. 
  ONTARIO 
 London, Ont., 10 Aug 1888

Your card of 8th to hand this morning. Dr. Harkness came yesterday afternoon, he will make a short visit, 5 or 6 days, with me—he is well. The weather continues pleasant here not at all unpleasantly hot. There is nothing special stirring. I should like to hear that you are gaining strength—I do not hear that—how is it? Nothing back from Willy Gurd—I still hope I may see you sometime next month but nothing certain about it

Your friend RM Bucke     R.M. Bucke See notes Aug 12, '88
