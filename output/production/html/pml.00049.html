Kirkwood N J​ Monday afternoon Aug​ 20 My dear friend,

I forward you the accompanying rec'd​ to-day by me. The other two will you please take, or mail to my sister in Camden, soon as you have read?

I am here yet, living a free outdoor life, alone with the pond & trees, lots of wild flowers, lots of butterflies (those in season now)—Shall report myself to you soon as I come up—

WW
