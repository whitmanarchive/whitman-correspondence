Camden Aug:​ 1 '91 Dearest sister,

I have just a minute before the mail & enc:​ $5—makes me uneasy & wretched [e]nough ab't​ y'r​ sickness & c:—hope & pray you may be easy by this time reaches you—I hope Dr B[ingham] will write to me forth–with if convenient—

Best love to you, sister dear—

Walt Whitman
