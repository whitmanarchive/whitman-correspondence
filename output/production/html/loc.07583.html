Camden Evn'g March 19 '89

Rec'd the Saturday Review with notice of Nov: B. wh' I will send you soon (thro' O'C[onnor])—Nothing different to write ab't—am rather easier in head &c.—a dark half rainy day not cold—sold two books to-day—am sitting here as usual in the big chair dawdling over the papers &c—Rather wonder I feel as well & hearty even as I do—

Walt Whitman
