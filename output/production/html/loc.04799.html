  Camden N J— Sundown Sept: 29 '91

Just had my supper, some oysters, bread & coffee—a rare fried egg for breakfast—(so you see I am not so ill but I eat something)—To day cloudy & rain, welcome change to me f'm​ the glaring sun & strange heat of the last ten days—Lou has been here—they are all well—yr's​ rec'd​ , dearest sister, & glad enough to get it—I sit here ab't​ same—might be much worse (may be shall be)—my books selling so so, lucky to keep up & sell at all—I will send Dr Bingham one—Best love to you—2 enc'd​

W W
