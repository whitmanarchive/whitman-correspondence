Camden P M Aug: 4 '89

Nothing very new—have not left my Mickle St: quarters this summer (hardly can)—am feeling fairly to-day—my friend Traubel has written (at their request) & sent on to "Liberty" (Boston) a piece abt Wm wh' if they print I will send you—I suppose you have heard from Ch's Eldridge at San Francisco (Revenue Ag't there)—

Best love— Walt Whitman
