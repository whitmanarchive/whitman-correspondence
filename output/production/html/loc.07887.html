Camden N J—U S America Feb: 8 '91—Evn'g

Nothing special to write but tho't I w'd send a word—Ab't as usual with me—a bad two months—Keep up tho'—Y'rs & Wallace's letters, mag's & papers rec'd—best thanks & love to you both—Dr B[ucke] has had a short sharp sick spell but is now out again—Warry well—

Walt Whitman
