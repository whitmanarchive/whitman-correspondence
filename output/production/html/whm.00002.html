328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey September 3 1884 Dear Mr Booth

Thank you for sending me the volume the Booths—It does not furnish me the picture I wanted—(I am very particular & shall not be satisfied till I get one that fills the bill)—But the volume is more helpful to me (in touches, corrections, guidance &c to my piece) than I can describe—& the reading of all about your father, of absorbing interest—He was a beautiful character.

Walt Whitman
