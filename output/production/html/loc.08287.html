Correspondent:
Remington Ward (1858–1921), the oldest child of A. Judson Ward (1830–1908) and Harriet F. Gould Ward (1830–1915), was a printer and stationer, and he was the proprietor of a printing office in his native Rhode Island. He was married to Myrtle Bull Ward (1871–1937), who had been born in Canada. The couple were the parents of at least two children.
