Camden Wednesday P M Sept: 19 '88

Still here in my sick room. I sit up—occasional visitors—bad weather lately—I rec'd a note from Rolleston (Ireland) that he had got first proofs of the German trans of L of G—I sent word to Dr Knortz but no answer comes—I get word often from Dr Bucke—the printing of November Boughs and the complete Vol. proceeds fairly—I am feeling satisfactory. H. Gilchrist here yesterday—

Walt Whitman
