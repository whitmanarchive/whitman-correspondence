  Dec​ 11 '76 My dear Linton

I have been for some weeks down in the country—half moping like—yet feeling rather better these days, upon the whole—Your note of some weeks since ought to have been answered before. I have been waiting for the chance to get from the bindery, or from my stack, (as I unwrap the books) a copy of Two Rivulets with the dark brown label you want—I have it in mind, & shall get it so, & send it you—   Meantime, let this remorseful note be my apology—

—My address here is still the same—

Walt Whitman Camden New Jersey
