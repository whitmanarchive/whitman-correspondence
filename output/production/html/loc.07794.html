Camden April 25 '90

Dark rainy day—warm—still under the bad influence of the grip—A London Eng: pub'r (I suppose pub'r) has sent to McKay asking my price for 100 sets sheets complete (big) book—I have ans'd $320 for them complete—suppose you rec'd the printed item acc't of the 15th April show—y'r letters rec'd & welcomed—Have sent off a page of poetic stuff (new) to Lippincott's—Did you know Tennyson has been talking very strongly in favor of L of G?—Ditto some big college gun at Boston—

W W
