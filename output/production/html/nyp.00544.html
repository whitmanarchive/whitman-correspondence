328 Mickle st—Camden— March 26

Am ab't the same as usual—Had a bad spell two weeks ago, but am now around after my sort, nearly the same (a letting down a little peg, if no more, every time)—Yes I have had superb treatment from my English friends—Yours of 23[?]d rec'd—I dont know whether I told you that a young Englishman who came into a fortune not long since, sent me 50 pounds—then the Nineteenth Cent paid me 30£ for the little poem—

W W
