  dear walt

i got your letter on sater with the 5 dollars and got one to day i am not feeling very well walter dear i am very nervious and have such a trembling in my whole system my appetite is very poor i dont know what ails me i feel bad maybee nothing Lou is not well to day she went out yesterday and came home sick

the 1 of next month if you are able you must try to come on here lou s aunt goes to nurse somebody then and the room will be vacant

  From Camden 1873
