Camden Evn'g Dec: 21 '88

Thanks for your good letter rec'd this forenoon—I sympathize with William as my brother in affliction, besides in soul—so wish I could show it practically—Very little by little & slowly, I seem to be improving—Ab't the worst is the forced confinement (now seven months) to in-doors & the sick room

Walt Whitman
