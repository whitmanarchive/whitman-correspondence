Camden Dec 7 Dear Miss Gilder

Yours rec'd—and I send you some thoughts of mine on Burns—(a much belabored subject—but I wanted to have my say)—Will make two & a half pages—I sh'd like $20 for it—

If you take it to print in next send me proof by Monday or Tuesday of next week & I will return same evening—

Walt Whitman
