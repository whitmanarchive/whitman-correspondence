  328 Mickle St: Camden, New Jersey U S America Feb: 18 1889

Nothing special or very different—continue laid up & imprison'd in sick room—y'rs of a week ago rec'd & welcom'd—I want to send over some copies of my big book (works complete) one for you of course, & think of enveloping them stoutly & sending by ocean express—(to be call'd for there, or perhaps sent by local express)—& may send the parcel if you are willing—congratulate Mr. C on his success—

Walt Whitman
