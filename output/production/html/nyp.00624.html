Camden Evn'g Jan 31 '89

Have had a favorable day in my condition—fine sunny pleasantly cool weather—hope much it has been easy with you—Dr B[ucke]'s establishm't has had a fire—one of the "cottages"—no loss of life or limb, but makes work & delay for him—A French periodical la Nouvelle Revue has given L of G. a mighty good notice wh' I shall soon send you a brief of trans:—Love & cheer to you—

Walt Whitman
