  Camden New Jersey June 30 Evn'g: 1890

I am well as usual—Hot weather here—eat moderately—dress light—bathe frequently—some one has sent me Volney's Ruins, a fine added-to ed'n—carries me back 60 y'rs—(my father had a treasur'd copy)—go out almost daily in wheel chair—have just had my supper—God bless you all—

Walt Whitman
