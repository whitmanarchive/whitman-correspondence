  Camden P M June 30 '91.

Warm wave continued—Y'rs of 28th welcom'd—Ab't same with me as of late—head-ache (think eat too much meat lately)—my bro: George here on quite a long visit to-day—Warry has taken notion to go on to N Y & will see you if convenient at 4 P M on board the Britannic on Tuesday 7th—he has one or two things (small) to hand over to you—one to go to Bolton—(returns here same evn'g)—be there at 4 P M Tuesday 7th—

Last evn'g rec'd word that my sister at Burlington Vt: is much better—great weight off my mind—(very ill lately—jaundice)—bowel motion yesterday—Chs: W Eldridge here yest'y—sit here in my shirt sleeves in big chair by the window—no decision yet ab't H. M. & Co: pub'ng O'C's book—Enc'd piece is f'm Boston Trans: safe & jolly voyage to you—

Walt Whitman   June 30
