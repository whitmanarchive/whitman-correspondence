  S.S. "British Prince." Voy. No. Oct. 8 1890 Phila Dear Walt Whitman

I have been honored with a small commission by W. Wallace and Dr. Johnston of Bolton England for you, a blanket of Bolton manufacture. I shall take the opportunity of calling with it at yr earliest leisure about Noon of one day this week, unless inconvenient to you, when I will send it.

Trusting you are in good health & hoping to renew my acquaintance with you

I am your faithfully S. Nowell
