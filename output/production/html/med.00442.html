April 13.

Your letter in 'Tribune' today headed 'W. W.'s Poetry' is like an artillery and bayonet charge combined—is splendid, earnest, and terribly live—(Wonder the T——ever printed it)—Bad spell this forenoon—F. B. Sanborn visited me today—Am going out and will see if I can get the T——to send you—am not sure—

W.W.
