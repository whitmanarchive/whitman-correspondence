Camden N J—U S America Aug: 11 '91

Thank dear J W W[allace] for his cheery pen picture (in letter of the old sea shore farm house to which you & the rest went for y'r outing)—I am still holding out ag't the heat & other lesions—pretty bad—but as I just told Warry, If they get their backs up we must show we can get our backs up too—

Love to you & all— Walt Whitman
