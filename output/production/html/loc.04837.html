  Anderton, nr Chorley. Lancashire, England 23. Feb 1892 Dear Walt,

Our dearest love to you always, & our tenderest sympathy & prayerful good wishes.

I wish that I were there to see you, & by a kiss to indicate this love that words are powerless to express.

Horace & Warry   both write us & every day a message comes. Every day we think of you with a tender love that only grows deeper and is immortal

God bless you! Yours affectionately always Wallace
