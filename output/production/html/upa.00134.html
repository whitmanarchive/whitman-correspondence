  BOOKSELLERS AND PUBLISHERS.  
  23 South Ninth Street, Philadelphia, 6.5 1882 Walt Whitman Esq Camden N.J. Dear Sir

You mentioned the other day while in the store, that you had not, as yet, made arrangements for another publisher, for your book. If you have not already made terms we will be pleased to see you or have you write us, with a view to our making the Leaves of Grass

Trusting you will consider above favourably

We are Sir, Very Truly yours Rees Welsh & Co. mck  upa.00134.002_large.jpg
