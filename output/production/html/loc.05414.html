  Bln Sep 16/91 Dear Cousin Walter

Most the time I am in the house, being weak & unwell. Margta is quite well, and often refers to her very pleasant visit to you about this time last year. We hear of you often through newpapers &c

We send our love to you,

As Ever Mgta & Wm A. Avery 185 Sterling Place.     NY World. Sep 16/91
