  Camden June 11 '91 Evn'g:

Y'rs of yesterday just rec'd—Horace must make up the acc't, & send it (or bring it) in double-quick time—for Stoddart is in a stew ab't it & is keeping the mag: back for it—If he H. gets here to furnish S with it Monday even'g, I guess he S will be mollified—if not, not. He has persuaded me to sign a hasty page (criticism) I sent for him to use or throw away—wh' he intends to put so verbatim in mag.—Letters f'm Bolton to-day—good weather, warm—I have just made my supper—some string beans & a dish Mary Davis makes very nicely, tomatos stew'd with onions & crumbs of well toasted bread—(a dish my mother made, & I inherit a weakness for)—But ab't as capital an item to transmit this even'g is of a fair bowel evac: this noon almost normal,& the best in two months. My idea for the scheme of y'r full book w'd be—

  • a rambling free art: by you
  • another by Horace
  • Sarrazin
  • the poet of Joy
  • Rolleston's Dresden lecture
  • Kennedy's Dutch Piece
W W
