  328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey April 21 '87—

I sh'd like all the proofs—(all that are taken, without exception)—sent to me here—I will return them, with what I have to say—Send them flat—if convenient—

Hand this note to Mr Cox—I am all right—rec'd $600 for my lecture. Andrew Carnegie sent me $350 for his box—

Walt Whitman
