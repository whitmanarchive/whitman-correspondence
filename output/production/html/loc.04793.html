How are you standing the hot weather, sister mine—if it is anything like here—two days now & probably more & worse to come—yet I get on fairly considering—eat very little (have no appetite)—had a fair night last &c:—just had my supper, bread & cold tea—$2 enc'd​ —y'r​ welcome card rec'd​ [illegible]
