  EDITORIAL OFFICE OF THE GALAXY, NO. 40 PARK ROW, New York, Mar 4, 1869 My dear Sir:

I am sorry not to be able to use your poem in the April Galaxy.

I supposed the poem had gone back to you     as it should have done some time ago.

I am Very truly yours, F. P. Church

Mr. Walt Whitman
