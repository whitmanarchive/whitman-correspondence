328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey Nov: 30 '85 My dear Wm Rossetti

Yours of Nov: 13 with 31 pounds 19 shillings has been received—the third instalment of the "offering"—my thanks are indeed deeper than words.

I have just been writing to Herbert Gilchrist ab't his mother, & am filled with sadness—nothing new with me, only my eyesight is better—

Walt Whitman
