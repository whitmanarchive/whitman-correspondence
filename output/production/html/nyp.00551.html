328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey Aug. 24 p m

Charles Eldridge was here yesterday noon—a pleasant 3 hour visit—went to Atlantic City in the 5 p m train en route for Boston—

Nothing very different with me—I get quite a good many letters from Europe (some buyers)—rec'd a German (Wisconsin) paper with a long notice of L of G. & me, to–day—Fine weather here, & I am alone.

Love to you— Walt Whitman
