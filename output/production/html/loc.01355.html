    Camden Jan. 3 '87—P M

Henry Norman, of the Pall Mall Gazette has sent me £81 over, in a very kind & good letter—enclosing some printed slips from paper—one written by you ab't my Camden entourage—very satisfactory & right to me—In the Reminiscences stick as much as possible to personal descriptions, anecdotes, & sayings—& don't make me too good—I am no angel by a long shot

Walt Whitman
