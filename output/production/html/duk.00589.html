  24 March 1870 24 O walt

aint it sad to think the poor soul hadent a friend near him in his last moments and to think he had a paupers grave i know it makes no difference but if he could have been buried decently but hes done poor fellow i was thinking of him more lately than common i wish walter you would write to Jeff and hann that he is dead i will write to george i feel very sad of course walter if he has done ever so wrong he was my first born but gods will be done

good bie walter dear  

walter send me one envelope next time you write
