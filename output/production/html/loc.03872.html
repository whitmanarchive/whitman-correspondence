  328 Mickle St: Camden April 25 '89

—The spirit seems to move me to write you a line even if no account—Here I am yet the same cribb'd up in chair & room, & little or no prospect—yet sort o' cheery hearted & comfortable (it might be worse you know)—How are you all & getting along—George & Ed & Harry & Van & Deb?—Mont was here Sunday & very welcome—I hear often from Dr B[ucke]. Herbert is all right—

Walt Whitman
