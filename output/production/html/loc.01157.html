  328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey Feb. 17 '87

Every thing very much the same with me—quite completely disabled in my locomotion—O'Connor has gone to Southern California (to Dr Channing's) Eldridge has gone with him—(I fear O'C is in a bad plight)—J B Marvin is in Wash'ton 1121 I street out of office—Dr Bucke is well & writes often—I have been out two hours midday, to day, driving in the sun—

Walt Whitman
