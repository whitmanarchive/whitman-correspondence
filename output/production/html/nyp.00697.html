Camden Evn'g. Sept. 19 '89

Nothing new or notable—have not heard for certain yet whether you are in Boston safe. Suppose so tho'—& that you rec'd the photo. of Wm: I lent—also the "Liberty" piece sent—(I sent one to C W E[ldridge])—much the same as when I wrote—not at my worst but bad enough—I sit here writing & reading in moderation—unable to move—

Walt Whitman
