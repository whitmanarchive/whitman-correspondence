Camden— noon—July 11—

Yours just rec'd—the acc't & formal letter shift the relative positions—but taking in Judge R[ay]'s remarks which are a part of it, the result seems to me absolutely & unequivocally what I took it to be, & what I suppose the papers (with some marked errors of detail) are stating—Though I havn't seen the said papers (except the Phila "Press")—the weather here is so hot I dont​ don't​ go out or over to Phila:—

W W
