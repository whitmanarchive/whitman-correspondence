  Camden— Feb. 11 '87

Nothing very new or special with me—As I write toward latter part of afternoon the weather is warm & dark & wet here. I go out hardly at all the roads are so bad—(the winter will soon be beginning to break)—I havn't heard from you now in some time—Dr B writes often—I am to be taken over to the Contemporary Club" Phila. in a few evenings to talk to them—

Best love— W W
