Camden N J—U S America July 26 '88

I am still above board & shall probably make a sort of rally—This is the 6th or 7th whack thro' the last fourteen years of my war-paralysis & a pretty heavy one—tided over the others, tho' probably weaken'd after each—I am still imprison'd to room & bed, this the seventh week. I am finishing the little "Nov: Boughs"—

Walt Whitman
