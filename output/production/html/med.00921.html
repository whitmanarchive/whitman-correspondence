Camden N. J. U. S. America June 26 '91

If you are feeling well enough and in opportune mood let me introduce my good friend & physician Dr Bucke—He is Superintendent (medical and other) of the big Insane Canadian Asylum at London Ontario—is an Englishman born but raised (as we say it) in America. I still stick out here in the land of the living but pretty tough pulling most of the time—

Walt Whitman
