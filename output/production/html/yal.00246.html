The undersigned takes the liberty of offering you the accompanying MS for your "Miscellany;"—The price is $8.—

The undersigned would be glad to furnish you with an article for each number, if it would be agreeable to you.—

Please forward an answer to the address given herewith, stating whether you accept or decline.

Walter Whitman June 1st W.W. 12 Center St N.Y.       Walter Whitman Ans. July 9. '42 Editor "Boston Miscellany" [illegible]
