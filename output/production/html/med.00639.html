1309 Fifth av: near 86th st: New York May 24 noon My dear Mr Bloor

I have returned the two pamphlets—which I suppose you have rec'd. In a letter in the Tribune of to-day I have printed (as I some time since notified you) what you said—(well said)—about actors—I remain here till latter part of next week—then to Camden, New Jersey, which is my permanent p o address—Shall count on getting the extracts from your Journal about Mr Lincon's murder & funeral soon as you can conveniently send them.

Walt Whitman
