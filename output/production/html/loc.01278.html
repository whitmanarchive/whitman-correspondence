  OFFICE OF THE GALAXY, AN ILLUSTRATED MAGAZINE of Entertaining Reading. Published Monthly. No. 39 PARK ROW, New York, Aug 8 1867 My dear Sir:

I was very much gratified to receive your fine Harvest Carol this morning. It seems to me to rank with the very best of your poems, and that it   is sure to last.

I am sorry to say that it comes too late to be put as the second article & will need to be put in one of the later signatures. But wherever it is, it cannot be hidden.

You shall have the proof promptly.

  I am Very truly Yours F. P. Church. Walt Whitman   Aug 8 '67
