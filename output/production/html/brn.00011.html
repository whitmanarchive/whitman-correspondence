  328 Mickle street Camden New Jersey U S America Feb:​ 11 '86 My dear K C

I send you the two Volumes, same mail with this, (same address as this note)—The price is one pound two shillings, which please remit me in post office order—I am ab't​ as usual of late, bodily disabled, but in good heart.

Walt Whitman
