  Sat. morn 6 am Dear W.W.

I shall count it a distinguished favor to get the loan of that Bucke letter anent T. Tenn was the bright particular star of my youth & early manhood—is a man who makes this dull earth godlike, & immortality not at all strange. I will sacredly respect yr wish as I mention & will be extremely careful not even to mention it to any dangerous person whatever.

W.S. Kennedy   see notes Dec 19 1891
