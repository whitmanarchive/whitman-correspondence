  431 Stevens Street Camden New Jersey Evn'g May 12 '82 Dear Sir

Yours of yesterday rec'd—I could send the MS of Carlyle from an American Point of View by the 20th or 21st a week from now—It would make about nine or ten pages—

—What I said about its being "a candidate for the place of leading paper" &c—was meant to be left entirely to the editorial exigencies & judgment—no condition at all—

Walt Whitman
