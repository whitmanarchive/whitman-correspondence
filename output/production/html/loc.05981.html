  N.Y. Oct 30, 1891. Mr. Whitman. Dear Sir,

Admiring you so much, my cousin and I have ventured to write and ask whether you will be kind enough to send us your autograph.

Hoping you will not refuse to send us two autographs as we have separate collections,

I remain very sincerely [cut away] [illegible]H. L. (Ida Strauss)
