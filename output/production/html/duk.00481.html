  June 4—91 Studio 21 Pearl St.

Beloved Friend truly—our dependance it should seem at times. Your kind letter with 5 dollars duly recd also all satsifactory papers, reporting due honors now by your Genius—How rich mentaly, socialy—substantiated by sentiment at home, and from eminent authors abroad. Han deplores her inability to write to you, thanking you, just as she feels—hopes to be strong enough to do so—but her feelings overcome her. And myself, how I appreciate tangible relief. Han needs some danties, luxuries, the best I can puchase for her—Dont hear, or receive any assistance from Burlington—of course—neighbors enquire—

Charlie
