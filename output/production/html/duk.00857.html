Camden Sept 7, Evn'g

I return S[ymonds]'s letter—All I can say ab't it is I myself like to get views from every quarter—then I go on the tack that seems to me rightest—As I write, it is clouding up dark for a thunderstorm—I expect Dr Bucke to-morrow or next day—Morse and Gilchrist still here—

Walt Whitman
