  1030 am Sunday Dear Walt

The boy forgot to tell you I am sick in bed with a very much swelli[cut-away] throat & will not be able to get down today—Have sent for Doctor.

—Please put Judge Wescotts name in the book—with your own. He said he w'd regard it as a great favor [illegible] over

James

We had a Halcyon & voceberun time. A trifle too Halcyon for me—

  Scovel
