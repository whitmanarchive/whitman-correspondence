see notes Aug 26 & 28–1888   FIELDS, OSGOOD & CO. JAMES T. FIELDS. J. R. OSGOOD. JOHN S. CLARK. (SUCCESSORS TO TICKNOR & FIELDS,) PUBLISHERS, No. 124, Tremont Street, Boston, and 63, Bleecker Street, New York. Boston, December 14, 1868. Walt Whitman, Dear Sir:

In order that your poem shall go into the February number of the Atlantic, it is impossible to risk the time it would take, to get the proof to Washington and back. So I promise you the piece shall be read, word for word, and point for point, with your copy, which being in print, there is no chance for an error.

Yours always James T. Fields   To J. T. Fields Dec. 8. '68
