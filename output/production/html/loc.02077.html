  Walt Whitman Esq Dear Sir

Allow me to ask, could we secure your sevices to give a Reading part of an evening at Chickering Hall for the benefit of our Society & what would be the remunerative required?   If you have any preferences as to Month, please state it.

Respectfully yours Mary A. Fisher 71 Java St. Brooklyn E.D. Sept. 21—89.

(at Chickering Hall)
