  Camden New Jersey July 27 '90 Dear Sir

Y'rs of 25th rec'd & thank you. I remember you & y'r call & the ladies very well & send my best wishes & respects to all—I w'd be glad to send you four (or three) copies of the big book, (complete works)—as you say, they w'd to you be $4 each (retail 6)—I cannot add in MS any thing more than is already in each & all—I w'd take back & allow for the vol. you speak of—they w'd best be sent you by express, (as the postage is 40cts a copy)—If you take, remit me pay in p o order. Enclosed find circulars—

Respectfully &c:

Walt Whitman   Whitman letter | written to | Robert Adams | Fall River.
