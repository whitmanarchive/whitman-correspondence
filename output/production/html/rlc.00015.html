431 Stevens st  
 Camden, N. Jersey—U. S. America Aug 11

Yours of July 24 rec'd, also your previous one six weeks before—I wrote to you two months ago—also sent papers—I send paper, also my War Memoranda &c by same mail as this—same address (is it right?)—I remain much the same—bad spells enough—I congratulate you both—love to both—

W.W.
