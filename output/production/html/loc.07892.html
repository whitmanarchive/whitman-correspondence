Camden New Jersey—U S America Evn'g M'ch 3 '91

Snow storm & east wind the last twenty hours—have just eaten my dinner—supper, fine sweet oysters & toast & coffee—& have been leisurely writing for two hours—So you can see I do not give up—Yet may have to soon, as I am in a bad way (digestion power very languid or gone)—papers & mags: rec'd—thanks—send to Wallace—

Walt Whitman
