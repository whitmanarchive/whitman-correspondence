Camden N J—U S America Evn'g Oct: 12 '91

Nothing very different or notable—printed leaves f'm you rec'd—J W W[allace] still at Brooklyn, New York, (perhaps gone down to West Hills)—Expect him daily—also Andrew Rome—Cold & wintry here—I have oak-wood fire—have put on one of Mr Hodgkinson's presented undershirts—first rate—fit & all—Love to you all—

Walt Whitman
