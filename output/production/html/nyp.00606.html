Camden noon May 7 '88

I send you some papers to while away the time—Was out yesterday driving in the country—never did the spring impress me more—it was just the right temperature—the very sun & wind and grass with sort o' human relations—(what a beautiful object is a young wheat field! what color!)—Had a good supper, oysters & champagne, at my friends the Harneds in the evn'g—I only eat two meals a day—

W W
