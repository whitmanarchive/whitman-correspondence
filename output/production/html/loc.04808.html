  Camden N J — Dec​ : 7 '91 Dear Sister

Y'r​ postal card came & was welcomed—my bad spell continues—an etcher in N Y​ has etched the photo (with hat on leaning back, I call it "the laughing philosopher") & sent me—it is very fine—I always liked the pict:​ tho​ many friends did not—it is the best American etching I have seen (he sells it at $15)—am sitting here as usual—lots of sickness ab't​ here—oatmeal & an egg for b'kfast​ —small appetite—$2 enc'd​

W W
