  EDITORIAL·DEPARTMENT THE CENTURY·MAGAZINE UNION·SQUARE·NEW YORK May 14. 1890. My dear Whitman,

All the poems you have let me print before are of such a kind that I felt they would "widen your audience" so to speak. This one I fear appeals only to the comparatively few—& is not really adapted to our   use. Please send along something else.

That soldier one just fitted in!

Faithfully R.W. Gilder.
