  Camden NJ US America April 22 '89

Y'r welcome letter has come & Mary's word—my best love to all, not forgetting the dear little ones—

—Nothing very new with me—am still a prisoner here in Mickle Street 2d story, sitting at this moment in the big ratan chair—are you (father, mother, &c) domiciled still at 44?—yesterday here almost hot—

Walt Whitman
