Camden N J—U S America Feb: 19 p m '91

Palpable slight turn for the better with me (or I take it so)—Suppose you have rec'd my frequent notes & papers—convey this to J W W[allace]—my copy is to go to printers in three days for the 2d annex—fine sunny middling cool day—

God bless you all— Walt Whitman
