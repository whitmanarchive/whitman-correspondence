Camden N J  
 U S A July 22—'82

We are all still here & about the same—I am well as usual—your letters rec'd & dearly welcomed—I am busy printing "Specimen Days"—the Staffords well—best love—

W W

first Phil. ed'n out last Wednesday—all sold in twenty four hours—not one left—2d ed'n ready soon—
