Camden N J—U S America PM July 17 '91

Y'r cable of Dr B[ucke]'s arrival & being with you at B[olton] & well rec'd this mn'g—& thank you heartily & love to you & him & all—hot weather continued here—bad day with me—but shall try to get out an hour (not sure) tow'ds evn'g—as Traubel is coming with horse & wagon—am sitting here same place &c:—

Walt Whitman
