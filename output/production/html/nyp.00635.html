Camden Feb: 22 '89

Snow & dark weather here, & rather cold (but I have just got in a fourth cord of oak)—rec'd a letter from John Burroughs—he is at Po'keepsie at present temporarily with his wife & boy & rather blue in health & emotionality (& heart as I call it)—N[elly]'s card came—I am sitting here doing nothing—no Dr B[ucke] yet—he leaves Canada Monday next—I shall seize the first chance to send you the big (complete works) book—

Walt Whitman
