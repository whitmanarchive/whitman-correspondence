Camden— March 23—noon—

Yours rec'd​ —I am around, much the same—nothing very new, or different—the b[aby] doing well, grows well, hearty & bright—the rest well as usual—E[dward] recovered, & all right—my (new Edition) books out at last—(sent a set to N. M. J[ohnson] 506 12th st.)—comfortable to-day—write this in haste—

W.W.
