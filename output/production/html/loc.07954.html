Camden N J—U S America Nov: 15 '91

Congratulations on y'r safe arrival after the (I hope) satisfactory trip here, & look at us even but for a day or two face to face—All ab't as usual—Traubel here last evn'g—Sunny fine weather—aff: regards to Dr J[ohnston] & all the friends—

Walt Whitman
