Camden May 1 '89

Well the N Y show goes over to-day in a blaze of glory, & Barnum's great circus comes in here at noon & exhibits to night—(Ed goes—I am favorable)—& besides Dr Baker "graduates" this afternoon—you know he is my first nurse—last June—& Osler makes the address—indeed I believe two of 'em—things ab't same as before with me—

Walt Whitman
