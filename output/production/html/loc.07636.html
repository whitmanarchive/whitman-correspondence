Camden 9 P M March 19 '88

I was out yesterday to my friends the Harneds, & took a 3 or 4 mile drive afterward at sunset. The weather is pleasent—seems settled—I still write bits for the H[erald]—Haywood the Mass: free lover here to-day, very cordial &c. I treat him politely but that is all. I rec'd yrs: with L[ippincott]'s declination—Eakin[s] has taken away his picture & I havn't seen him for ten days

W W
