  HARPER & BROTHERS' EDITORIAL ROOMS. FRANKLIN SQUARE, NEW YORK. September 19th 89. Walt Whitman, Esqre, Dear Sir,

Enclosed please find Messrs Harper and Brothers'—check for Ten Dollars, in payment for the poem "Bravo! Paris Exposition," to be used in the "Weekly."

Yours Truly, John Foord Editor, "Harpers' Weekly."
