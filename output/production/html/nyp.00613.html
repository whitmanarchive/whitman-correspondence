Camden Sunday Evn'g Oct: 7 '88

Your welcome letter came—but I wish it had bro't me better news than that ab't the eyes—I still remain coop'd in my sick room—send you a copy of November Boughs same mail with this—Fine sunny Oct. weather now here—Am comfortable & send best love to you & dear N[elly]—

Walt Whitman
