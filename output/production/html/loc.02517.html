  Blackpool. England Sept 19. 91.

Came here yesterday Grand weather, magnificent sunset last night—such a display of gorgeous colour—golds, silver's crimson's, greens, blues, purples, blacks, greys, browns

Brilliant moonlight night

Another grand day this—write this sitting on beach—the rising tide breaking in curling waves at my feet—sun shining & shimmering on the heaving water. Air most exhilarating & everything bright & beautiful. My love to you & to HLT & the rest

God bless you yours affectly J Johnston
