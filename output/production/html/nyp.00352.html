 N.​ Jersey. Aug​ 21.

Your last, & paper, rec'd​ —have had a tolerably good week but nothing to brag of—middling comfortable to-day—very hot here to-day—tell your RR. chum if he sees me in Phil.​ he must make himself known to me.
