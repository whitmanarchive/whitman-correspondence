  Mar 9th 92 7 Taylor Ln— Bolton My Dear Walt Whitman

Just a few lines in response to your loving message to me, contained in Traubel's letter to Dr Johnston.

How very kind of you to send such tokens of your love to me, tokens which go deep into my.​ my​ heart, making life bright and cherful​ cheerful​ .

My little message will convey my gratitude, my deepest sympathy, and my very best love to you now— and always

your affectionatte​ affectionate​ Friend and Lover George Humphreys
