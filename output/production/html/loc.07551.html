Camden P M Jan: 7 '89

Every thing much the same—Y'rs of 4th rec'd—dark lowering weather—no Dr Walsh now four days (Is it a good sign?)—Any thing like strength is very slowly coming to me—but I fall back thankfully on being not such in a wretched way as three or four weeks ago—Ed W[ilkins] is well (but has disagreeable spells of heart burn)—

Walt Whitman
