Phila: July 19—

Rees Welsh & Co:'s new ed'n L of G. looks well—is a fac-simile of the Boston ed'n—announced ready by 17th (being pretty cautious printed only 1000) began to come in from the bindery late that day—Early this forenoon they were all clean'd out—hadn't a copy left—are hurrying up their second batch—I have commenced type-setting on Specimen Days—Keep well—awful hot weather—

W W
