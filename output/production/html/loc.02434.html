  Camden NJ Jan: 2 '91

Thank you dear girl for y'r kind welcome letter safely rec'd. Am here still holding the fort sort o' (as I call it)—has been four days of glum weather here but the sun is just shining out.—pretty fair spirits but bad grip & other bothers—Best love to yr dear father & Alma, & all, & God make the new year happy to you all

Walt Whitman
