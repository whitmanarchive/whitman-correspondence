  Medical Superintendent's Office. INSANE ASYLUM LONDON ONTARIO 2 Feb 1891

Your good long letter of 30 & 31 came to hand this morning. Also Springfield Republican piece enclosed—but what year is it do you know—?—it is 13 Nov. but no year given. I finished 3 days and nights in bed yesterday morning—bad cold and facial neuralgia—not sick—and now am at my office as lively as ever—I am one of the tough old sort—seems so and hope will turn out so. Glad to have your report: "if any thing easier" trust you will stick at that for a time. All well with meter—the first day, will be done tomorrow or next day—then for some business! When shall we see the "annex"? Some time in March? I hope so. So Ernest Rhys is married? I wish him luck! It is however a dubious step!

R M Bucke
