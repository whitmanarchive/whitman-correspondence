Camden Oct: 30 '87

To-day winter blowing & cold, but the sun shining & I feeling comfortable & hearty—took quite a drive yesterday afternoon & out to supper in the evening to my friends Mr and Mrs: Harned here—enjoy'd all—Have had letters from Dr B[ucke], Burroughs, H B Forman & Dr Knortz—the German—L of G. is out or ab't out, (at Zurich, Switz) Dr. K says—God bless you—

W W
