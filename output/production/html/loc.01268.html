  TRÜBNER & CO. AMERICAN, 
 EUROPEAN AND 
 ORIENTAL 
 LITERARY AGENCY. 57, & 59, Ludgate Hill, London E.C. Oct. 20th. 1888 TELEGRAPHIC ADDRESS, "TRÜBNER, LONDON." My dear Walt Whitman

I send you on behalf of Trübner & Co a draft for $14.43 for 39 copies of "Democratic Vistas" which is all your commission stock they had left on hand.

I was sorry to hear from our mutual friend Dr. Bucke that you are unable to get out in the open air and are confined to the house; it must be a great deprivation for you. I hear     now and again from Dr. Bucke & he frequently mentions you.

I remain my dear Walt Yours Sincerely Josiah Child
