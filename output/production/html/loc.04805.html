  Camden N J​ — noon Nov:​ 1 '91

Ab't​ same with me—tho'​ I hear the papers have made me seriously worse, but don't you get alarm'd​ —(the facts are bad enough)—rec'd​ a letter f'm​ Mary, Greenport—nothing new—it seems that George was down there, (probably a few hours) after all—my English friend leaves for home from N Y​ in the City of Berlin next Wednesday mn'g​ —am sitting here present in big chair alone as usual—winter here—dark & raw out—the people however well dress'd​ in crowds going home f'm​ church—

W W
