  TRÜBNER & CO AMERICAN, EUROPEAN AND ORIENTAL LITERARY AGENCY. 57, & 59, Ludgate Hill, London E.C. July 10th 1880 My dear Walt Whitman.
Enclosed I send you statement of a/c​ up to date from Trübner & Co, and draft on New York for eighty dollars fifty cents, the amount of sales up to date. When you get home will you kindly send T & Co as before per JB. Lippincott & Co. Philadelphia
  • 10 "Leaves of Grass"
  • &
  • 5 "Two Rivulets"
   T & Co as you will see have sold right out of "Leaves of Grass."

Please tell Dr. Bucke I have received his letter an​ and​ papers for which I am obliged I shall take the earliest opportunity of answering his letter, as soon as I can snatch a few leisure moments.

I hope you are in good health and enjoying your visit to the "Dominion". Is it, think you, likely  to become a great country in spite of its severe climate?

I have from time to time sent you various papers to Camden, those that may have reached after you left, you will probably get when you return.

I remain My dear Walt Whitman Yours Faithfully Josiah Child
