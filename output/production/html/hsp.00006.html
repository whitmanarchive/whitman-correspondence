  EDITORIAL · DEPARTMENT THE CENTURY · MAGAZINE UNION · SQUARE · NEW · YORK 
  Marion, Mass, 1st July 1887. My dear Whitman,

I am delighted that you liked Miss Phelps's story so well. We sent your card on to her—knowing she would prize it. The story has made a profound impression.

Sincerely R.W. Gilder

I wish we could put you in front of our big chimney.
