  EDITORIAL DEPARTMENT THE CENTURY MAGAZINE UNION SQUARE NEW YORK R. W. GILDER, EDITOR. R. U. JOHNSON, ASSOCIATE EDITOR. C. C. BUEL, ASSISTANT EDITOR. My dear Mr Whitman:

I am very glad you are pleased with the pictures. They all came back safely this afternoon except mine. If you will kindly look over those you have kept you will find one with my name on & a few words from the   etcher below. If so will you please express it to me.

I hope you will live to great many new years

Yours sincerely William Carey 8 December 1891
