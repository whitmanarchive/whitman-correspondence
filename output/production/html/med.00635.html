431 Stevens Street Camden, New Jersey Jan​ 14 '80

After I left you I went to Colorado, & there & in Missouri have spent the time since—came back here about a week ago—I sent you a little Volume—also two little pictures—You take the one you prefer & give the other to Linton—Send me word if they come safe. I am all right in health.

Walt Whitman
