Mickle Street Camden May 12 '90

No very mark'd change in the situation. Warry (my young man) has just been in to urge me to get out in wheel chair, wh' I shall—the sun is out, & the temperature mildens.

I send off a little piece to Century this evn'g. Have also written a little five line bit in compliment to Queen Victoria's birth day (May 24) wh' I shall send out—Alys Smith was here yesterday—it is quite possible Mary & her husband & children may come to America forthwith on a flying trip—Herbert Gilchrist has gone to Centreport (L I near Huntington) for a long stay—Shall look for you Thursday—or Friday at furthest—

Walt Whitman
