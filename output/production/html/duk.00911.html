Camden P M Dec: 29 '88

Y'rs rec'd ab't the books—many, best thanks—have rec'd letters from Mrs: F[airchild] and F B S[anborn] acknowledg'g them. Also from Baxter—am slowly improving probably—am sitting here alone by oak-fire as I write—Just now comes a letter from Harland receipting his book—Tell me in y'r next what was the express freight please—Dr B[ucke] writes most every day—O'C[onnor] is poorly but gritty.

Walt Whitman
