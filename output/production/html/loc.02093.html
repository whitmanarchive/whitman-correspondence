  Adel Grange. Leeds. England. 30 Dec: 1888. Dear Mr. Whitman

Edward Carpenter sent us on a letter he had received from you—in which you ask him for our address. Here it is at the top.

Thank you very much   for remembering Bessie & me. We never never forget you—nor the great great debt we owe you for all you have written. We shall like to have an "Autumn Boughs" very much—it is good of you to think of sending us one. I wish you were feeling better & stronger.

A friend of your's​ yours​ saw you   lately in America—& wrote to us about his visit to you.

Goodbye. Both Bessie & I send you our warmest greetings for the New Year & for all times.

Your friends,

Bessie & Isabella Ford     Bessie & Isabella Ford
