 U S America March 31, '76 My dear friend,

(Later than my letter dispatched last evening I wish merely to say) make cheques, orders or drafts payable by Brown brothers, bankers, Philadelphia, U. S. A., corner Chestnut and Strawberry streets, on my endorsement.

Walt Whitman

I am feeling quite comfortable to-day—I wish this information—the address above—conveyed to Robert Buchanan—
