  Camden— April 7 '91

Keeping on fairly I guess—Dr L here yesterday—take medicine every day—bowels mderately loose—inertia & headache quite heavy—appetite so-so (no nausea)—sleep middling—the proofs (prose) have pass'd page 51—D McKay has just paid me $127.87—Warry has gone over to Phila: with last proofs—

W W
