  Philadelphia, PA. Aug 15 1888 Dear Mr. Whitman:—

Wont you read over carefully Amelia Rives' poem in today's Herald and give me an expert's opinion on it for publication in the Herald? I will call for this tomorrow, for I am sure you will have something instructive to say about the poem.

Very truly yours, C.H. Browning
