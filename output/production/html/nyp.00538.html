Camden New Jersey October 1 18841

Received from Critic Fifteen Dollars for my piece "What Lurks" &c—

Walt Whitman

The slips & papers came safely—thanks—

Correspondent:
Joseph Benson Gilder (1858–1936) was, with his sister Jeannette Leonard Gilder (1849–1916), co-editor of The Critic, a literary magazine.
