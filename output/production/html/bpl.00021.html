  Camden, New Jersey Aug: 13 '91

Let me send my little word too to J R Lowell's memory. His was the true American's and Humanity's heart in the light of his own convictions; and he wrought it out faithfully. His written pages preserve a certain altitude everywhere. As Emerson says, we are at any rate all beholden to kings and eminencies for their grand standard of atmosphere & manners, or suggestion of them.

Walt Whitman
