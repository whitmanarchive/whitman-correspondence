Camden New Jersey June 3—P M

Yours of June 1 rec'd this afternoon—Thanks—best & joyfulest thanks to you & Baxter & all—I will write to you to-morrow, (or next day,) after thinking it over a bit, & tell you detailedly—at present I have not settled on spot—but am fill'd with gratitude & pleasure at the prospect of having a country or perhaps sea shore shanty of my own—

Walt Whitman
