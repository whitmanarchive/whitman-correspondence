  328 Mickle Street Camden New Jersey U S. America Sept: 10 '86—

The summer is drawing to a close, but we are having a warm spell here—(now the fourth day of it)

—I am ab't as usual in health—my locomotion however giving out pretty fast—I shall be looking for Logan before long—Love to you, Alys, & all—

Walt Whitman
