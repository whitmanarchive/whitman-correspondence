  Camden Wednesday Evn'g: Aug: 22 '88

Still keeping in my sick room. Eva was here, with the little girl. Welcome. Harry is pretty well now. Herbert leaves London to-day in the ship "British Princess" for Philadelphia. My brother Eddy that was at Moorestown is now at Blackwoodtown. Is well. I have just eat a hearty supper if that's any sign—

W W
