Camden noon [Sept:] 2 '90

Y'r card rec'd ab't piece—don't know of Williams having any mark'd Welsh blood—never heard ab't that—one of the stock names on the womens' (Williams') side was Kossabone (doubtless Causabone) (Jenny Kossabone my g't grandmother mother's side)—Yes, keep it awhile, no hurry at all—If you feel to do so, send MS to me to see if points right—but do as you have a mind to—no hurry ab't piece—

W W
