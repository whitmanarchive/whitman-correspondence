  OFFICE OF THE NEW YORK CITIZEN, 32 BEEKMAN STREET, N. Y. Terms of the Paper, $5.00 PER YEAR. 10 CENTS PER COPY. August 9 1867 Friend Whitman,

I publish in tomorrow's Citizen, Rosetti's​ Rossetti's​ article from the Chronicle. I congratulate you on the article. It will wake people up.

I wish you could send me a copy of your book—a thing which I don't possess. I will mail you a copy of tomorrow's paper.

Very respectfully yours W. L. Alden (Associate Editor)     Mr. Alden. N.Y. Citizen Aug. 9 '67. ans. see notes Dec 7 1888
