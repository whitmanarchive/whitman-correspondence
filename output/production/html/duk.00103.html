Cumberland Ctr. Feb. 22—'91. Dear Sir,

I have always felt that you were a relative of mine, and on the strength of that, am going to ask for your autograph. My mother was a Whitman of Bangor, has relations in Mass. and N.J. by that name, and the late Judge Whitman of Portland was also a relative. Do you belong to that family of Whitmans.

Yrs very truly, Mrs J.S. Harris. Cumberland Centre, ME
