  ? | May 1870 march 23 My dear walt

i have just got your letter with the money in it i received the within letter last monday was glad to hear from matty i was very much worried for fear the storm had overtaken her and she had been detained but it seems she got home all safe i will write a few lines and send that part of the letter about the masons they could have just as well have accommadated her as not for a few days but i gess its all for   the best she dident go) i wasent favorable to her going at all but she had such a wish to go i dident say so much against it) George came home last saturday very unexpeted he came to see about getting the inspecting of some pipe for the gas company but they dident make an agreement so he went back on monday he is well looks most too fat and pussy but seems to be well) you must write the particulars about the new cloths walt) i have had mrs Brown and willie s wife here this afternoon) i aint quite so lame to day

O walt can you send the week for i have got up to the 17th

i am reading the story good bie
