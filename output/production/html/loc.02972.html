  March 23d. 1889. Dear Walt,—

I hoped I should be able to send you good news to-day; but instead I have to tell you that William has had two epileptic attacks; one at 8.30 this A. M. & one at 10.30 Now, at 3 P.M., he is asleep & I hope may wake up better. It is very cruel to have him suffer so.

With love— N. O'C.
