  Camden Feb: 4 '91

Cold & sunny here—Lou was in this mn'g​ —all are well as usual—Jess is getting along at St​ St.​ Louis—nothing very new or different—Ed is the same at Blackwood—Have not heard f'm​ Mary lately— A pretty bad fortnight with me the last, but might be worse— am sitting here as usual as I write this—Dr​ Dr. ​ Bucke is quite sick abed—Love to you, sister dear—$2 enclosed—Sun out shining beautifully—the 1 o'c whistle just blown

Walt Whitman
