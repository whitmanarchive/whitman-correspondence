Camden, New Jersey, U. S. A. Oct​ 2nd.

I merely write to say at once that your letter and the postal order have both been safely received. The books (to the addresses given) will be sent immediately. I am well for me. H[erbert] G[ilchrist] is at John B[urroughs]'s on the Hudson. Mrs G[ilchrist] is ill in bed. Harry is well.

Thanks and love. W.W.
