Camden noon Nov: 19 '88 Dear Lou

I continue getting along pretty well considering—Eat my rations & sleep fairly—(the Graham biscuits taste good—have them most every meal—I only take two meals daily)—the publisher got an order for 250 copies Nov: Boughs yesterday from Scotland—the enclosed (I send to George) was forwarded to me by Capt. Wright—(I don't know what he wants George's address for—have not written myself)—Coolish, dark, rainy here to-day—I am sitting here yet by the stove in my sick room. The big book (all my writings collected complete) will be done in ab't a fortnight—I shall send you one—

Walt Whitman
