Sent to Rolleston, Ireland, Sept. 20 '84.

Write now to acknowledge yours from Ireland just rec'd.—to send you congratulations on return home with wife and children—and prayers for good future to you all—will write in time, what I have to say about the new phase of the trans[lation] enterprise. I am about as usual in health—one point worse—thre[atened] with total and permanent loss of walking power—
