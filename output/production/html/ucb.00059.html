  St. Louis To the Editor

Let me give you some flying impromptu notes confessedly all too meager (a hiatus every where) of my journey starting three months since from the Atlantic Coast and so railroading through New Jersey, Penn, West Virginia, Ohio, Indiana, Illinois, Wisconsin over Kansas and the Great Plains into the very height & heart Rocky Mountains with the canons, parks and peaks. Them I reserve for a special letter. Returning to Denver for a few days, then by the southern road to Pueblo, over the Plains, to Kansas City, stopping there a while. *Then through Missouri again to St Louis, where I have been spending a month.
