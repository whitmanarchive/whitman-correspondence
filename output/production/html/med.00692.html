All going on well with me—Your good letter rec'd—The type-setting of "Specimen Days" will be all finished the coming week & the book out ten days afterward—same sized vol: same sort of type, binding, general appearance &c. with L of G—same price—As I write (Sunday afternoon) up in my 3d story room, heavy clouds, the rain falling in torrents—
