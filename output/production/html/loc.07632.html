  Nov. 10. 13 Emser Strasse Wiesbaden Germany. My dear Walt

The enclosed will interest you. From all accounts the reception of book here is very satisfactory.

I hope your 71st year finds you cheerful——I can hardly hope well, for I have heard of late illnesses with great grief. We are here for my wifes health, which I am glad to say is much improved.

Ever yours T. W. Rolleston.
