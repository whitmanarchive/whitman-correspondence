431 Stevens Street  
 Camden New Jersey March 31 '81​ Dear Sir—

In compliance with your request in letter of 28th I this day send you by mail to same address as this card my two Volumes Leaves of Grass and Two Rivulets (Memoranda of the war embodied in latter Vol:​ ) The price is $10, which please send me here by p.o. order.

Walt Whitman
