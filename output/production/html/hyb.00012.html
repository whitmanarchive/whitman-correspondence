431 Stevens Street  
 Camden New Jersey March 31 '81​ Dear Sir1—

In compliance with your request in letter of 28th2 I this day send you by mail to same address as this card my two Volumes Leaves of Grass and Two Rivulets (Memoranda of the war embodied in latter Vol:​ ) The price is $10, which please send me here by p.o. order.

Walt Whitman  cor.00004.001_large.jpg  cor.00004.002_large.jpg

Notes

  • 1. Whitman recorded an order of $10 filled on March 30 for "G Wm Harris, Ass't Lib: Cornell University"; this is the only order on that approximate date (Daybooks and Notebooks, ed. William White [New York: New York University Press, 1978], 1:235). [back]
  • 2. This letter is now lost. [back]
