  Direct to George at Camden Iron Works, Camden N.J. Oct 5 my dear walt

i send you the receipt for the money george has sent you by addams s express 100 dolr he wants you to write to him at the above direction when you receive it all right to day is the 4th i want to go down to the post office to morrow and see if alls right with the order george is here but will go back to morrow our company is here yet and i dont know how long they will remain i have to work but i suppose i shall get through i will write again walter dear i got your letter satterday with the money it come very acceptable

good bie   from dear mother
