  Camden Evn'g Dec: 25 '88

Thanks dear Kitty, dear friend, dear girl, for the beautiful photo—it is indeed beautiful—give my best love to your father and mother & sisters & Al & wife & Harold & Calder—

I have had a bad sickness seven months but am now some better—have sat up nearly all day, & eaten some dinner.

Happy New Year to you & all—

Walt Whitman
