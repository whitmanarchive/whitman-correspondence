Department of the Interior, 
  Washington, January 12, 1865. Sir:

Upon reporting at this Department and passing a satisfactory examination you will be appointed to a First Class Clerkship at a compensation of twelve hundred dollars per annum. I am, Sir, very respectfully, your obt servant

W. T. Otto, Assistant Secretary.

Walt Whitman, Esq. 
  Brooklyn, N. Y.
