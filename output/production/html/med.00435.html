All goes on about the same —still unwell, but up—yours of March 5 rec'd and welcomed—O how I should like to see you, every day, dear Mitch—my own dear boy and comrade of the war—the hospital—I have to sit here alone much of the time, and think of those old times—very cold here, yet—this is the 21st April, and the ground is frozen here—I keep cheerful spirits, and still hope to get around—Love to you, and to the wife and little girl—Write soon—Address, Camden, N. Jersey.
