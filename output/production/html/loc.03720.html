  Camden NJ 12th 6th 1880. Walt Whitman My Dear "Old Boy"

I was so full, Sunday, of the "ample ether, and the divine air" of your presence—and the aurum potabile which always surrounds our symposiums     that I forgot to say that I expended $9.50 in pursuit of the recalcitrant, pirate—Worthington, in New York City.

Please send me this amount by Bearer, & oblige Your Devoted but "impecunious" friend

J M Scovel

PS. If you want me further, you know how to "whistle" & "I will come to you my lad.
