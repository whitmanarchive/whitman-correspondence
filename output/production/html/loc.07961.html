Camden N J—U S America Aug: 6 '91

Good welcome letters rec'd to-day f'm all of you, (yourself, J W W[allace] & Dr B[ucke])—thanks for picts of Dr B, seem good—have just written a word to B.—Nothing very new—some favorable features (& g't easements & reliefs they are)—but a glum week the past & present—have however eaten a fair b'kfast & am sitting here in big chair by window—Mrs: D[avis] has gone for the day 4 miles to see some friends—Warry is here—all well—

Walt Whitman
