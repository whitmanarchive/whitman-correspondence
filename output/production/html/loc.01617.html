  Furze Down. Streatham Surrey. SW. July. 16. 71. Dear Mr. Whitman.

Tennyson writes to you this mail he lays upon me the blame of not having written to you sooner, & I am willing to bear it the fact is the books went to his London address & were not forwarded.

Yours affectny​ , Cyril Flower     from Cyril Flower 1871 see notes 22 Sept 23 1888
