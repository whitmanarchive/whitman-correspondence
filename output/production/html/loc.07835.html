Camden NJ US America Sept: 8 '90

The photos have come safely, & we all like them well—Warry has his—he is well. Is it practicable to fac-similize your plate (of course at our expense here) of my picture (in 1890) to send here for my forthcoming little (2d) annex? We all like it much, & I sh'd like to have one to keep here to print from. Am well as usual & send love & thanks. Warry & Mrs: D[avis] same—

Walt Whitman
