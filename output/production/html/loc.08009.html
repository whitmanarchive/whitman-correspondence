Camden N J—America Feb: 23 '91

Still badly off, but a shade easier if anything this forenoon—the Art Magazine has come—it is a fine number—thank you—the first batch of copy for "Good-Bye" has gone to the printer—but tho' a little matter sh'l not have proof of it for ten days—the next (March) number of N[orth] A[merican] Review is adv'd to have an essay (very scrappy it will be) by me—

W W
