431 Stevens Street Camden New Jersey Dec. 15 '83 Dear Sir

In compliance with your letter of 12th I send a piece herewith—It is indispensable that I shall see the proof—if convenient set it up & send me proof any time during next week. I reserve the right of publishing (including) the piece in future book—I shall want 30 papers.

Very respectfully &c Walt Whitman
