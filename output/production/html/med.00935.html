November 5, 1890 Dear Sir,

above find receipt for the money—& thanks to you. Give my respects to Miss Wixon—I am sometimes very ill for days & cannot read, write, or talk or be talked to—& on such occasions answer no letters. . . . am sitting here in my den in great old ratan chair (with big wolf skin spread over back) sunny but pretty cold—have a good oak wood fire.

Walt Whitman
