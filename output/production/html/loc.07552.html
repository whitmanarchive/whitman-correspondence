Camden noon Nov: 27 '88

We have had (glum & dark yet) a severe storm & blow & destruction hereabout but I believe I am as serene as ever & getting on comfortably—had a present of some plump sweet partridges—& half one for my breakfast—went well—I suppose you rec'd the Critic and American sent last night—I believe the books, printing &c. are going to eventuate satisfactorily from my point of view & plans—no cyclone of success—but no special mishap—wh' is a great victory considering my wreck'd bodily condition—

Walt Whitman
