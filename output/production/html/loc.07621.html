Camden May 9 pm '89

No word to-day from O'C[onnor] leaving me to gloomy apprehensions—Sunny & warm here & I am expecting an out-door chair to get forth in, under Ed's convoy. If the weather keeps good & no hitch in my condition, I shouldn't wonder if I got out that way, before you receive this—

Walt Whitman
