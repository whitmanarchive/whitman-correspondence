  Camden Sunday P M April 20 '90

Fine sunny day—expect to get out a little in wheel chair—was out yesterday—feeling dull & leaden four or five days—nothing very new—some oysters for my breakfast—drink a little sweet champagne—y'rs rec'd, thanks—sit here as usual in big arm chair with the wolf-skin spread on back—generally get down stairs in the little room an hour after supper—

God bless you all— Walt Whitman
