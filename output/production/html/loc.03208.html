  Camden New Jersey U S America Feb: 9 1890 Dear Sir

I sent you a little cluster of poemets "Old Age's Echoes" ab't 1st of Nov: last—wh' I suppose you have rec'd—No word rec'd me ab't them—If you can't use them please kindly return the slip copy (it was printed on a slip) to me here,

respectfully &c: Walt Whitman   Walt Whitman—9 Feb 1890 | as to his verses on | 'Old Age Echoes'?
