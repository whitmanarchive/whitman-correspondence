Camden Sunday Evn'g

Yours of yesterday rec'd—I had heard nothing of the Boston P. M. action—(it is not some mistake?—As you describe it?)—The price of the book such a volume as I sent you is $3—I sent one to Prof. Loomis—The heat here to-day has been awful, but I seem to stand it well—

W W

I decidedly approve your non-answer to Sigma—
