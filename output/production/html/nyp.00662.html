Camden Evn'g March 17 '89

Pleasant visit f'm Mrs: Spaulding of Boston, friend of L of G. & of me—I rather think Dr [Bucke]'s meter business will be practically started, & will be a success—Horace Traubel will be Secretary.

Things with me abt same—I sit here in my big chair alone most of the time, as ever, same old monotonous story—yet I keep a good front I hope—

Walt Whitman
