  Camden early P M 24th July 
 '89

Nothing special or new—Every thing going on the same & fairly —think quite a good deal of O'C[onnor] lately—breakfasted on an egg & some blackberries—have sent the little "Donnelly" book to John Burroughs (at his request) at Hobart N Y. Have just sold to Chicago purchaser one of the big book (three the last week, but that is exceptional)—

Walt Whitman
