Camden NJ—US America June 30 '91

Y'rs rec'd today—Ab't same continued with me—head ache—Dr Bucke leaves N Y 8th July on Britannic—is well—Of the picts: sent to Dr Johnston when duplicated you are to have one each—the fuller birth–day acc't is to be in Lippincott's for August—warm weather keeps but I stand it fairly—good nights help me.

Walt Whitman
