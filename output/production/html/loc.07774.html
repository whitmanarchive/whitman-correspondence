  Camden April 14 PM '90

Feeling fairly—had an easy night—Superb weather sunny & warm—Am going out presently in wheel chair—ate four raw oysters for my breakfast—Expect to give the Lincoln Death piece to-morrow evn'g Phila: (Shall probably skip my daily letter to you to-morrow)—A little headache as I write.

God bless you all— Walt Whitman
