431 Stevens Street  
 Camden Nov​ 10 '82​

Your valued letter rec'd​ , it is like a kindly living talk and hand clasp—I shall forward it to Burroughs—So good to hear you & yours active, full of receptivity and well—I have lately had a bad spell, prostration &c again, but am now well as usual. Dont forget the Academy if it appears—

Walt Whitman.
