  Bolton England Oct 31/91

Just a line to send you a greeting from the Bolton "chaps" & a word of cheer & comfort. Hope things are "good" with you

I send you a copy of Nature notes with little article on your old friend John Burroughs

Fine sunny weather here all this week.

Presume JWW is now "all at sea"

Love to all yours affectly J. Johnston
