Camden N J, U S America Nov: 18 '90—

Nothing particular to write but tho't I w'd send a word. Still here in same spot (holding out the fort sort o')—Still grip & bladder trouble on me badly—writing a little—I believe I sent you Ingersoll's lecture—A visitor to-day f'm England Mr Aidé (with Stanley)—I am sitting here by oak wood fire—

Walt Whitman
