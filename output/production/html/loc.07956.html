Camden NJ—US America Nov:22 '91

Much the same—Y'r P C rec'd yesterday—Dr B[ucke] well & hard at work—H T[raubel] well—Mrs: D[avis] has had a bad spell but easier to-day—Warry well—I am in depress'd condition—a "phrenologist" here to day once before I was up—I declined—

Aff: regards to you, J W W[allace] & all— Walt Whitman
