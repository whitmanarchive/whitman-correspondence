Washington, D.C.,10 August 1867. W. L. Alden. My dear Sir:

Your note has been received.—Accept—for yourself, the Citizen, & Gen. Halpine—My sincere thanks for your kindness. I fully appreciate it.

As I have not at my control, at this moment, any bound copies of Leaves of Grass, would you allow me to send you a copy in paper I forward it by same mail with this.

I send best remembrances to the General.

Walt Whitman
