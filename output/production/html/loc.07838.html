Camden PM Sept: 20 '90

Best remembrance & love to all—I am getting along better than you w'd suppose (have this tenacious grip though)—was out last Sunday—was propell'd in the wheel chair—to my Camden friends Mr. and Mrs. T B Harned who treated me to a splendid meal of oysters & champagne—to wh' abstemious me did fullest justice (I think the best ch: I ever drank)—I am writing a little annex (the 2d) to L of G. & giving out the design of my burial vault (on a little wooded slope in Harleigh Cemetery here)—

the Lord bless you all— Walt Whitman

Give this to Alma & the girls—
