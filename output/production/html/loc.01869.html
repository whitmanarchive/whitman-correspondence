  June 1st 1871 Dear Fathe​ Father​

It a long time cince​ since​ I heard from you and thinking you would like to hear from me thought I wright​ write​ you a line or two to see how you are geting​ getting​ along I am as well as can be I should like to hear from you very much I have got a good place and are doing well I like it very much I wilull​ will​ send you a card 480 Bway I supose​ suppose​ you will   come on here soon to spend one or two months and then I shall see you It is very warm here yesterday we had a very hard thunder storm and it done a great deal of dammage​ damage​ along the North River & distroye​ destroy​ Houses and trees and fruit things is very quite​ quiet​ here except a murder now and then so now I will close with my love

good by from you affecinate​ affectionate​ Son and Friend John M Rogers

To my Dear Father

write Soon

  John Rogers ans. June 2 '71.
