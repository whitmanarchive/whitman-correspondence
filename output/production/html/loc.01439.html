  books sent July 1 143 King Henry's Road South Hampstead London. England. June 20. 1890. Dear Sir

Will you kindly send me your large 6 dollar edition called "Complete Poems and Prose"; also "Leaves of Grass. Including Sands at Seventy and Backward glances. Autograph & 6 portraits. Small edition bound in pocket book style. 5 dollars. 1 copy of each. I enclose an order for £2 8".   I have seen these editions at Mr H Buxton Forman's.

Allow me to thank you warmly and gratefully for the great joy and profit your works have brought me.

I am, dear sir

Yours very truly Louisa Drewry (address Miss Drewry.) To Walt Whitman Esq  

P.S.) I shall be glad to have the books as soon as convenient to you.
