Harper & Brothers' Editorial Rooms. New York, Oct. 22, 1884. Dear Mr. Whitman:

Please find enclosed Messrs Harper & Brothers' cheque for thirty dollars in payment for your poem Of That Blithe Throat of Thine. And you have the Messrs Harpers' permission to use the poem in a collection of your poems six months (or later) after our publication.

With thanks, sincerely yours, H. M. Alden. Editor of Harpers' Magazine.
