  48, Gower Street, Bedford Square, W.C. London Saturday 23 Sept. 1876 Dear Walt Whitman

I have received your volume and thank you heartily My Father, who had the pleasure of meeting you in America—which I had not, though I have lived in & love the land—and to whom you kindly gave a copy of your 'Leaves of Grass' sends his warm regards & desires to be remembered.

One of the humblest of your admirers Justin H. McCarthy
