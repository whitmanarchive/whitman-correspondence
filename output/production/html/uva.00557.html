328 Mickle St—Camden New Jersey U S America May 3 '87

Yours of Ap: 20 just rec'd & welcomed. Write oftener—The Staffords & I remember you with greatest affection & esteem—I also with deepest gratitude—I am still here in the same little old house—of course gradually sinking & dissolving—Harry S[tafford] had a surgical operation on his throat—it seems to have been properly done, & the cut is healing—He is at Marlton, New Jersey, married, has a child—I send you some papers—always best love—

Walt Whitman
