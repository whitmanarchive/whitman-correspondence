Camden Evn'g March 18 '89

Sunny mild March day—Dr B[ucke] leaves at 8—will be due home in London ab't noon to-morrow—The practical outset of the meter enterprise collapsed at the last moment for the want of capital investors—

N[elly]'s card came this mn'g—I am easier to-day—Hope the cold in the head is "petering out"—

W W
