Camden New Jersey U S America Sept: 30 '90

Y'rs rec'd—thanks—I send the little p'k't-b'k of L of G. (paid) by same mail with this—coolish weather has set in, but very fine, sunny to-day—John Burroughs has been visiting me—all well—Salutations to you & Dr J[ohnston] & the friends all—

Walt Whitman
