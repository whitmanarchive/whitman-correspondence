  "Black & White" 33, Bouverie Street, London, E.C. 16th March 1891. Sir/.

Allow me to thank you for the "Conservator" and the photograph you have been good enough to send us for a reproduction of your portrait in the paper for the pleasure of our English readers.

Hoping that you are enjoying fair health.

I have the honor to be, Sir,

Faithfully yours M.H. Spielmann Art Editor Walt Whitman Esq. Poet Boston U.S.A.     see | notes | Aug 14 | 1891
