Camden June 18 '91

Nothing very new or different. Lippincotts will have a fuller more detailed report of the proceedingses of the birth day supper—Horace T. is back on his old routine—just rec'd word f'm Dr B[ucke] Canada—all right—cool & east wind to-day after a horrible hot spell (sudden change)—am sitting here by window as usual—

Walt Whitman
