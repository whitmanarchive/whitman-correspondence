  73 Henry Street Brooklyn NY 18 March 1892 Dear Mr Whitman,

With this I mail to you a copy of a collection of verses entitled "Loves Seasons or Tides of the Heart,"—which I hope you are well enough to find some pleasure in reading.

But the real poetry you will find I think in "Note 4" in   the Appendix, to which I invite your attention.—

Notes 3 & 4 present for the consideration of thoughtful minds a new view of man; & the Fowler & Wells Co:—are so much impressed by it that they are about to send these notes out as a leaflet to the men of thought at present engaged in studies of this nature.

I shall be glad to hear from you & to know that your health is quite restored.—I remain

Very Sincerely Yours Thaddeus Hyatt
