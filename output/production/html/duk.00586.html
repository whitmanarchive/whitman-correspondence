  '69 ? December 7 My dear Walt

i got your letter yesterday morning it come real quick i was quite astonished to see it was wrote sunday afternoon George has been home and left yesterday in a tremendous snow storm he went to new york to send you 200 dollrs by express i suppos walter you have got it all right he sent some to Jeff also) well walt we have stood it through the storm it was a very bad storm indeed

i am about the same as usual feel pretty well your aunt becca is better old mrs man   is back from mobeal walt the clapp i wrote about is the one hellen price spoke to you about being to the falanks Jersey)

poor mr Beecher gets it right and left

walt when you write again tell all you gain about the new attorney Ge George dont think he will be to camden all winter as he thinks the brooklyn board will stop having pipe made if he staid for the new york board he would lose his place on the brooklyn)

good bie walter dear i looked for mr Oconor monday and tuesday  

from dear mother
