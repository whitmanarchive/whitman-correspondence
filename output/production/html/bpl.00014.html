PS. I have a shrewd guess that you intend to sell or rent yr present house, & build or buy the new one on the Long Island shore. But I shan't impart my surmise to any one else. How happy I shall be to come & see you when you are in the new house! Egad, think of that, man,—the new house, I mean. Mrs F. has just sent on some plans for a building. They don't seem to know that you are a house builder.
