  The Truth Seeker Company, Booksellers And Publishers, 28 Lafayette Place. New York, Jan 31 1891 book sent by mail Feb:3 Dear Sir

The Ingersoll–Field Discussion is out of print in paper. We have copies in cloth—at 100

Will you please send to Wm. J. Nicolay, Minier, Ill. 1 Complete Works of Walt Whitman 1 vol. Octavo, hf. cloth—

McKay says this book is owned by Mr Whitman & that he can only give us 20% on it. Hope you can do better—thro   Mr Whitman, as this book is to be sold again, and we will have to give Mr Nickolay a discount. Please send to him the cheapest way, & send us bill.

Yours Truth Seeker Co B.
