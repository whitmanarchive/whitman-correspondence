Editorial Office of the Galaxy, 40 Park Row, New York. Wednesday noon  
 12th 1 John Swinton

Will call at 12, noon, to-morrow, 13th, to see you—

Will also call at 6 this afternoon—

At Galaxy office—

Walt Whitman

Notes

  • 1. The date of this note is conjectural, but "O Star of France" appeared in the June 1871 issue of the Galaxy, and Whitman was in New York in July 1871. [back]
