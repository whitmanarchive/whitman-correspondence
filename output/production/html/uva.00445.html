431 Stevens Street  
 Camden New Jersey Nov: 15 '82

The return'd Scribner, R Schmidt's book, the Danish papers &c. received. I think neither John Burroughs's Notes or O'Connor's pamphlet is now for sale. I send you (same mail with this) Specimen Days which please keep as a little present from me. (See pp. 316, '17 Specimen Days.) Dr R M Bucke, of London, Ontario, Canada, is preparing a book about me, in which he is going to reprint O'Connor's pamphlet—

Walt Whitman
