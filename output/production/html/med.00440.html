431 Stevens st.  
 cor West.  
 Camden,  
 N. Jersey. Dec. 20, '75 Librarian Congress: 
 Dear Sir:

Please furnish me with Copyright for "Two Rivulets," as per title enclosed. Is the $1 the right sum?

Yours truly Walt Whitman

If perfectly convenient—& you have a printed slip with Copyright laws, (latest) please enclose with document.
