  Camden N J— Dec​ : 15 '91

Merry Christmas to you sister dear—tho't​ I w'd​ send you the 5 in one as now & 5 in the next (10 altogether) instead of the P O​ order—

—Mary is well enough to go around but has rheumatism pretty bad—Ansel is pretty well—works regularly in the ship yard—their two daughters & families are well—Lou has been here twice—Did the box she sent come?

God bless you sister dear—

W W
