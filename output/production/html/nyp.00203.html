ATTORNEY GENERAL'S OFFICE, Washington, March 23 1866 Dearest Mother,

I ordered the draft to be paid to you, of course. All you have to do is to go down & present it—they may ask you who you expected money from, & where.

I dont know, but I expect Jeff or Mat would do just as well.

Walt.

Mr. Lincoln, the post master, knows about the letters & money being stolen—the Post office here has conferred with him on the subject.
