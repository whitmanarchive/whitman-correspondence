  155 So 4th St Phila Jan'y 7th 1892 Mr Whitman Dear Sir

Enclosed please find a sea weed from the Pacific Coast. In gathering them I was often reminded of the title of your book "Leaves of Grass" and think it might please you for a passing moment I send it with the hope you may pass a comfortable day.

Yours Respectfully Harry E. Boutelle To Mr Walt Whitman Camden N.J.
