Feb: 18—Evn'g—

Yours of 15th rec'd​ , with the $10 for Centennial Edition—2 vols​ —Sincere thanks—As I am absent from home for a week or so—& shall not be able to send them till I get back (I will see to it immediately then)—thought I would send you this word right away—

Walt Whitman
