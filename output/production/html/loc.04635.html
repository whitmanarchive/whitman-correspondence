  Sag Harbour L.Island Dec 24th 89 Dear Sir

Your volume of poems ordered by Miss Stoddart arrived safely—it was really intended for her Uncle Howard Helmick an Artist—who has been residing abroad many years—but who even there learned to know and love the poet and prohet​ prophet​ of his Native land—We will be able now to enjoy and appreciate it in full—as   we were able only to get a small selection in London—

Wishing you heartily the compliments of the season

I am Yours Sincerely E.C. Waters—     E. C. Waters
