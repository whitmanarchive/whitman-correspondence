  Boston December 1st, 1860 Dear Walt,

Things look immensely dubious today. Can't tell you anything encouraging at present. We are working hard. Do not use the check yet.—Will write you again Monday or Tuesday.

Hoping for the best good of us all we remain your friends & brothers T&E—

P.S.—My wife sends her warm regards to you. She desires much to see you. W.W.T.
