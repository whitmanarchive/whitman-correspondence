328 Mickle Street Camden N J p m Feb: 14 '89

Y'r card came yesterday—Rolleston has rec'd in Ireland my big Vol. (complete works)—I send one to you today by express—it includes Nov. Boughs & all—send me card when it comes safe.

I hear from Dr Bucke often, he expects to come here next week—my friend O'Connor is very ill at Washington—

I am imprison'd in the sick room now in the ninth month, entirely disabled in movem't—pretty good heart tho'—

Walt Whitman
