1890 Camden Sept: 16 early PM

Y'r two impromptu cards rec'd—thank[s]—you will doubtless get the pretty little Phila: magazine "Poet–Lore" with this with a word of mine in it on that huge jungle question the Shakspere one—I keep pretty well—was out Sunday to a champagne & oyster supper to Mr & Mrs. Harned's (both good to me)—all right yesterday & to–day with me—rainy weather here (broken)—another letter f'm Symonds (I think there's something first class in him)—One of my two boys 26 yrs old was married last evn'g—he came yesterday to talk ab't it & hung on my neck & kiss'd me twenty times—

Walt Whitman
