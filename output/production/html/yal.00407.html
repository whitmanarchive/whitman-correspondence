Tuesday afternoon  
 3 o'clock.

I wrote you Sunday enclosing the $20, which I suppose you rec'd all safe.

I am still anchored here in my bed—I am sitting up now on the side—Mrs. O'Connor has just been to see me—I was glad to see her—I am still improving, but slowly—the doctor did not come yesterday, which I suppose is a good sign—I expect him this afternoon or evening—he evidently thinks I am on the gain—Pete has just come in, & will take this to the p. o. for me—Love to you, dear mother, & to all—

Walt.
