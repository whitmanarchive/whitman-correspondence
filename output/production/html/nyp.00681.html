Camden April 26 '89

Deeply hope this will find you easy & comfortable—N[elly]'s card rec'd yesterday—weather rainy wh' we wanted—temperature mild—I have a big bunch of lilacs on the table near, (from my yard)—Have been looking over some vols: Stedman's big "Am: Literature"—seems to be all admirably done—I was reading Paul Jones's report to Congress (or his fight with the Serapis)—it all came back to me.

Love always— Walt Whitman
