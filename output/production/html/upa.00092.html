  Camden Nov: 10 P M

The "Fusiyama" has come all right & I have no doubt will be beneficial—best thanks—I am under a cloud to-day physically—& the weather is dark & rainy—but no doubt a change for the better soon

—I saw Morse ab't an hour after you left & paid him the $10— next forenoon rec'd your card countermanding—Conway has been here lecturing ag't the President —as he did ag't the Devil—Probably both are indispensable & immovable—

Walt Whitman
