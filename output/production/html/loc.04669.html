  LIPPINCOTT'S MONTHLY MAGAZINE Philadelphia, April 29th, 189 1890 Walt Whitman, Camden, N.J. Dear Mr. Whitman:—

Herewith enclose check for Sixty Dollars ($60.00) in payment for the four poems entitled "Sounds of Winter, Unexpressed, The Sunset Breeze, After the Argument.

Kindly acknowledge the safe receipt of this check and oblige

Yours very truly, J.M. Stoddart
