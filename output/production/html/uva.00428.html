  Camden March 18

Yes—I have rec'd such a letter from T—& do not object to your itemizing it—though it must be done carefully—Some such way as the following?

—Walt Whitman

Tennyson has written an affectionate and thankful letter to Walt Whitman on the comments of the latter—see Critic of Jan. 1st upon the supplementary 'Locksley Hall.' Is not this the only instance known of the English Laureate formally 'noticing a notice'?
