  Camden early pm March 16 '91

Still very poorly—obstinate rigid horrible indigestion (now two months and over) at the bottom and base of all—wonder that I keep up as well as I do—all the poetic bits for the little 2d annex proof finished & sent in—printers now on the prose pages—all will be very brief & scrappy—a fair night past, considering—eat a little—drink Fred:' water every mn'g & have taken four powders no mark'd effect

WW
