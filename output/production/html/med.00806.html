July 28/87

Had for dinner stewed blackberries—a favorite tipple of mine—and boiled rice. . . . Sidney Morse has made a second big head—an improvement, if I dare say so, on the first. The second is the modern spirit, awake & alert as well as calm—contrasted with the antique & Egyptian calmness & rest of the first. We have decided on the second.
