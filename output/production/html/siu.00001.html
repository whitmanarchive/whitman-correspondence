  Washington, Oct. 17, 1871. Dear Sir:

In answer to your letter of September 5, (I beg pardon for not replying to it before,) I have to inform you that some time ago Dion Thomas, bookseller, 2d story, Fulton st. north side, about midway bet. Nassau and Broadway, had some copies of 1st edition Leaves of Grass—but whether he still has them to sell I cannot say—

   

I can procure you of the artist a good photograph,—the price is $1.

Walt Whitman.

The last edition of my poems complete I publish & sell myself.
