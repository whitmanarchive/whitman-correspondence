  CHARLES F. WINGATE, CONSULTING SANITARY ENGINEER, No. 119 PEARL STREET, NEW YORK. New York, April 18th, 1890 Dear Sir,

As an old admirer will you permit me to ask what book or books have you found in your experience to supply comfort or consolation to the sorrowing.

I am compiling a little memorial volume—

Yours truly C. F. Wingate
