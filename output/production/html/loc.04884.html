  Sep 14. '91 1833 SPRUCE STREET PHILADELPHIA. My dear Mr Whitman

I should like to bring Dr. de Schweinitz one of the very best oculists in the city to look at your eyes on Wednesday at about 3 30 You will like him. He is your kind of man & I hope the hour suits you. Please let me know as above

Yours cordially Talcott Williams   Sands—20 | Good Bye 20 | Backward Glance 18
