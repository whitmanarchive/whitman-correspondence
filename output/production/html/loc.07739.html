Camden Evn'g Dec: 13 '89

Continuing fairly—have been out in the wheel chair I guess two miles—Sunny mild weather here—So Browning is dead—as it has happen'd I never read him much—(Does he not exercise & rather worry the intellect—something like a sum in arithmetic?)—Am sitting here alone as usual in my den—all right I guess—

Walt Whitman
