Camden New Jersey U S America Nov: 25 '90

Y'rs of 15th rec'd & welcomed—the celluloid photo plate came safe (rec'd gratefully) & I grant its use to a magazine article ab't me (N[ew] E[ngland] Magazine) by Horace Traubel wh' he is finishing—will send you, if printed. Will soon send you (soon as I get some) a Phila: mag. with my latest poemet. Sunny & cold here—Ab't same as usual. Love to you & Wallace (show him this) & to all the boys—

Walt Whitman
