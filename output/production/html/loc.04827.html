  Anderton, near Chorley Lancashire, England 16 Dec. 1891 Dear Walt,

I haven't time now to write a proper letter, but I cannot let the mail go without sending a line or two to express my constant thoughts of you, & my loving good wishes.

I called at Dr J's last night, & he lent me Lynch's "Modern Authors" which he had just received. Greenhalgh called while I     was there. All well

Hoping to hear a better account of you

Yours affectionately J.W. Wallace
