  Mr Whitman, Sir,

I took from Dr Russell your letter to Mr Redpath, to stir some warm hearts to aid you in your blessed work among our sick & wounded boys. My sister, Mrs Charles P. Curtis, has already written you, her husband's tears and her own ones—your touching words, coined into gold, or greenbacks. I inclose you to-day $30, the result of an application to my friends, the Misses Wigglesworth.

Respectfully, Hannah E. Stevenson 80 Temple St. Boston
