  Camden Aug:2 '89

The sun is out—quiet & warm & very moist—nothing very new— Dull & rather poorly with me—I send two copies of the little new morocco bound ed'n of L of G. by this mail—is it that way you wanted? Yr letters come, always welcome. Had a letter f'm Hamlin Garland—with first rate carte photo:—notice a good potrait of Tennyson (in old age) in Aug: Century—All well—

Walt Whitman
