  Washington D.C. Aug 22, 1871. Dear Mr. Dowden,

I have received your kind letter, & your review in the Westminster, & thank you heartily. I wish to write you at more length, & may do so before long. I take real comfort in the thought that I have such friends in   Ireland, including yourself. I wish to hear more of Mr. Tyrrell, whom you speak of.

Walt Whitman
