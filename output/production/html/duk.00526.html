Only a word my darling to say how precious precious your letter is & how much I feel your thinking of me now, but​ But​ my dearest you will be better & then I may write to you you have my constant prayers & thoughts always. but​ But​ my dear dear brother only get better. there​ There​ is​ are​ no words to say what I feel for you
