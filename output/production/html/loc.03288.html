All right my dear J R—$60 for the Booth article will do, in full—(I reserve the right of printing it in future collections of my writings—this is indispensable.) I have been & am lingering under the miserable inertia following my sunstroke—otherwise should have sent you one or two articles—have them on the stocks—

Am very slowly gaining the tally of my previous strength—had none to spare before—

Thank you, dear friend, for your services & affectionate good will.

Sent James Redpath | Aug. 12, '1885.
