  PAN-REPUBLIC CONGRESS COMMITTEE. OFFICE OF SECRETARY AND TREASURER, 20 SPRUCE STREET. WM. H. ARNOUX, Chairman. WM. O. MCDOWELL, Secretary. NEWARK, N.J., Aug 21st 1891. "Walt Whitman" My dear Sir

On Oct 12th (Discovery day falls this year on Sunday) our Pan Republic Congress organizing government in Independence Hall Phil to adopt and added to all peoples and to organize the Human Freedom League In the evening we have a public meeting in the Academy of Music The time, the place, the work are all suggestive and is it to be wondered at that our Comt turn to you for the poem of the occasion.

Hoping that you will favor us I am Very truly yours W O McDowell       The Enclosed letter settles many things ahead of elaborate publicity see notes Dec 14 1891
