  c/o C. V. SKOKOWSKI, 
 36 BROMFIELD STREET, 
 BOSTON. 24 / July 88. Dear Mr. Whitman,

a few days ago I have returned from a short trip to Holland and England. Met Rossetti, young Gilchrist and others of your London friends, they all send their love to you. How is your health?

Always Yours C. Sadakichi Hartmann
