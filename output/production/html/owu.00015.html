  Camden PM April 22 '90

Sick yet with grip (the tail of the comet I call it)—the confections rec'd, thanks—Sat'day's Trans. rec'd—yes send me the 6 if conv.—Horace T. is faithful—Dr Bucke is worn & half sick & is coming down here to seaside—many visitors (mostly denied)—lots of applications, comp'ments flatteries, &c. &c.

God bless you & Mrs: K— Walt Whitman
