Camden March 3 '89

Am feeling better all day from the encouraging & sunny news my young friend Horace Traubel brings back to me of you—God grant it may all be fulfill'd, & I have great trust it will. H T will send hence (March 4) two copies of my big book by express, one for you & one for Nelly—yours has the German booklet also—if you want more of the Sarrazin trans: I can send them—I have not yet seen Dr B[ucke] but it is sure he & H T had a happy & illuminating visit—

Walt Whitman
