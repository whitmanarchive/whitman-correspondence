  J. H. TENNENT.  
 Veterinary Surgeon,  
 LONDON, ONT. OFFICE—KING STREET, OPPOSITE MARKET.  
 RESIDENCE—COR. KING & WELLINGTON STREETS  
 TELEPHONE—OFFICE, 688 : RESIDENCE 278. London, Ont. 1889

What money I have I expected it to put me through the last term, & run me up untill now; what I have not used is out on interest & will be unable to get it untill next fall, so under the circumstances I think I had better take a position for a while, & make money enough to run me through.

I would like you to answer this right away & tell me what you think is best for me to do, I will take your advise no matter what it is. I had made arrangements to go to college this fall, & would be through in a year & a half from next Oct, but I guess I will have to remain in the soup a while longer.
