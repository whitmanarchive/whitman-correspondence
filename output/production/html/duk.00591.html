  5 April 1870 Brooklyn April 5 My dear Walt

i have just received your letter and the order all safe am glad to hear you are better of your cold am glad walter dear its in your power to deal so generously to your mother)

i am rather better of my lameness now i was very lame for a week or so we have so much easterly winds that makes me worse helen price has been here to day she comes from the office has just gone)

George has been home he went away yesterday monday he wont come   come again if nothing happens till the 1 of may i had a letter from matty too she says Jeffy is coming to pittsburgh this week she dident know but he would come to brooklyn

i thought probably he would after hearing of poor jesse death i doo wish the doctor would write something about the poor unfortunates death it would be some consolation to hear

good bie walter dear

i have also written to hanna i found some envelopes directed to you walter
