  see notes April 12 1888 "The Nineteenth Century," 1 Paternoster Square, London, E.C. May 19th 1887 My Dear Sir

I greatly regret being unable to avail myself of the Poem "November Boughs" which you so kindly sent me with your note dated May 2d. In order not to put you to inconvenience by delay, I return them at once enclosed herewith—& with very many thanks for your kind thought of me I remain

Yours very truly James Knowles Walt Whitman Esq
