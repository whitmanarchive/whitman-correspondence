328 Mickle street Camden New Jersey March 20 '89 Dear Sir

Am not definitely certain f'm yours of 18th (rec'd—thanks) whether you request the new big 900 vol. complete poems & prose works—If I were I sh'd send it at once—The price is $6—please if you wish that send me the am't in p o order, & I will immediately dispatch the Vol. by express.

Respectfully &c: Walt Whitman
