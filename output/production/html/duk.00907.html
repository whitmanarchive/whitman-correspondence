Camden Tuesday Evn'g Oct: 16 '88

Thanks for the Thackeray pamphlet, (wh' I have been reading through this afternoon)—& the Transcripts—Pretty much the same sing song with me—no worse—no better—I am waiting with anxiety to hear from O'C[onnor]—the bad trouble with his eyesight—

Walt Whitman
