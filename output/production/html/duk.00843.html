Camden Saturday 
  3 P M

Showery & coolish here the last two days—I am now sitting here by the open window—have had my dinner, eaten with relish—Sidney Morse is still here working—You shall see a photo of the figure he has made of me soon. Herbert Gilchrist is here in America. The news from O'Connor is unfavorable. I suppose you rec'd the pictures I sent for Mrs. F[airchild]—

Walt Whitman
