Camden NJ—US America PM Sept: 29 '91

Altho' you doubtless have full & particular news f'm J W W[allace] & perhaps Dr B[ucke] I will send you these—probably by the time all this gets to you W is here with us—At present I feel like writing myself & condition the same subject continued—possibly a little plus as it is to day cloudy & tolerable after an unprecedented September hot spell & continued sun-glare—bad for me—I am up & sitting here by the window as I write—Love—

Walt Whitman
