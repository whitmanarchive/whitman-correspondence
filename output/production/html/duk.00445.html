  Van Ness House L. S. Drew & H. N. Clark Managers U. A. Woodbury, Proprietor. Burlington, Vt. 1890 April 22d Bro Walt.

Very chilly morning—

Han not so well—Concernd about yourself—Thinks that the exertion of the occasion may have weakend you—

She cannot write—She is very poorly—

Gratefully Yours— You will remember us— Charlie
