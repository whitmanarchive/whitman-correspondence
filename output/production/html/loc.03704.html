  FREDERICK A. STOKES, Successor to 
 WHITE, STOKES, & ALLEN, 
 PUBLISHERS, STATIONERS, AND IMPORTERS, 182 Fifth Avenue, New York. April 30th, 1887. Mr. Walt Whitman: 328 Mickle St., Camden, N.J. Dear Sir:

I have your kind card of the 29th inst., and in reply would say that you are indebted entirely to Mr. Browne for the copy of 'Bugle Echoes' which you have received.

I take pleasure in forwarding the card to him as you request, also in saying that in case you desire another copy of the book for any purpose, I shall feel honored if you will allow me to send it to you with my compliments.

Very truly yours, Frederick A. Stokes Dictated.   Fredk A Stokes
