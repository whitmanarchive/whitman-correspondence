  Camden near noon Sept:4 '89

Fine weather—nothing very different or new—am feeling passably comfortable—rec'd y'rs of Dick Flynn's safe return & of y'r satisfaction with the picture! Did it come in good order? We too all like it well—T B and Frank and H Traubel and Herbert G all here last evn'g—Mr & Mrs Ingram this forenoon—

I am sitting as usual in the big chair in second story room as I write—was out in the wheel chair last evn'g—

Walt Whitman
