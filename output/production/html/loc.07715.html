  Camden P M Oct: 22 '89

Fine sunny weather continued—Warren Fritzinger, one of Mrs D's sailor boys, is acting as my nurse & helper—I have just had a good massage—get along fairly—send you the London Telegraph with Sir E A's letter. Y'rs rec'd—Suppose Ed is there all right by this time—Shall I send you my N Y Critic after rec'd? You shall have the 1872 ed'n of L of G. & I think I have Harrington for you too—

W W
