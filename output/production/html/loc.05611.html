  JAMES R. OSGOOD AND CO.  
 PUBLISHERS BOSTON May 20, 1882 Dear Mr. Whitman:

Please find our check enclosed with orders for the delivery of your plates, sheets &c, and receipt for the same which please sign & return if found correct.

Very Truly Yours: B. H. Ticknor Walt Whitman Esq.
