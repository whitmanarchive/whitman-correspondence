  Camden Oct. 10 '89

Y'rs of 8th rec'd—weather pleasant here—nothing very new— shall send you the sheets of the "Dinner book" soon as I get them (or Horace will)—the press work seems to have been delayed, but I believe it is now done—McKay was here yesterday—p'd me $88.56 royalty &c (the past six months)—will send the N[ew] E[ngland] Magazine—the carpenter &c here to-day making repairs, shores, &c—(the old shanty some danger of sagging, tumbling &c.)

Walt Whitman
