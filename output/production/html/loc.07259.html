  Superintendent's Office 
 ASYLUM FOR THE INSANE 
 LONDON. 
  ONTARIO 
 London, Ont.,25 Oct. 1888

The "Press" came to hand yesterday and leaves from Lippencotts​ Lippincott's​ this morning—thanks. Nothing new here about going East—expect to hear something decisive almost any mail now—shall notify you as soon as I do. Am deep into another report to the government on a subject they expect to legislate on the coming session

Will soon write again

Affectionately R M Bucke     See notes Oct 27 1888
