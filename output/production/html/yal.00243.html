  Camden Jan. 17 '87— P M

The "Press" newspaper of Philadelphia has just sent over to say to me that they would like to have the article "New Orleans in 1848" (MS. sent on by me) to print it in their columns—with your permission—Jan. 25, giving you due credit, & saying it appears in your columns that day—So if agreeable send them an early proof, for that purpose—address "R W Kerswell, "Press" newspaper Philadelphia"—(I have just promised Mr K to write the foregoing)—

Walt Whitman
