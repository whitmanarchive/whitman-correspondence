  New York, Friday afternoon—July 19. 1872 1872 Dear boy Pete,

I rec'd your letter yesterday—nothing very new with me—am better than I was when I wrote you before—shall return to Washington next week somewhere about the middle of the week.

Pete, you must try to keep good heart—Perhaps this will find you at work again—if not, you must keep up a cheerful heart, all the same—I have just been spending a couple of hours with Joaquin Miller—I like him real well

Walt

$10 enclosed
