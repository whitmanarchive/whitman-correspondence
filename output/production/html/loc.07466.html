Camden P M Nov: 8 '88

Yours of 6th just rec'd—still leaning to favorable—I have [just?] had a complete bath & flesh-rubbing—very good—Ed: W[ilkins] is very good and attentive to me—No visitors to-day yet—cloudy, damp, coolish weather—

As you see Harrison is probably elected—this one tack—but the ship will go on on her voyage many a sea & many a year yet—

Walt Whitman
