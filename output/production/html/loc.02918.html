  The North American Review. 3 East Fourteenth Street, New York. Dictated. December 16, 1890 Dear Mr. Whitman:

We intended to use your article on National Literature in the January number, but unforseen circumstances prevented us. Probably it will go into the February number, and in any event you shall have the proofs you ask for.

I am Faithfully yours, William H. Rideing To Walt Whitman, Esq.
