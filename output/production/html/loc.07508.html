Camden March 15 '88

Everything continuing on ab't the same with me—was out to dinner at my friends the Harneds Sunday—Harry Stafford has been here—the throat trouble still—otherwise well—O'C[onnor] is taking massage treatment—the H[erald] has paid my first bill—I continue on. Y'r letters rec'd—A terrible three days' storm & cold & gale & snow—but has not affected me particularly—

W W
