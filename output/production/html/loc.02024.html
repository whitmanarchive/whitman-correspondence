  Camden New Jersey April 5 '87

Thanks dear friend for your letter—the third—from California & ab't William—Continue to write, for I am very anxious & look for the letters—I continue ab't the same,—very feeble, but in good heart.—Am to deliver the "Death of Lincoln" screed to-night before the Unitarians, here in Camden—& the 14th April in New York, (if I can get there) the same lecture—Conway has been here to see me—I am writing & collating a paper ab't Elias Hicks—Write soon—

—Walt Whitman
