  Howard Lake, Minn​ July 21, 1875 My Dear Friend Walt

I was glad to hear from you But Sory​ Sorry​ to know that your Health was not Improving But glad that you remember me My health's Failig​ Failing​ Fast as I have told you In letters Before that I am trying for an Envelid​ Invalid​ Pentian​ Pension​ I have Now only the Need of to​ two​ witnesses of my Comrades they Live in W. Va​ and if as you have Been in the habit of Asisting​ Assisting​ Soldier People I would Like to have the Lone​ Loan​ of 65$ untill​ until​ Next Aprilth Want to go   to W Va​ and get those witnesses I will Pay you Intrest​ Interest​ Let me know at once If you Please, I can not find out their adress​ address​ and to get then​ them​ I must go to West. W. Va.​ M D Halans is my Pentian​ Pension​ agent St. Paul Minn​ Howard Lake is Improving Fast I wished you would come and visit this place My wife sends her Best wishes with mine

Write soon Wm Stansbery   Wm Stansberry July '75
