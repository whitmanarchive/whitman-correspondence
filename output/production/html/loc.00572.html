  New York May 17th Dear Walt.—

What the devil is the matter? Nothing serious I hope.—It seems mighty queer that I cannot succeed in having one word from you.—I swear I would have thought you would be the last man in this world to neglect me.—But I am afraid.—

Lizzie is married!, Johnny is dead! Walt has forgotten.

Such is life, Yours, Fred.
