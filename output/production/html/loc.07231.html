  Superintendent's Office. 
 ASYLUM FOR THE INSANE 
 LONDON. 
  ONTARIO 
 London, Ont.,3 Sept 1888

I have your note of 31st written on "November Boughs" paper—it is first class and will make a handsome book—I hope you will not trim the margins but leave the paper its full size—of course you will not trim the margins in vol. of C.W.s? The weather here is wonderful—so cool, bright and cheery—the place looks well—autumn flowers at their best—I fear, dear Walt, you are feeling pretty miserable but you must not give up—there is a glorious future ahead of you yet.

RM Bucke     See notes Sept 5, '88
