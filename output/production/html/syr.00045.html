[London, Ont.,] 26 March [188]9

The two post cards (re Mrs Costelloe and O'C.) are this moment to hand. We absolutely must not look for any but the worst kind of news from poor O'C. the only consolation in the case is that /he/ does not suffer, at least not much. Some day (quite likely soon) he will have a series of fits that will end his life. It is bad, infernally bad, but we must stand it.
