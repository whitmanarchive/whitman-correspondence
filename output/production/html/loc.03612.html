  5 ENDSLEIGH GARDENS. N.W. 11 Jany/86. Dear Whitman,

This note is written beforehand, in expectation of my paying-in tomorrow at a Post-Office the ￡33.16.6. wh.​ I named to you in my recent letter. The postal order, on my obtaining it, will be enclosed herein, & dispatched   to you. Since the date of my last something further has come in: it will be accounted for at a future opportunity.

On 13 Jany I expect to leave London, & stay some four weeks with my family at the Clarendon Hotel, Ventnor, I. of Wight.

Yours with affectionate regard, W. M. Rossetti     recd Jan: 25 '86 ￡33.16.6
