  Camden New Jersey U S America June 14 '89—

Thanks for letters—& papers too—We are all (Dr. B particularly) more interested in movements & your fortunes &c: there than you suppose

I am getting along the same, fairly.—get out daily in the wheel chair—write a little—keep up pretty good spirits—cloudy rainy warm weather—

Love to you Alys, Mr S & all not forgetting the little girls. Walt Whitman
