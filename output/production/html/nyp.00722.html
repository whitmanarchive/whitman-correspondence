Camden Nov: 20 '88

Still ab't holding my own & comfortable—nothing very new or notable—The Transcript I send with Hamlin Garland's notice of Nov: Boughs—Still keep my sick room—Clear sunny cool to-day. How are you getting along?—The pub'r yesterday had an order for 250 copies Nov: B. for Scotland—

W.W.
