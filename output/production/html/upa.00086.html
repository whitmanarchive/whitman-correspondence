  Camden New Jersey US America March 12 '88 Dear H—

Yours of Feb: 17 is rec'd all right—best thanks to Mrs. Rosamund Powell for continuous subscription, safely rec'd—I am in good heart & still writing a little but near the end of my rope I opine—Mrs.S[tafford] was here lately—& she is first rate—all the rest pretty well—

Tell me the fortunes of the bust, whatever happens

—Morse is still west—Rhys is in Boston

W W
