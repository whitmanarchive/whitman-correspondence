  LIPPINCOTT'S MONTHLY MAGAZINE Philadelphia, March 11 1891 Dear Mr Whitman.

The bearer of this is my son—who inherits his father's admiration for you. He will explain his mission & if you can gratify him, I will greatly appreciate the kindness.

The Whitman articles are exciting considerable attention.—Will get up the notices after while and send them to you for perusal.

Ever yours truly J. M. Stoddart—
