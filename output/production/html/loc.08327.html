Camden N J—US America Sunday Noon Oct 3 '91

Word yesterday f'm J W W[allace] f'm Fenelon Falls, Canada—Expect him here in a few days—Y'rs f'm Annan &c: rec'd—thanks—Traubel just in—matters much the same—Dr. B[ucke] doubtless back home—Aff: regards to Fred Wild—bad times with me—constipation & bladder trouble to the fore—

W W
