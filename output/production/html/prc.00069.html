Camden April 19 '88

Billy Thompson, of Gloucester, has just been here to invite me down to baked shad dinner at his place, Tuesday next, abt 2.—Wishes me to invite you in his name & my own—You come here say ½ past 12 & we will drive down in my rig—Be back by dark or before. . . .

Walt Whitman
