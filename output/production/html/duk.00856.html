Camden Thursday noon Aug 4 '87

All going ab't as usual. I hear from Dr B[ucke] and from Rhys. O'C[onnor] is at Bar Harbor, Maine. I am feeling quite comfortable to-day. Not so hot here—S[ylvester] B[axter] has sent me nearly $800 for the cottage—I send you two Wash'n Stars—send them to Dr Bucke—I am going out for a long drive this afternoon—

Walt Whitman
