Jan 23 '92 pm

Am deadly weak yet—otherwise inclined to favorable—bowel drain sufficient—appetite fair—The plaster cast come safe to Dr J[ohnston]—Bolton—Ralph Moore is dead—Tom Harned well—my doctors & attendants cont[inue] first rate—Horace ever faithful—am propp'd up in bed—

God bless you all—

Walt Whitman
