Camden N J Sept: 6

I cheerfully give permission to print "O Captain My Captain" in your book1—

Walt Whitman

Notes

  • 1. Brander Matthews (1852–1929), professor of English literature at Columbia University from 1892 to 1924, included the poem in Poems of American Patriotism (1882), 268–269. [back]
