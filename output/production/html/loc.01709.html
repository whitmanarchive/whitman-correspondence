  Attorney General's Office, Washington. Nov 5 1870 My dear Mr. Huntington:

I write to say I would like to postpone the pleasure of my visit to, & breakfast with, you & Mrs. Huntington till Sunday morning 13th inst.—instead of to-morrow.

—Best respects to Mrs. Huntington.

Walt Whitman

I send the Galaxy with one of my last pieces—as I am not certain whether I sent it to you at the time.

Walt Whitman
