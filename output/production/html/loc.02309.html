  800 SIXTEENTH STREET, LAFAYETTE SQUARE. Washington D.C. Mar. 22. Dear Walt Whitman

Have you a copy of the Centennial Edition of your Poems you could let me have—or any edition with your autograph? I want it very much for a friend.

Do not send the cheque back even if you have not the book.

Can I be of any service to you? if so you need only command me.

Yours affectionately John Hay         If not delivered in 5 days | return to 800—16th St. | Washington D. C. see notes Mar 24 1892
