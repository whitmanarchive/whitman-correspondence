  Superintendent's Office. Asylum for the Insane London. Ontario London, Ont., 28 Sept 1889

Yours of 25th reached me last evening. I was real glad to get it and the note from John Burroughs enclosed. This morning the Harper's Weekly came with the little gem of a sonnet—thanks, hearty thanks. A letter today from our New York meter partner—he has seen William Gurd and the new index—pronounces it first class. The meter prospects look at present exceedingly bright. All well here. Perfect weather, bright and cool. Glorious nights to sleep!

Love to you R M Bucke     Removal of stamp before
