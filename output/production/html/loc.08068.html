Camden NJ—US America Sept: 15 '91

Perfect weather cont'd—fairly well with me—y'r letter & "New Review" rec'd—Thanks—no d[oub]t J W W[allace] is having good time in Canada—I have advised him to just lay quiet with little or no excitement or unrest—fallow—(he has excitement enough in himself)—I am arranging a really complete ed'n of L of G. 438 pp: wh' I will send you when ready—nothing new—affectionate compliments to all—

Walt Whitman
