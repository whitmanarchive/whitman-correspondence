  Nov. 8. 1889.

I have just this day sent word to Horace Traubel that I hope to see you next week, & to-night I get your postal sent from Boston, to me here, South Weymouth, Mass. Very glad to hear from you. I must now turn my face homeward. I hoped to gain more, but am so much better than I was that I hope I shall gain more. I will send word again.

With love always, Nelly O'Connor.
