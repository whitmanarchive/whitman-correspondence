I bt Traubel's piece—The New Englander Whitman number—today & frau & I are sitting here by kitchen fire enjoying it. It's the best thing ever done here in Massat. & on W.W. good for T. & Mead! What, I wonder, do the Vice Society, idiots here think of these things?

W.S.K. May 12 '91 see notes May 18 1891
