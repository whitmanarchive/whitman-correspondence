  Farringford, Freshwater, Isle of Wight. Jan 26/92 For Mr Walt Whitman

My father thanks you cordially for yr new edition of 'Leaves of Grass'—He is not allowed by his doctors to write more than is absolutely necessary—We are very sorry to hear that you have been unwell but hope that—as the spring advances—your health         will improve.

With my greetings for the New Year.

Yr faithfully Hallam Tennyson   To Walt Whitman
