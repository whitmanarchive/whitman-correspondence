  Anderton, near Chorley. Lancashire, England 2 Jan 92. Dear Walt,

Just a few lines to express my constant thought of you and my dearest love. After the suspense of the last two weeks I am heartily glad that the beginning of the New Year finds you rallying from your sickness & spared to us once more. God bless you & our best love to you.

Horace kindly sent us a cable message each day during the worst period, &   we at once communicated the news to your chief English friends. I have had letters in return from most of them including Symonds. We are all pleased to know your improved condition.

I have just received a post card from Carpenter who is spending a few days in Kent & London. I have heard from him several times lately.

All going on much as usual here. Yesterday, (New Year's Day) was a perfect day—the finest I have seen since my return.

 

I write this rather hurriedly. But with love to you & best wishes from my inmost heart.

I remain always Yours affectionately JW Wallace
