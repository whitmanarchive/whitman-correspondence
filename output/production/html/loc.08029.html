  Camden mid PM April 15 '91

Y'rs of 13th comes with the bad foot acc't—a spell of warm weather here—got out to-day for 15 minutes in wheel chair, (first out in four months)—but it was so confusing & blinding I came back soon—Every thing goes on fairly I guess—proofs nearly done—Dr L here yesterday.

Walt Whitman
