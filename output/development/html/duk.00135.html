Feb. 9, 90.



Walt Whitman

Camden, N.J.

Dear Sir:

I have this day rec'd your Complete Poems & Prose by mail.

Will you kindly favor me with a bill of the same? I shall be most happy to remit by return mail.

Yours very respectfully

John W. Cook
