London Ontario Canada
June 17 '80



Editor Denver Tribune:

Herewith find a letter from me for the paper. The price is $10—If used it must be printed in the paper of Tuesday, June 22 (or afterwards)—The letter is sent in the same manner as this to several other papers (one each in New York, Boston, Philadelphia, Cincinnati, Chicago) on the same imperative condition—this condition being a point of honor—If used, please send me the pay by mail here, as this place will be my head-quarters for the ensuing two months.



Walt Whitman
