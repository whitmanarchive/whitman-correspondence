Camden Oct: 19 '911

Dear sister, I am fairly to-day, sitting here alone as usual — My Bolton, Eng. friend2 still here. Had buckwheat cakes & coffee for b’kfast — have not yet sent the big book3 to Dr Bingham4 but shall soon — Best love to you & and hope this will find you comfortable — y’rs rec'd & welcomed always — $1 enc’d



W W
