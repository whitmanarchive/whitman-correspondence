431 Stevens st.
cor West.
Camden,
N. Jersey.
Feb. 19.1

Had another par[alytic] stroke Tuesday last—but not severe. This time, right side —Am now about as usual—Snow storm as I write, but I am going out, but not far—



WW

Notes:

1. This postcard bears the address, "Peter Doyle, | M st South—bet 4½ & 6th | Washington, D.C." It is postmarked: "Camden | Feb. | 19 | N.J."

The four brief postcards to Doyle in February 1875 were written on Fridays, and the discussion of his ailments is confirmed in Whitman's letter to John and Ursula Burroughs, which can be positively assigned to this year. Obviously this postcard was written before Whitman's visit to Washington in November 1875.[back]
