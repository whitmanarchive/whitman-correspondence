Camden Oct: 26 ‘91 1

Dear sister Am sitting here so-so— Nothing very different — get along fairly, considering — have buckwheat cakes for b’kfast — relish my meals fairly — visitors, some f’m a distance — some f’m Calcutta not long ago — fine sunny weather — children in to see me bro’t a big bunch of fall wild flowers — the big stout Dutch woman is out in front playing f’m “Sonnambula”2 on a capital organ & a little boy going all around collecting the pennies — How hearty (& dirty) they look — — God bless you Han dear — $2 enc’d —



W W
