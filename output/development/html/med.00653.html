Camden,
April 9, 1882

I rec'd the $7 for the Longfellow bit1—thanks.

Notes:

1. "Death of Longfellow" appeared in The Critic on April 8; it was reprinted in Essays from "The Critic" (1882), 41–45, and in Specimen Days, ed. Floyd Stovall (New York: New York University Press, 1963) , 284–286, 355. Whitman sent the article to the magazine on April 2 (Whitman's Commonplace Book, Charles E. Feinberg Collection of the Papers of Walt Whitman, 1839–1919, Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.).[back]
