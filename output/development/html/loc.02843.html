Camden New Jersey
U S America
Oct 231

I send to-day to same address as this card my Vol. Two Rivulets—Please notify me, (by postal card will do) soon as it reaches you safely—



W W

Notes:

1. This letter's envelope bears the address, "H S Theobold | 20 Talbot Road | Bayswater | London W | England." It is postmarked: "Camden | Oct | 23 | N.J."

H. S. Theobold was a London art collector or dealer, probably a friend of William Michael Rossetti's brother.[back]
