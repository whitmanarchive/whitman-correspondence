Dear Mr. Whitman:1

If it is not asking too much, dare I beg that you will do me the great pleasure to transcribe your poem, The Pallid Wreath, for my collection of autographs?

The favor would be valued more than I can tell you.

I have the honor to remain

Your obliged servant,

Gustav A. Roedell.

Gallipolis, Ohio.
