Camden
Dec 11



Dear friend

Your father's kind note & yours recd—Yes with pleasure—I'll be there by 6 Saturday, Evn'g,1



Walt Whitman

Notes:

1. Whitman was with the Smiths from December 15, Saturday, to December 17 (Whitman's Commonplace Book, Charles E. Feinberg Collection of the Papers of Walt Whitman, 1839–1919, Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.).[back]
