431 Stevens Street

Camden New Jersey

U S America
Nov: 26 '80

Yours rec'd with Enc: thanks—I forward to day by mail (to same address as this card)1 my Two Vols: as requested—Will you please send me a postal soon as they reach you safely notifying me?



Walt Whitman

Notes:

1. Doggett lived in Bristol, England (Whitman's Commonplace Book, Charles E. Feinberg Collection of the Papers of Walt Whitman, 1839–1919, Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.).[back]
