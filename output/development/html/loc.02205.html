431 Stevens Street

Camden New Jersey
Jan: 8



My dear Miss G

Yours of yesterday rec'd—glad you like the papers—the $20 rec'd—thanks—Send the proof (which is important) & it will be sent back the same night, so that you will get it next forenoon—

Yes, you shall have two more1



Walt Whitman

Notes:

1. Whitman sent the next two articles in the series on April 9 (Whitman's Commonplace Book, Charles E. Feinberg Collection of the Papers of Walt Whitman, 1839–1919, Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.).[back]
