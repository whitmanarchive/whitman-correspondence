328 Mickle Street

Camden New Jersey1
Dec. 19 '87

I send you same mail with this, Leaves of Grass and Two Rivulets —2 vols. Please send me a card soon as rec'd notifying me—pay rec'd—



Walt Whitman

Correspondent:
Loftus was an instructor at Riverview Academy in Poughkeepsie in 1887. He paid $5 for the volumes (Whitman's Commonplace Book; Charles E. Feinberg Collection of the Papers of Walt Whitman, 1839–1919, Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.).

Notes:

1. This postal card is addressed: J. P. Loftus | Poughkeepsie | New York. This postal card is postmarked: Camden, N.J. | Dec 19 1887 | 4:30 PM.[back]
