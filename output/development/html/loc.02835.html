431 Stevens st
Camden N J
May 311

Letter & enc: recd—Thanks—will write more fully soon—middling comfortable to-day—



W.W.

Notes:

1. This postcard bears the address, "John Swinton | 124 East 38th st | New York City." It is postmarked: "Camden | May | 31 | N.J.

Though the text of this postcard is not helpful, it seems to refer to Swinton's support of Walt Whitman's 1876 edition; see Whitman's and letters to Swinton. (This postcard also resembles Whitman's postcard to Edmund W. Gosse in appearance and in handwriting.)[back]
