Camden NJ US America
April 22 '891



Y'r welcome letter has come & Mary's word—my best love to all, not forgetting the dear little ones—

—Nothing very new with me—am still a prisoner here in Mickle Street 2d story, sitting at this moment in the big ratan chair—are you (father, mother, &) domiciled still at 44?—yesterday here almost hot—



Walt Whitman

Correspondent:
Alys Smith (1867–1951) was a daughter of Robert Pearsall Smith and eventually married the philosopher Bertrand Russell.
