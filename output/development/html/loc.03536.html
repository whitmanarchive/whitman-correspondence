NEWARK, N.J.
Jan. 2, 1876



My dear Mr. Whitman;

Many thanks for your letter, & the promise of an early copy of your book.

Enclosed please find what I made of your facts for the New York Herald of this date.

Faithfully yours

Jeannette L. Gilder


Mr. Walt Whitman

Camden N.J.
