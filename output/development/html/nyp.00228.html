Carver Hospital

Washington D.C.
May 14th 1864



Mr Walt Whitman

Dear Sir

Will you please come to this Hospital as soon as possible, and see a member of your Brothers Company. immediately after I was wounded the Capt gave me your address and told me to send for you as soon as I got here. I arrived here this morning. You will find me in Ward 23.

Very Respy Your Obdt Servt

Aaron Smith

P.S. I will enclose the Capt's address to you AS
