Feb 11th 1875

I received your Postal Card Feb-5-1875

But you have not forgoten your littel Michigan friend yetâ€”Well i have not forgotton my Hospital Friend yet and I hope that I never will

I read in the newspaper that you had been sick but I trust that you have got well again

I had a shock of Paralasis on the right side of my person I was layed up three weeks you see I can not write as I used too before I was afflicted. I am not abel to work on my farm and I have rented it for 5 years Well Walt I think I will go to California next fall is noth hapens

This is the first letter I have writen since this accidence hapand

I will writ better one next time

Good by God Bless my old acquaintance Walt Whitman

Yours Truly

Ruben Farwell


address

Nankin P.

Wayne Co

Michigan

You see that it bothers me some to make letters

Excuse all mistaks

Reuben Farwell Feb. 11 '75
