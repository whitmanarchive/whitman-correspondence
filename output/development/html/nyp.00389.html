Department of Justice,

Washington,
Mar. 10, 1873.



Walt Whitman, Esq.,

Sir:

You are hereby transferred from the office of the Attorney General to a clerkship of the third class in the office of Solicitor of the Treasury, to take effect on the 1st instant.

Very respectfully

George H. Williams,

Attorney General.
