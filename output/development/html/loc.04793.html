Camden
Evn'g June 16 '911

How are you standing the hot weather, sister mine— if it is anything like here—two days now & probably more & worse to come—yet I get on fairly considering—eat very little (have no appetite)—had a fair night last &c:—just had my supper, bread &cold tea—$2 enc'd— y'r welcome card rec'd



Walt Whitman
