THE CRITIC

EDITORIAL ROOMS

52 LAFAYETTE PLACE

NEW YORK1
15 Jan. 18912



Dear Mr. Whitman:

I take the pleasure in enclosing the amount you fixed as the price of "The Pallid Wreath." The slips have already been sent to you.

With best wishes for the New Year, I remain

Sincerely yours

Joseph B. Gilder

(See our review of last ten years in this week's Critic: to-day is our tenth anniversary.)

Correspondent:
Joseph Benson Gilder (1858–1936) was, with his sister Jeannette Leonard Gilder (1849–1916), co-editor of The Critic.
