431 Stevens Street
Camden New Jersey
Feb: 16 '81



Dear Sirs

Yours of 14th with the $5 enclosed, has arrived safely1—The slips came all right—Thanks—



Walt Whitman

If you have some spare numbers of your World running back six or seven weeks send them to me—

Notes:

1. The Literary World printed "The Dead Carlyle" on February 12 (see the letter from Whitman to Jeannette L. Gilder of ).[back]
