431 Stevens st.

Camden, New Jersey.

U. S. America.



Messrs. Trübner & Company,

Dear Sirs,

Please make out acc't of sales of my books, Leaves of Grass &c. for the closing year, & remit me am't due, by mail here, by draft payable to my order.

Respectfully, &c.

Walt Whitman

Dec. 27, 1873.

Correspondent:
Trübner & Company was the London agent for Walt Whitman's books; see Whitman's letter to Trübner & Company.
