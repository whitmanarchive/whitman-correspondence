Camden NJ
Jan: 2 '911

The sun is just out the first in four days—(has been very glum)—

—y'r good letter came this mn'g & was welcomed—Matters continue on much the same with me—

—try to while away the time reading & writing,—am preparing a 2d annex & completion to L of G. Am sitting here in my 2d story room same—

affectionate uncle

Walt Whitman

Correspondent:
Jessie Louisa Whitman was the second daughter of Jeff and Martha Whitman.
