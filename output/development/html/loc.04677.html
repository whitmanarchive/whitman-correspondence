Philadelphia,
Jany 13 18911



Dear Mr. Whitman

I am coming over on Thursday about noon or say one o'clock p.m. with the proofs &c.— & I am going, unless you stop me, to bring with me several young ladies and Francis Wilson (the actor)2 who is much anxious to meet you—

Will it be all right?—

The young girls are beautiful

one of them is Julian Hawthorne's3 daughter—

The March number is to be a great one—Whitman-galore—

Yours Truly

J.M. Stoddart

Perhaps DuChaillu4 will be in the party—Do you know him?—
