Washington,
1865.



Garaphelia Howard,

My dear friend:

Accept the thanks of many sick & wounded soldiers, for the $10, used to purchase articles for them, small sums, gifts, fruit &c. distributed by your money, through me.



Walt Whitman

Correspondent:
Garaphelia "Garry" Howard was one of the poet's Washington friends. See Whitman's letters to Ellen M.
