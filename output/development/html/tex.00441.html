TELEGRAM.
April 5 82

Dated Camden N J



To Jas R Osgood & Co

211 Tremont St Boston

No I cannot consent to leave out the two pieces               I am only willing to carry out my letter of March twenty third.1



Walt Whitman

Notes:

1. When Whitman did not reply to their letter of (see the letter from Whitman to Osgood & Company of ), Osgood & Co. sent a telegram to the poet on April 5 (The Library of Congress; The Complete Writings of Walt Whitman [New York: G.P. Putnam's Sons, 1902], 8:296).[back]
