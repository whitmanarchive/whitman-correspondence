Camden April 20 '911

Fine weather here & Warry2 thinks I ought to get out doors, even if — & I may if it is not noon yet — Nothing very new or different — (of course I skip all the many bad points & botherings as you have plenty enough y'r own) — Lou3 was here Saturday —all well as usual— my 2nd annex proofs4 are substantially done wh' is a g't triumph & relief—Dearest Han, I will send you one as soon as I get— I am sitting here alone expecting the doctor—$2 enclosed—

God bless you, sister dear—



Walt Whitman
