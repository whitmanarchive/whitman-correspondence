April 23d /76 140 East 38th st

New York



My dear Mr Whitman:

Your note was received and [ I am only?] [ waiting?] until the 1st of May when I shall [ be most "flush?] to send you an order for five copies of your complete works.

I am glad that you are publishing [ these?] works in this complete form, and [ feel sure?] that you will be well [ repaid?] in every way for the effort

I hope to do more than this in the way of getting [ subscribers?] .

Very sincerely Yours

J.Q.A. Ward
