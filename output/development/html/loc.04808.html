Camden N J — Dec: 7 ‘91 1

Dear Sister y’r postal card came & was welcomed — my bad spell continues2 — — an etcher in N Y has etched the photo (with hat on leaning back, I call it “the laughing philosopher”)3 & sent me — it is very fine — I always liked the pict: tho many friends did not — it is the best American etching I have seen (he sells it at $15)4 — am sitting here as usual — lots of sickness ab’t here — oatmeal & an egg for b’kfast — small appetite — $2 enc’d



W W
