Franklin Square,

New York,
Dec. 1 1873



Mr Walt Whitman,

Dear Sir,

I send enclosed Messrs Harper's & Brothers' check for Sixty Dollars ($60), in payment for your poem "Prayer of Columbus." I will send proof.

With thanks, yours truly,

Henry M. Alden\

Ed Harper's Mag

The Editor of Harpers Weekly begs to return the enclosed [illegible] to Mr. Walt. Whitman with his compliments and many thanks.

harpers Weekly,

Feb 26, 1863
