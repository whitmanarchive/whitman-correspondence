431 Stevens Street

Camden New Jersey
Dec. 15 '83



Dear Sir

In compliance with your letter of 12th I send a piece herewith1—It is indispensable that I shall see the proof—if convenient set it up & send me proof any time during next week. I reserve the right of publishing (including) the piece in future book—I shall want 30 papers.

Very respectfully &c

Walt Whitman

Notes:

1. Whitman sent "An Indian Bureau Reminiscence" to Baldwin's Monthly, published by "Baldwin, the Clothier"; it was printed in February, 1884, and was reprinted in To-Day in May, 1884.[back]
