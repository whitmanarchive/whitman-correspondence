Camden New Jersey

U S America
July 261

I have to-day sent, same address as this card, my Two Volumes—Please notify me (by postal card will do) soon as they [reach] you safely.



Walt Whitman

Notes:

1. Whitman mentioned sending these books on this date to Tottie at 64 Seymour Street, London (Whitman's Commonplace Book, Charles E. Feinberg Collection of the Papers of Walt Whitman, 1839–1919, Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.). Tottie acknowledged receipt of the volumes on August 11 (Library of Congress).[back]
