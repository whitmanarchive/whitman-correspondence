Camden April 3 '911

Have just sold a couple of books—$2 enclosed (suppose you got the one four days ago)— nothing new with me—think ab't you every day—dark wet weather—am partly busy with the proofs of the little 2d annex2 for L of G. to conclude it—Have been visited on business3 & feel tired out—

Love to you, sister dear—

Walt Whitman
