328 Mickle street1

Camden New Jersey
Dec: 23 '86



Dear J C C

Your good letter has reached me, with the generous contents—hearty thanks—



Walt Whitman2

Correspondent:
Jessie C. Chamberlin was a student at Sage College from 1885 to 1887.
