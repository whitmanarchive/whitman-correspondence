431 Stevens street

Camden New Jersey1



Messrs Scribner & Co

I send you to-day2 by mail one copy Burroughs's Notes on Walt Whitman, as ordered by yours of yesterday.

The price is $1, which please send me by mail here—



Walt Whitman

Notes:

1. This note is endorsed: "R | 4 | 2 | 77."[back]

2. Walt Whitman sent John Burroughs's book on March 30 (Commonplace Book, Charles E. Feinberg Collection of the Papers of Walt Whitman, 1839–1919, Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.).[back]
