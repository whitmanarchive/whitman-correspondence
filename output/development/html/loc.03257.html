Karns City

Butler Co Pa
April 14th 1876



Walt Whitman Esq

Dear Sir

Your paper with prospectus rec'd & Enclosed find P.O. Money Order for Ten Dollars for your complete works to date

You can send them by mail or expreſs as we have expreſs Co's here & you can get the rates at your place

Money order from Petrolia Pa office

Hoping you may sell many copies I remain

Your Truly

B.G. Morrison

Direct to B.G. Morrison

Karns City
Butler Co
