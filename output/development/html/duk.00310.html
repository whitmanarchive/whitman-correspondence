I had commenced a letter to you but as it embraced abt what Mammy has said I will not send it just now. I will send the paper1 &c to day I guess. I am sorry that you omitted to put in Probasco's2 name in the list of those that sent money, could it be easily corrected. You will hear from me in a day or so.—



Jeff

Correspondent:
Thomas Jefferson Whitman (1833–1890), known as "Jeff," was Walt Whitman's favorite brother. As a civil engineer, Jeff eventually became Superintendent of Water Works in St. Louis and a nationally recognized figure. For more on Jeff, see Randall Waldron, "Whitman, Thomas Jefferson (1833–1890)."
