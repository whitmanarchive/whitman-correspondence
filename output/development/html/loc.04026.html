431 Stevens Street Camden
Sunday afternoon



My dear Herbert

Though I am pretty well physically it is very lonesome & dreary to me here, & I have been thinking all day how much I would like to come over & see you all, & stay awhile with you. Herbert, see about the stove & have put up soon as convenient—& have some dry oak wood sawed the right length, split, & carried up there, & piled in the room. Send me word before the end of the week. I wish much to come—Love to all—



Walt Whitman

(rec'd yesterday a letter from Wm Rossetti, which I will just enclose— & take again when I come over)
