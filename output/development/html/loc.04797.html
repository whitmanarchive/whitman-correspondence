Camden Sept: 8 ‘91 1

Dear sister nothing very different — am ab’t same — eat my meals — — was out Friday afternoon an hour ride to cemetery — my friends Dr Bucke2 Mrs OConnor3 and Horace Traubel4 & his wife with me — it is my design to gather the remains of our dear mother & father & have them buried there too in the tomb5 I have had built for myself — cloudy wet day — $2 enc’d — I sent letter same ab’t week ago — many visitors — best love to you sister dear



W W
