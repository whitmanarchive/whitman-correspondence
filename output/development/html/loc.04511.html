Editorial Rooms.

The Youth's Companion.

Boston, Mass.
Jan 14th, 18911



Dear Sir,

We are very glad you have sent "Ship Ahoy!" to The Companion, for it breathes a spirit which it is a pleasure to help in spreading, with our hearty thanks first, in hope you will accept Messrs. Perry Mason & Co's cheque for fifteen dollars ($15.00) in payment for the manuscript. Copies of the paper containing the lines will be sent you when

they appear.2

Believe us,

Faithfully yours,

The Editors.

Correspondent:
As yet we have no information about this correspondent.
