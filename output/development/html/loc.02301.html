ROOMS OF The Overland Monthly

San Francisco.
Apl 13th 1870



My dear sir,

I fear that the "Passage to India" is a poem too long and too abstract for the hasty and material minded readers of the O. M..

With many thanks, I am

Your obt svt

F. Bret Harte

Ed. O. M.


Mr. Walt Whitman

Washington D.C.
