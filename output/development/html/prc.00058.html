Penna Depot W Phila
June 13 1878

TELEGRAM.

To Watson Gilder | at Scribner | 743 Bway

Am coming on to B[ryant]'s funeral1 Come to Johnstons Jewelery Store one fifty Bowery at nine oclock a m for me if convenient



Walt Whitman
