Minneapolis1—

Minnesota—
Jan 29, 1878.



Walt Whitman Esq

Dear Sir—

Please inform me what the price of "The two Rivulets" is, & where it can be procured.

Yrs Most Truly

W. L. Tiffany


(325 South 9th St.

Minneapolis—

Minn).

Notes:

1. Whitman crossed out this letter and wrote a prose draft about cremation on the back of it.[back]
