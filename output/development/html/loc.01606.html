PROGRESSIVE PUBLISHING HOUSE.

A.J. DAVIS & CO.,

Standard Books on Harmonial Philosophy, Free Religion and General Reform.

24 EAST FOURTH St., NEW YORK.

P.O. BOX 82, STATION D.

New York
27 Apl 1876



Brother Walt Whitman

Please send us by Express (address as above) 2 sets your books, $10. Enclosed.

The books are for my wife "Mary F. Davis" & for her brother Tracy [ Robin?] —son—both great admirers and lovers of you & your works.

Mrs Davis bids me give you her most cordial regards, and love.

Accept the same with a 1,000 greetings from

Your friend

A. J. Davis
