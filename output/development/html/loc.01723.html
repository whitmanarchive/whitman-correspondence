S. W. GREEN,

Printer, Stereotyper, Electrotyper, and Binder,

OFFICE, 18 JACOB STREET, CORNER FRANKFORT,

New-York,

3 May,1876
May 3, 1876



Walt Whitman, Esq

431 Stevens St. Camden N. J.

Dear Sir:

I have your letter of yesterday, and the "Leaves of Grass" with corrections. The making of the corrections, Paper like the sample [ indeed?] , and printing of 600 copies, 348 pages, will come to $157. If I have an order to that effect from you day after tomorrow morning, I will have the 600 copies ready for the Binder, as you ask, three weeks from to-day, 24 May, in unfolded sheets.

I am also ready to bind them as you may direct, and will give figures if you indicate the style

Yours truly

S.W. Green
