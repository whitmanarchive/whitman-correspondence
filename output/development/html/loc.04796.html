Tuesday Evn’g Sept: 1 ‘91— Camden 1

Nothing very new — sun shining today & cool here — have got down stairs every day for the last three after noons wh’ is quite a feat — sold a big book — rice pudding in old yellow earthen dish (& brot up in same) for my supper — visitors (two little boys to-day dear little fellows) — Am looking for Dr Bucke2 on his return f'm England — Mrs: O’Connor3 is here visiting a friend — here yesterday—am half blind & deaf — Love to you & God bless you sister dear $2 enc’d:



Walt Whitman
