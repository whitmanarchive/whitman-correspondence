431 Stevens Street, Camden,

Jan. 16, 1881.1



Dear Sir,

Yours of Dec. 24 rec'd & I have thought the shortest way would be for me to send the books without further ceremony...The price is $10, or its equivalent in your currency...



Walt Whitman
