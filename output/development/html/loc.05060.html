431 Stevens Street

Camden New Jersey
May 31 '82

In accordance with yours of 29th (the $10 have come safe—best thanks) I forward the two Vols. Centennial Edition, by same mail with this—Please send me word soon as they reach you safely.1



Walt Whitman
