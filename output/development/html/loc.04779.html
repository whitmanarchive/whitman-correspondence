Camden Dec: 22 18901

Dear sister Han,

Merry Christmas to you first thing, from my heart and soul—Nothing very new with me—fine sunny to-day, (just after noon) and I want to get out if possible in wheel chair2—Send you enclosed 10—you might give one of the 5s to C3—havn't heard any thing further f'm Jessie4 f'm Saint Louis since—Y'rs recd—Love to you, sister dear—& God bless you—Y'r affectionate brother



Walt Whitman
