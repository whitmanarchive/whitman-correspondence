To W. Whitman. Esq

Camden.

N.J.

Tobyhanna.

Monroe Co. Pa.
Oct-3-84.



My Dear Sir

Would you please inform me where to address "Martin Tupper, the author of Proverbial Philosophy"?

Hoping you will excuse this intrusion upon your time and attention, and in your courteous reply

You will greatly oblige

Yours Very Respectfully

Richard Kaines

Pastor of M.E. Church. Philadelphia Conference1
