Camden PM
July 13 '911

Another hot wave here—getting along much as ever—Lou2 & young Amy Dowe3 were here this forenoon— Geo4: has gone off to N Y— may go down for a few hours to Greenport to see Mary5—(last news she was badly suffering f'm rheumatism)—Lou bro't me a nice basket of rasp & blackberries—I had some &a little oatmeal for my breakfast—Am sitting here yet, the same old story— God bless you—love to you, sister dear—2 enc'd



Walt Whitman
