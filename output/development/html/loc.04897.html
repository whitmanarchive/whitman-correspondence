Ann Arbor. Michigan.
August 12th 1884.



Mr. Walt Whitman.

My Dear Sir:

In Baldwin's Monthly for July there was quite a long article relating to yourself, written by me. I do not know whether you read it or not, but fancy that you did. You have doubtless forgotten me, though I met you—as I have [ mentioned?] —in the article.

Correspondent:
