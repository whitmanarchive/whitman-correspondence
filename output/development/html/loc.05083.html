James R. Osgood & Co., Publishers

211 Tremont St. Boston
March 21, 1882.



Walt Whitman Esq.,

Dear Sir:1

Since our letter of yesterday2 we have received a memorandum of the passages & lines which are recommended to be Expunged. Please look it through (we enclose it in detail) and advise us at your earliest convenience whether the suggestions meet your approval. In case they do we shall then proceed to consider the mechanical difficulties in the way and how far and how easily they may be overcome.

Yours truly,

Jas R. Osgood & Co.
