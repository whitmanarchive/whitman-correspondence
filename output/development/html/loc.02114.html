431 Stevens Street

Camden
Jan: 26 '81



My dear friend

I am sorry to have to send you word that I am not feeling in good train at all to-day. I shall be unable to meet you & the other friends at dinner—I send you herewith a couple of pictures (I call it my Quaker picture)—one is for your father—also the books herewith—

also my love to you—

Walt Whitman

Walt Whitman

26 Jan 1881
