U.S. Gunboat "Monocacy"

Shanghai, China
Septmr 2. 1874.



My Dear Sir,

Last February, from Hong Kong, I sent you a line or two requiring an original edition of "Leaves of Grass." Have been able to ascertain where a copy may be pur? Iam very anxious to obtain one.

Will you oblige me—for a friend—with a copy of annulled lines in your autograph?—on any scrap of paper.

Wish best wishes, I remain, your unseen friend, in faith, hope, and charity,

Thomas Gibbons

Exult, O shores! and ring, O bells!
But I, with silent Tread,
Walk the spot my Captain lies,
Fallen cold and dead.

Correspondent:
As yet we have no information about this correspondent.
