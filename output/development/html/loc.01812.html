Lockport, N.Y.
August 26, 1867— Washington D.C.



Dear Sir:—

Send me two [ full?] copies "C.O.D." of Your "Leaves of Grass"—best style & oblige

Yours Very Truly

Geo. W. Perrigo

Correspondent:
George W. Perrigo was one half of Perrigo & Finn, who worked as the U. S. Army and Navy's "Bounty, Pay and Pension Agents." Perrigo & Finn had an office at No. 20 Arcade Building in Lockport, New York, in 1867.
