My dear Mr. Whitman:

Your postal1 has just come & I write to ask you for particulars before making the request of Mr. Cox.2

Will you kindly let me know who it is wants the negative & for what purpose.

I was sorry not to be able to grasp your hand on your birthday.

Yours very truly,

William Carey
