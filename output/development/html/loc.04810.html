Just a line sister dear Have been very sick & suffered — & they say am better, but still at death’s door —

Have the best attention & watching — send best love & God bless you always — 5 enc’d — bodily functions better than you might suppose —

It is all right whichever way Lou2 & Geo3 & Jess4 often come



W W
