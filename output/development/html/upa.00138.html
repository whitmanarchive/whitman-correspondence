OLD BOOKS BOUGHT.



REES WELSH & CO.,
BOOKSELLERS AND PUBLISHERS.
23 South Ninth Street,

Philadelphia,
6, 26 1882



Walt Whitman Esq

Camden NJ

Dear Sir

On the 21st inst we wrote, accepting your proposition, regarding Leaves of Grass, and other works, "as far as their incomplete condition would permit" but since that time have not heard from you, as there can be a number of copies L.of.G. sold at once, it is our desire, to announce the work at the earliest possible moment.

Hoping to receive an early reply

We remain Sir

Very Truly Yours

Rees Welsh & Co

McK
