Office of the Secretary and Treasurer,1

CAMDEN & ATLANTIC RAILROAD CO.
Camden, N. J., Jany 25 1881.



Walt Whitman Esq

Dear Sir:

Enclosed herewith please find Complimentary Pass over this Road for the present year.

Please acknowledge receipt, and oblige2

Notes:

1. Whitman composed a draft of the poem "As Consequent, Etc." (first published in the 1881 Leaves of Grass) on the back of this form letter.[back]

2. The rest of the letter has been cut off.[back]
