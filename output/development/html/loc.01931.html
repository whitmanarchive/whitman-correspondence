Queensbury
March 12th/74



Dear friend

I received your letter last weak & was glad to hear from [ you?] I received some papers about three weaks ago last summer I was in brooklyn & I [ hunted?] for you but could not find you I mad up my mind that I would not hear from you again but I have I received your picture & I thought it too [ look?] natural [ it?] is night now & I was agoing to the village tomorrow so I thought I would write A few lines to you my helth is good & so is my [ families?] I have got three boys & one girl

the way that I came [ to be?] in brooklyn I was [illegible] last summer & we went to new york new jersey brooklyn port morris newark new jersey & [ several?] places but did not do any thing of worth in any of the places I talk some of [ canoeing?] this summer but don't now for certain yet I wat you to tell me where Camden is whether it is in the upper or lower part jersey or off towards newark so that if I do come down there I can find you I would like to see you verry much [ last fall?] I bought A small farm & paid fifty dollars down & fifty this spring it makes pretty hard time with me this spring I [ am?] owing some debt that I dont no whether I can pay them or not this spring one of my horses is so lame that I cant work him & I have every thing to buy I have got one cow & the two sheep that is all of my stock youre letters was directed rite the two firts years of my mairage my wife was sick the most of the time so it used up what money that I had then but her health has ben verry well since then

[ there was so much sun this?] winter that it [ has ben bad?] gitting around in this part of the [ country the snow is about?] 4 feat deep [ now in?] the woods but not so deep in the [ fields?] I like your [ peaces?] in the paper verry much I [ am to pay?] four hundred dollars for 39 3/4 [ acres of land A small?] house & barn [ on it I dont?] now as I can think of [ anny more to?] write at present [ So?] good [ buy ?] for this time

this is from

Bethuel Smith your friend
