ATTORNEY GENERAL'S OFFICE, Washington,
Dec. 1, 1866.



J. S. Jenks,

My dear Sir,

I have rec'd your note of 30th Nov. with $3. Please accept my special acknowledgments. Should the letter of Nov. 17, ever appear, I will return it to you.



Walt Whitman
