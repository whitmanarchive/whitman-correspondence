January 29th 18911



My dear Sir

If there is a wounding word in the "Overland" article in which I speak of you, I trust it may be pardoned one who admiringly reads your writings, and who fancies she feels their spirit

Sincerely Yours

Laura Lyon White

1616 Clay St

Sand Francisco

Cal.
