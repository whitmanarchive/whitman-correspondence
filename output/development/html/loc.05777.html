Commonwealth of Pennsylvania1
State Library

C. L. Ehrenfeld,
Librarian.
Harrisburg Oct. 25 1880



Walt Whitman Esq.

Dear Sir:—

Please send me a copy of the Centennial edition of your works for this Library, with bill, and I will remit you the amt. at once.

Yours truly,

C.L. Ehrenfeld

Notes:

1. This letter has been crossed out. On the back Whitman wrote part of a prose draft titled "Poetry in America—Shakspere—the Future."[back]
