Aug. 9 1867



Friend Whitman,

I publish in tomorrow's Citizen, Rosetti's article from the Chronicle. I congratulate you on the article. It will wake people up.

I wish you could send me a copy of your book—a thing which I don't possess. I will mail you a copy of tomorrow's paper.

Very respectfully yours

W. L. Alden

(Associate Editor)

Correspondent:
William Livingston Alden (1837–1908) was associate editor of the New York Citizen.
