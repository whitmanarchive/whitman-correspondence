Camden March 31 pm '911

Am getting along fairly & wonder how it is with you, sister dear—have not seen Lou2 or heard f'm Jess3 very lately— the Dr4 has just been in (comes in every 2d or 3d day—I like him)—I have better strength wh' is a great help—$2 enclosed—Still at my proofs 2d annex5 tho' it will be only a little wee affair—sold a big book yesterday—it is ab't 3 pm as I write, & every thing goes fairly. God bless you my darling sister—



Walt Whitman
