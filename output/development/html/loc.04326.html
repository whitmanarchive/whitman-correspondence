Brooklyn
Oct 12th/91.



Dear Friend Water,

I was reading an article in a Magazine the other day; & came across an interesting account of a birthday meeting of your friends, & at this late day would like to add my greeting to you, as an old friend.

May the good "Lord" give you continued life and loving friends to cheer you on the journey.

Please remember me to Jeff,1 can you give me his address.

From your friend,

Sam[illegible]l G. Stanley

Correspondent:
As yet we have no information about this correspondent.

Notes:

1. Thomas Jefferson Whitman (1833–1890), known as "Jeff," was Walt Whitman's favorite brother. As a civil engineer, Jeff eventually became Superintendent of Water Works in St. Louis and a nationally recognized figure. For more on Jeff, see Randall Waldron, "Whitman, Thomas Jefferson (1833–1890)."[back]
