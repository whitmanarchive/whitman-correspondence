Camden Sept: 14 ‘91 1

Dear sister, Getting along fairly — eat my two meals every day — — one of my greatest botherations is the dimming of eye sight — incipient blindness — a long stretch of perfect sunny weather here, pleasantly cool — I went out on a two hours drive to what we call “Pea shore”2 Saturday afternoon — receive some good letters f’m England3 — am sitting here early afternoon in big chair alone by window as usual — have lots of papers & magazines — best love to you dear Han



W W
