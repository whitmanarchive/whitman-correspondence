Queensbury
December the 10 1874



Dear friend

it has ben along time since we have heard from you iwill rite you afew lines that you may now i have you in rememberance yet you were kin to my son Bethuel Smith when in the hospital afriend in need is a friend indeed Bethuel father has gone to his home to rest i and my younger son are left at the old home lonely Bethuel lives not far from us he is well as iha but little time to rite god bless you



from Maria Smith


to Mr Walt Whitman


Address

glensfalls Warren

County N Y

Correspondent:
Maria Smith, mother of Bethuel Smith wrote about her family on . Walt Whitman's draft was written on the verso of her letter. In reply to Walt Whitman's letter and later ones, she wrote again on , and (Charles E. Feinberg Collection). In the latter she said: "it always seemed to me that god sent you to save the life of our son that he might Come home and see his parents once more."
