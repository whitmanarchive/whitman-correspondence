431 Stevens Street

Camden New Jersey

March 21 '80



I send today same address as this card, my Vol: Two Rivulets (as requested by Mr Rossetti)—Please let me know soon as it reaches you safely2—



Walt Whitman

I have also mailed to you a little photograph—
