Jan 27 noon1

Much the same — weak & restless — otherwise fairly — y'r letter came — 2 enc'd — Geo2 was here — my new fuller best ed'n3 is out — have written to Mary4—very cold to day— am propp'd up in bed — read the papers &c — appetite fair — body sore & feeble —

Best love & God bless you



WW
