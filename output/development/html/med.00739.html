Camden,
July 26, 1886

I send you the Hospital article at last.1 I have preferred to give human cases, with their emotional accompaniments, sketched in on the spot, to any statistics. [illegible] the price of the article if you want it is $150. [illegible]



Walt Whitman

Notes:

1. "Army and Hospital Cases," which Walt Whitman had begun in 1884 (see Whitman's letter to Robert J. Underwood, ), appeared in Century in October 1888. Whitman received $150 from the magazine on August 7, 1886 (Whitman's Commonplace Book).[back]
