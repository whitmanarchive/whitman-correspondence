Executive Mansion, Washington D.C.
Mch 6th 1874,



Dear Sir:

The President desires me to acknowledge the receipt of your very kind letter of the 27th ultimo, also the numbers of the N.Y. Weekly Graphic, containing your reminiscences of the war.

He wishes me to assure you of his appreciation of the polite attention, and his best wishes for your speedy recovery.

Very resptly, Yours

Leon P. Luckey

Secretary.

Mr. Walt Whitman

Camden, New Jersey

Correspondent:
Leon P. Luckey was the secretary of President Ulysses S. Grant.
