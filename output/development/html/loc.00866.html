Printing Office of M. BRADSTREET & SON,

S. Spruce St., New York.
Sept 20th, 1865



Walt Whitman Esq

Sire:

When I1 receive your extra sheet will then bind you 500 "Drum Taps."

Can you give me an order on any person for a copy of your "Drum Taps" as I want to match them.

As I have to pay nett cash for the materials used in your work would thank you to forward me the money.

Respectfully,

Abraham Simpson

Notes:

1. Abraham Simpson bound 500 copies of Whitman's Sequel to Drum-Taps in a dark red binding with goldstamped title on the front cover (and blindstamped on the back).[back]
