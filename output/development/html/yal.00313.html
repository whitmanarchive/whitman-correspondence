R. WORTHINGTON,
PUBLISHER,
770 BROADWAY

New York1
July 25 1882



Mr. Walt Whitman

Dear sir,

I enclose you check for 44.50 being copyright on Leaves of Grass sold since you last received check.

Yours truly

R Worthington

Notes:

1. Whitman wrote a series of numbers on the back of the envelope that accompanied this letter.[back]
