Attorney General's Office,

Washington.
Jan. 20, 1869.



Charles F. Blanch

Dear Sir:1

Your note enclosing $3:50 has just been received. I have enveloped & sent you by to-day's mail, a copy of "Leaves of Grass," as desired. I have also sent a magazine, containing a piece of mine.



Walt Whitman

Notes:

1. Charles F. Blanch is unidentified.[back]
