431 Stevens st. cor West. Camden, N. Jersey
April 23, 1874



My dear niece,

I received your nice good letter—and also Hattie's a week or two before1—& have been very much pleased with them. I want you both to continue. I am not much different in my health—no worse. All the rest here are well. This little cut picture was one I intended to send last Christmas, but it got lost in my papers—so I enclose it now, for fun2—Best love to you, dear child, & to my dear Hattie too, from



Uncle Walt—

I will write to Hattie soon—

Correspondent:
Jessie Louisa Whitman was the second daughter of Jeff and Martha Whitman.
