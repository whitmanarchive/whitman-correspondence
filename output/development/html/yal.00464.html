July 21st 1890

Mr. Walt Whitman



Dear Sir

I am very anxious to secure your autograph to insert in a copy of Half Hours with Best American Authors.

May I ask if you will kindly send it? I enclose a stamped envelope

Yours respectfully

Chas. A. Burkhardt 55 Noble Street Greenville N. J.
