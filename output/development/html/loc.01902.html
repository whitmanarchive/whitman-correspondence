Correspondent:
Little information is available about Annie Tolman Smith. She wrote several articles about education, including an essay titled "Progress of Education for Women" that appeared in the Annual Report of the Department of the Interior (U.S. Government Printing Office, 1871). In the late 1880s and early 1890s, she worked at the Bureau of Education in Washington D.C. (see American Association for the Advancement of Science, General Program of the Meeting, [Washington: The Association, 1969] 40:77).
