Camden April 14 '911

Am getting fairly on, considering—weather almost warm— Warry2 comes in advising me to try & get out a few minutes in the wheel chair3 (he to push it) — & I havn't give it up yet—But I am very feeble & listless yet & shall probably not—Dr Longaker4 here yesterday — he encourages me— roast apple for my breakfast, (a little 7 y'rold boy, friend of mine, bro't me two yesterday)—

Love to you, sister dear—$2 enc'd—



Walt Whitman
