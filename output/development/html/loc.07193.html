328 Mickle Street

Camden New Jersey
June 14 P M

I am inclined to say yes to Mr McClure's proposition. Will a week or ten days from now do, to send in the piece, if at all? Please send me Mr. McClure's address, as I sh'd like to treat with him.1



Walt Whitman
