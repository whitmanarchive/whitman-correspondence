Thomaston
Nov. 4th 81



Mr. Walt Whitman

Dear Sir

Inclosed you will find a card. Would you favor me with your Autograph to go with a collection of Autographs of Poets Authors and Distinguished Persons. You will oblige me much

I remain

Yours Truly,

Louis Karpstyin


Thomaston

Maine.
