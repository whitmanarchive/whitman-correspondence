431 Stevens Street
Friday noon Feb 4 '81

Thanks dear Horace1 for the warm affectionate letter & for the $10, just rec'd—

—I am kept in by the weather & a special spell of feebleness,—but the first fine day will bring me out—



Walt Whitman

Notes:

1. According to the entry in Whitman's Commonplace Book (Charles E. Feinberg Collection of the Papers of Walt Whitman, 1839–1919, Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.), Whitman sent Two Rivulets to Horace Howard Furness (1833–1912), the distinguished Shakespearean scholar whom the poet met in 1879.
