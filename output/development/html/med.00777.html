If convenient would you1 [send] horse & wagon pass for '86—same as this returned—many thanks.2



Camden City
Office Horse RR

Notes:

1. The Camden Horse Railroad Company, constructed in 1871 and expanded through the 1880s, was the city's first attempt to connect inland parts of Camden with the ferryboats. The lines were converted to electricity in 1889.[back]

2. Whitman used the verso of this draft as a mailing label for S. R. Henderson's copy of Complete Poems & Prose (1888), sent on December 23, 1890. The label reads: "S R Henderson | p o box 838 | Los Angeles | California | f'm Walt Whitman | Camden | New Jersey." Henderson appears to have noted receipt of the package on December 30, as "12-30" appears in pencil in another hand.[back]
