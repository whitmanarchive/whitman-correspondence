The Pilot Editorial Rooms.
April 26.



Dear W. Whitman

Hope you got safe home. James R. Osgood wants to see the material for your complete book. Can you let him have a look at it, or write him & tell him about it?1

Always faithfully yours

John Boyle O'Reilly
