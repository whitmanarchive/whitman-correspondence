S.S. "British Prince."

Voy. No.
Sep. 16th 1891 1



My Dear Walt Whitman

For the sake of the good your works & life have done me I should have liked to have called on you to say "goodbye"—as I am [ returning?] from the sea—but—I found a family affair, which caused me some astonishment & some pain which took up all my time.

I hope to join the friends at Bolton La[illegible] & to hear of you from time to time.

Wishing you goodbye

I am yours sincerely

L. Morrell
