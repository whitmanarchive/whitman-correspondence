Camden1
April 12

I will come down Saturday afternoon in the 4 o'clock train to Kirkwood—& stay over till Sunday afternoon2—



W W

Notes:

1. This letter is addressed: Mrs Susan M Stafford | Kirkwood | Glendale | New Jersey. It is postmarked: Camden | Apr | 13 | 7 AM | N.J.[back]

2. Whitman went to Glendale on April 14, Saturday, and stayed until Monday (Whitman's Commonplace Book, Charles E. Feinberg Collection of the Papers of Walt Whitman, 1839–1919, Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.).[back]
