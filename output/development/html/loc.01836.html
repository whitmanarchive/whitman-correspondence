OFFICE OF

D. & J. Jardine, Architects

1267 Broadway

D. JARDINE, J. JARDINE, J. H. VAN NORDEN

New York,
April 26th1876.



Walt Whitman Esq

My dear Sir:

I ask you to excuse me for neglecting so long to answer your note. It came to my house instead of the office and was consequently overlooked.               It was Mrs. J. who wanted something of you and she now asks me to write for your "Leaves of Grass"

Please send it and I will send the price by return post.

[ Truly?] yours

D. Jardine

Correspondent:
David Jardine (1840–1892) and his brother John E. Jardine (1838–1920) were Scottish architects practicing in New York City.
