431 Stevens Street1

Camden New Jersey
Nov 1 '80

According to your letter just rec'd I forward the two Vols. L of G and T R by same mail with this. Please send me the pay $7 here by mail2



Walt Whitman

Notes:

1. This post card bears the address: A Williams & Co: | Booksellers | 283 Washington Street | Boston Mass:. It is postmarked: Camden | Nov | 1 | N.J.[back]

2. This transaction with the Boston book dealer was noted in Whitman's Commonplace Book (Charles E. Feinberg Collection of the Papers of Walt Whitman, 1839–1919, Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.). The card was marked by the bookseller—"Paid Nov. 3/80 $7.00."[back]
