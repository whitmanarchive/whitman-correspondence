New York, June 14th 1886



Walt Whitman Esq.

Dear Sir

Trusting that the intelligence conveyed will plead for my presumption, I venture to enclose you a few lines. At a sale of Autographs, & Books a few days ago the following prices were obtained,

"Autograph letter, Whitman, Walt, Poet," $80.00
Leaves of Grass 1st Edition 18.00

Which prices were the highest paid for any similar lots. Thinking you might not get the above information, I venture to enclose it.

with best wishes

Yours Truly

W. I. Whiting1
