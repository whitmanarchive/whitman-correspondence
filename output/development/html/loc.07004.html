328 Mickle Street

Camden New Jersey
Feb. 7 '85—

Yours just rec'd with contents (thanks)1 I forward you the Volume same mail with this—When safely rec'd. will you kindly send me a card notifying me?



Walt Whitman

Correspondent:
William C. Skinner (1855–1922) was a New York businessman, working most of his life for Colt's Manufacturing Company.

Notes:

1. On , Skinner wrote to Whitman requesting a Centennial Edition of Leaves of Grass. Whitman noted in his daybook sending the book on February 7 (Daybooks and Notebooks, ed. William White, 3 vols. [1978], 2:351).[back]
