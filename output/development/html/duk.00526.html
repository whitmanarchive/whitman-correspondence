Burlington Vt
Jan 24 [1892]1

Only a word my darling to say how precious precious your letter is & how much I feel your thinking of me now. but my dearest you will be better&then I may write to you you have my constant prayers & thoughts always. but my dear dear brother only get better. There is no words to say what I feel for you

Want to send you so much love and do feel so thankful that you are better

The $5 came safe my dear kind brother

Dear dear brother only get better again



Han

Notes:

1. Richard Maurice Bucke dates this letter 1892, confirmed by Hannah's reference to the five dollars Whitman had sent her on December 15, 1891. See Walt Whitman, The Correspondence, ed. Edwin Haviland Miller (New York: New York UP, 1969),5:273.[back]
