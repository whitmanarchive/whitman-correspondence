Walt Whitman,

Be thou accursed,—who, calling thyself a poet, in the extremist tone of Vanity!—fail to recognize a poet—a bard, far greater than thou!!

Yea! Be though, doubly accursed!!—for thy ignominious and dastardly attitude toward this loyal bard! So far above thee in Love Greatness that, thou it seemeth must needs in gross pusillanimity assume to be totally oblivious of his existence!!!

Yea!! Be thou trebly accursed!!!



Luther Carlyle Jr.
